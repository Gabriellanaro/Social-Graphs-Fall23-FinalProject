===Description===

A great way to cook butternut squash, so quick and easy.
===Ingredients===

| 1 butternut squash
| 1 tablespoon olive oil
| Pinch of salt and pepper

===Method===


| Peel the butternut squash
| Cut in half and remove and discard the seeds
| Cut into slices
| Rinse well and soak in salted water for 30 minutes if possible
| Add the olive oil to your air fryer
| Air fry for about 35 minutes or until they start to brown slightly
| Keep an eye on them as they 'go over' very quickly once they are done
