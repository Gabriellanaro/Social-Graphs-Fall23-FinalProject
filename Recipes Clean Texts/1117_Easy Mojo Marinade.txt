===Description===

This is the easiest recipe for a superlative citrus based marinade perfect for pork, chicken or fish!

===Ingredients===

| 1 jar Ile Alino
| 1 litre orange juice

===Method===


| Combine ingredients
| Let pork marinade for at least three hours
| chicken only needs one hour
| fish just 30 minutes
