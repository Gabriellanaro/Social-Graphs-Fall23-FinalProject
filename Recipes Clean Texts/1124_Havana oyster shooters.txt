===Description===


Did you know the habanero pepper derives its name from the city of Havana? So how about a spicy oyster shooter, the Cuban way, using a sprinkle of Goya's adobo with habanero, a dash of Baron's Green Seasoning and Salsa Aji Nativo

===Ingredients===

| 12 fresh oysters on the half shell, chilled
| Goya\u00ae Adobo con Chile Habanero, to taste
| Baron's Green Seasoning
| Salsa Aji Nativo

===Method===


| Carefully sprinkle the adobo mix over the oysters and add a dash of the other sauces.
|  Serve.
