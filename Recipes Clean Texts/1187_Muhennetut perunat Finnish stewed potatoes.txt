===Description===

This Finnish recipe involves cooking the potatoes in cream, with no added water!
===Ingredients===

| 6 medium potatoes, sliced or diced
| 500ml single cream
| Seasoning
| 1 tablespoon chives, snipped

===Method===


| Put the potatoes in a saucepan, to which you have added 375ml of the cream
| Bring to the boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer
| Gradually add the remaining 125ml of the cream.
| The cream should all be absorbed by the time the potatoes are cooked
| Season and serve, sprinkled with the chopped chives
