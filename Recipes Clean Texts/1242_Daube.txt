'''Daube''' is a classic Provencal (or more broadly, French) stew made with inexpensive beef braised in wine, vegetables, garlic, and herbes de Provence, and traditionally cooked in a daubi\u00e8re, a braising pan.

A daube can be made with beef, chicken, lamb or fish.
Currently our daube recipes are all Mauritian in origin.
* Chicken La Daube - Chicken daube
* Daube de Thon - Tuna daube
* Daube de Poisson - Fish daube
