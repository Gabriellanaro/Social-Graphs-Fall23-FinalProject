===Description===

[[Image:Dragon traditional Caerphilly cheese.jpg|thumb|300px|right|[http://www.dragonwales.co.uk/products/caerphilly/ Dragon traditional Caerphilly cheese]]]
An interesting 'fruit salad' recipe from South Caernarfon Creameries, using their Dragon traditional Caerphilly cheese, the only Caerphilly cheese now made in Wales.
===Ingredients===

| 8 large strawberries washed, hulled and sliced
| 1 avocado peeled and sliced
| 100g Dragon Caerphilly, crumbled
| Freshly chopped mint
| Fresh ground black pepper
====dressing====
===Ingredients===NB
| 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
| 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
| 1 teaspoon runny honey
| A squeeze lemon juice

===Method===


| Arrange avocado & strawberries on a platter
| Drizzle over the dressing
| Sprinkle over crumbled cheese and scatter with mint leaves
| Finish with black pepper to taste and serve immediately
