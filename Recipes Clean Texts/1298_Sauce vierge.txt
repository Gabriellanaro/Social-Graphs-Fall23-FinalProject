===Description===

A sauce for fish and sometimes pasta, which can be served warm or cold
===Ingredients===

| 2 very ripe tomatoes, chopped
| 2 shallots, finely chopped
| 20 sprigs of chervil, finely chopped
| 10 chive leaves, snipped
| The juice of 1 lemon 
| 100ml olive oil

===Method===


| Mix all of the ingredients in a bowl, using a fork
| If you like, you can warm it through in a pan
===Serving suggestions===
Particularly good with shellfish and delicate white fish
