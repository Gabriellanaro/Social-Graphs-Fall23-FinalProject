===Description===

From the Ministry of Food leaflet \"All about Herrings\" - reprinted in 1948!

===Ingredients===
| 4 herrings, cleaned and boned
| 2 level teaspoons salt
| 2 level tablespoons finely chopped onions (if liked)
| A few peppercorns
===Method===


| Season the inside of each herring with a little salt
| Sprinkle on the onion
| Pack closely into a greased pie dish or casserole
| Sprinkle the top with salt and a few peppercorns
| Cover with a piece of greaseproof paper and bake in a moderate oven (180\u00b0 C / 350\u00b0F / Gas 4) for about 10 minutes
| Then remove paper and allow fish to brown for a further 5 minutes
