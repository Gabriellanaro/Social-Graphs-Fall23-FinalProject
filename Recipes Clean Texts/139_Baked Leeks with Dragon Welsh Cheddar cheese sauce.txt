===Description===

What better way to celebrate Welsh cuisine, Baked Leeks in a Dragon Welsh  Cheddar cheese sauce?
===Ingredients===

| 8 medium small leeks with most of the green parts removed
| 8 slices of Parma ham or serrano ham
| 120g Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese, grated
| 300 ml cr\u00e8me fraiche
| 1 heaped dessertspoon wholegrain mustard
| Dash of Worcestershire sauce
| Black pepper & salt to taste
===Mise en place===

* Preheat your oven to 200\u00b0 C (400\u00b0 F - gas 6), [fan oven 180\u00b0 C & reduce cooking time by 10 mins per hour]

===Method===


| Cook the leeks in a pan of boiling salted water for 5-6 mins until tender. Drain leeks thoroughly and allow to cool, pat dry with kitchen roll to ensure all excess liquid removed
| Wrap a slice of ham around each leek and place seam down in a shallow baking dish
| Place cr\u00e8me fraiche in mixing bowl and mix in Worcester sauce, mustard and seasoning. Add 100g of cheddar and mix thoroughly.
| Spoon over the leeks, sprinkle over remaining cheddar and bake for 20 mins until golden and bubbling.

Image:Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese.jpg|[http://www.dragonwales.co.uk/products/cheddar/mature/ Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese]
Image:Dragon reduced fat mature Welsh cheddar cheese.jpg|[http://www.dragonwales.co.uk/products/cheddar/mature-reduced-fat/ Dragon reduced fat mature Welsh cheddar cheese]

===Serving suggestions===
Serve immediately with plenty of crusty bread to soak up sauce and a crisp green salad
===Variations===
For a lower fat alternative, use Dragon reduced fat mature Welsh cheddar cheese
