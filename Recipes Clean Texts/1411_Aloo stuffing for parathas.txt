===Description===

Primarily for making stuffed parathas but I make this recipe separate as it occurred to me that a spicy potato stuffing might be useful on its own

===Ingredients===
| 2 medium potatoes (Desiree, King Edward) 
| ½ teaspoon garam masala
| ½ teaspoon salt
| ½ teaspoon cumin seeds
| ¼ teaspoon turmeric
| ¼ teaspoon chili flakes
| Handful coriander leaves, chopped
===Method===


| Boil the potatoes in their skins until a knife penetrates easily
| Allow to cool a little
| Rub the skins off
| Pass through a potato ricer or mash thoroughly
| Add the remaining ingredients and mix well
| Divide into eight even sized balls
| Set aside ready to fill the parathas
