===Description===


An Indian seasoning for salads, bhajis and pakoras.  Don't be put off by the smell!

===Ingredients===
| 4 teaspoons mango powder
| 1 teaspoon finely ground black pepper
| 2 teaspoons cumin seeds, roasted and finely ground
| 2 teaspoons black salt powder
| 1 teaspoon asafoetida
===Method===


| Mix all the ingredients well and store in an airtight container.
===Servings===
Servings: 4 - About 4 tablespoons of chaat masala
===Chef's notes===
Black salt powder can be bought from most Indian shops.
