===Description===

An Indian spice mix which can be used to substitute garam masala.

===Ingredients===
| 20g cassia bark
| 20g cumin seeds
| 10g cardamom seeds
| 10g Clove buds
===Method===


| Dry-fry the spices
| Grind and keep in an airtight container or Lock&Lock box
===Servings===
Servings: 10 - Makes about 50g
