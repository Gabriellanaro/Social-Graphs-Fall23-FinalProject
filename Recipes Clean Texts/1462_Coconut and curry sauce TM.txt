===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 3 egg yolks
| 200g milk
| 50g coconut milk
| Pinch of salt
| Pinch of pepper
| 1 teaspoon curry powder
| 1 teaspoon flour
| 1 teaspoon butter
| A pinch of nutmeg
===Method===


| Place all of the ingredients in the TM.
| Blend '''4 minutes / 80\u00b0 / Speed 4'''.
| Serve hot.
===Serving suggestions===
It accompanies all kinds of meat, especially pork, but also chicken, lamb and turkey.  It is exquisite with fish, pulses and vegetables.
===Variations===
Curry sauce is one of the most popular in India.  It is very aromatic, but mild.  If you prefer more flavour add more curry powder or pepper.
