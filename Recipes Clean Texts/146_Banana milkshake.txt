===Description===


Easy to make, reasonably healthy and delicious.

===Ingredients===
| 568 ml (20fl oz / 1 pint milk)
| 2 bananas, peeled
| 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
| 2 teaspoons sugar

| 3 drops vanilla essence
===Method===


| Add all of the ingredient to a food processor
| Blend for 2 minutes
===Serving suggestions===
Serve chilled
