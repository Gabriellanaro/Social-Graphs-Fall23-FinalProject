===Description===


To quote Nik or Edwina:
''\"This is a nice touch, a garnish added to many Keralan curries to zizz them up a little. Make just a bit each time you need it and use it fresh\"''

===Ingredients===
| 3 tablespoons coconut oil
| 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
| 3 or 4 shallots, finely sliced
| 10 curry leaves
===Method===


| Fry all the ingredients gently in a little coconut oil till the shallots and leaves are golden
| Sprinkle a little over a curry just before serving
