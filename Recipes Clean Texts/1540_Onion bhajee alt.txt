===Description===

Now you can make everyones favourite Indian snack at home

===Ingredients===
|½ lb gram flour (Chana flour - made from ground lentils)
|2 medium onions, sliced finely (top to bottom) or chopped in a food processor
|½\" fresh ginger, grated
|3 tablespoons of freshly chopped coriander leaves
|1 fresh green chili, de-seeded and chopped
|1 large potato, sliced finely with a mandolin or a food processor
|½ teaspoon of freshly ground cumin powder
|½ teaspoon turmeric powder
|¼ teaspoon chili powder
|½ teaspoon of baking powder
|¼ teaspoon of salt
|Juice from ½ a lemon
|½ cup of water
|Oil to deep-fry
===Method===


| Mix all of the ingredients above with ½ of the water, carefully adding more water as the onions and potatoes will add more moisture to the batter.  It should be thick so can be shaped by a tablespoon and hold it's shape
| rest the mixture for 1 hour if possible
| Heat the oil as you would for deep-frying chips
| Shape with a tablespoon and deep fry for a few minutes until golden.
| Drain on kitchen paper and serve
===Serving Suggestions===
Serve hot with mint and onion raita
