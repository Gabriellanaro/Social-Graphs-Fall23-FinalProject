===Description===

A nice spicy relish that is a good accompaniment to most spicy dishes.

===Ingredients===
| 2 fresh red chillies ( or 1 red and 1 green)
| 1 teaspoon allspice berries
| Â½ teaspoon black peppercorns
| 1 teaspoon dried oregano
| 2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
| 2 medium white onions
| 100ml (3.5 fl oz) white wine vinegar
| 200 ml (7 fl oz) cider vinegar
| Sea salt
===Method===


| Roast the chillies as described here
| In a mortar and pestle or a coffee grinder, grind the allspice, peppercorns and oregano until coarsely ground
| Slice the onions very thinly (a food processor is good for this) .  Add to a bowl
| Skin and de-seed the chillies, slice finely and mix into the onions
| Stir in the spices and the crushed garlic and mix well
| Add the vinegars and season with salt
| Refrigerate for at least 12 hours to allow the flavours to mingle
===Serving suggestions===

Serve chilled
