===Description===

These are easy to make and can be utilised with many different flavoured sauces.

===Ingredients===
| 450 g (1 lb) lean minced beef
| ½ cup grated onion
| 2 cloves of garlic, peeled and crushed
| ¼ a teaspoon dried oregano
| 1/8 of a teaspoon of dried mint
| 2 slices white bread, crusts removed - this can be stale bread
| ½ cup white wine or water
| 1 egg, beaten
| Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
| Flour for rolling
| Olive oil for frying
===Method===


| With clean hands, mix the mince, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, oregano, and mint.
| Moisten the bread in the wine or water.
| Add bread and egg to meat. Knead well.
| Shape into 2.5 cm (1\") balls, dust with flour, and saute in hot olive oil until browned and cooked to your liking.
===Serving suggestions===

Use them to make New York-style stone baked pizza, Meatball pizza or serve them with spaghetti and one of the following sauces.
* Balsamic vinegar gravy
* Sofrito (Spanish tomato sauce)
* Spicy tomato salsa
* Tomato sauce for pizza
* Roast garlic and sherry vinegar sauce
===Variations===

Add 1/2 a teaspoon of chilli flakes for spicier meatballs.
