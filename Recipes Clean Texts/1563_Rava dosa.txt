===Description===

These are crispy spicy pancakes that are great with relishes and chutneys

===Ingredients===
| ''For the batter''
| 2 cups semolina/rava
| ½ cup rice flour
| ½ cup maida (plain flour)
| 1.5 teaspoons cumin seeds, lightly ground
| 1 teaspoon grated ginger
| coriander leaves - 2 tablespoons
| A few curry leaves
| 1 finely chopped green chili
| Sea salt to taste
| 6 cup water (approximately)
| ''For frying''
| Vegetable oil for frying
===Method===


| Add all of the ingredients, mix well then leave to rest for an hour
| It should have the consistency of single cream, adjust with water or flour as required
| Ideally, heat a lightly greased tava or a large no-stick frying pan
| When hot pour a little batter in a circle around the edge of the tava to make a huge doughnut shape, then fill the space in the middle with a little batter and swirl the tava to distribute evenly.
| Cook unit browned and crispy.  You can flip it over and brown the other side if you wish
===Serving suggestions===

Serve immediately with a selection of chutneys, pickles or dips.
