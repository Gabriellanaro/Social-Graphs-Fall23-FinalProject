===Description===

A red hot, spicy chutney that can be eaten as soon as it has been prepared

===Ingredients===
| 1 big handful of coriander leaves,  most stems removed
| 1 big handful of mint leaves, most stems removed
| 6 green chillies, deseeded
| 1 teaspoon hot chili powder
| Â½ teaspoon cumin seeds, dry roasted
| 1 pinch asafoetida
| 2 teaspoons mango powder or sumac
| Juice of 1 lemon
| Salt to taste
===Method===


| Add everything to a food processor and blend to a paste
| Add to a serving bowl and drizzle with lemon juice
===Serving suggestions===
Serve immediately
===Servings===
Servings: 10 - Makes 125 g (4 oz) of hot chutney
