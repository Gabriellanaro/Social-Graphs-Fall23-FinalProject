A mild spicy satay sauce ideally suited to dipping pork, chicken or vegetables.
===Ingredients===
| 100 g (4 oz) peanut butter
| 120 ml (4 fl oz) chicken stock
| 120 ml (4 fl oz) groundnut oil
| 1 tablespoon lemon or lime juice
| 1 tablespoon soy sauce
| ½ teaspoon salt
| ½ teaspoon chili powder or flakes
| ½ teaspoon sugar
===Method===


| Mix all ingredients in a pan and simmer over a low heat until all ingredients have combined
| Remove from heat and allow to cool
| Store in an airtight jar in the fridge
===Serving suggestions===

Serve cold with most meats.

Especially good on barbecued chicken.
