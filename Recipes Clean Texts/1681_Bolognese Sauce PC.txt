===Description===

A version of this traditional Italian sauce for a pressure cooker.  Timing is based upon 7lbs pressure.

===Ingredients===
| 2 tablespoons olive oil
| 130g rindless bacon, chopped
| 1 large onion, finely chopped
| 1 large or 2 small carrots, scraped and chopped
| 250g minced beef
| Garlic to taste, crushed
| 4 tablespoons tomato pur\u00e9e
| 1 x 400g tin chopped tomatoes
| 1 teaspoon mixed herbs
| 2 bay leaves
| Salt and pepper, to taste
| 370ml beef stock or Marmite consomm\u00e9
===Method===


| Heat the oil in the open pressure cooker and fry the bacon, onion and carrots until lightly browned.
| Remove from the cooker and drain.
| Fry the minced beef in the hot oil until evenly browned.
| Add the garlic, tomato pur\u00e9e, chopped tomatoes, herbs, bay leaf, stock.the bacon, onion and carrots and stir well.
| Close the lid, bring to pressure and cook for 30 minutes.
| Release the pressure quickly.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with spaghetti and Parmesan cheese or use in shepherd's pie or lasagnes.
