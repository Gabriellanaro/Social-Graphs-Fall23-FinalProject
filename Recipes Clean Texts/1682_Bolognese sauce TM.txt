===Description===

A recipe for a basic Bolognese sauce

'''For the sauce'''
===Ingredients===TM
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 130g carrots
| 130g onions
| 180g mushrooms
| 50g celery or sweet pepper
| 1 clove garlic
| 500g chopped tomatoes
'''The remainder'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 500g minced meat
| Salt
| Pepper
| Oregano
| 1 bay leaf
===Method===


| Put all of the ingredients for the sauce into the bowl and chop '''15 seconds / Speed 5'''
| Push down the ingredients inside, that are on the wall of the bowl '''7 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon'''
| Add the minced meat, salt and spices, pushing down the other ingredients '''15 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon'''
| If you want the sauce to be a little less liquid, cook for '''5 minutes longer'''
| Adjust seasoning
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
