===Description===

A basic pesto recipe for the Thermomix

===Ingredients===TM
| 50g Parmesan cheese
| 30g Ewes cheese (optional)
| 1 clove garlic
| 30g pine nuts
| 80g basil leaves
| Salt, to taste
| 150g olive oil
===Method===


| Wash the basil leaves and dry them thoroughly.
| Put them in the bowl together with the cheeses, pine nut and garlic clove '''20 seconds / Speed 7'''.
| Add the oil and the salt  and emulsify '''20 seconds / Speed 7'''.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
