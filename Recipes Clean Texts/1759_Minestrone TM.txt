===Description===

A version of this famous soup for Thermomix
===Ingredients===TM
| 500g various vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, chard, turnips etc)
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 50g onion
| 1 clove garlic
| 50g pancetta, in pieces
| 1kg water
| 1 Thermomix stock cube or salt
| Pepper
| 50g rice
| 1 sprig parsley, chopped
| 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
| 50g butter
===Method===


| Put the vegetables in the bowl '''5 seconds / Speed 4'''
| If you want the vegetables chopped finer, process for a few seconds more
| Remove from the bowl and reserve
| Next, add the onion and garlic '''2 seconds / Speed 5'''
| Push the ingredients down into the bowl from the sides and the lid
| Add the oil and pancetta '''5 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 2'''
| Add the water '''8 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 2'''
| Add the reserved vegetables, the stock cube or salt, pepper and rice '''15 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Reverse Blade / Speed 1'''
| Finally, tip into a soup terrine, add the parsley, cheese and butter and stir
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
===Variations===
If you wish, in place of the rice, you can use pasta to taste.  When chopping the vegetables, put the leafy one on top of the others.
