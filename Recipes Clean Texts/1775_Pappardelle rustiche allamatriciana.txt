===Description===

[[Image:Pappardelle rustiche.jpg|thumb|300px|right|Pappardelle rustiche]]
Papperdelle with tomato and bacon sauce.  'Rustiche' meaning rustic, refers to the pasta which has ridges down the length of it.  This recipe is usually made with spaghetti.

===Ingredients===
| 120g pancetta or streaky bacon, cut into thin strips
| Olive oil
| 1 onion, finely sliced
| Garlic to taste, chopped
| 500g ripe tomatoes, finely chopped
| 2 roasted red peppers, finely chopped
| Salt
| 400g pappardelle rustiche
| 20g Parmesan cheese, grated
===Method===


| Heat a little oil in a pan and gently cook the pancetta or bacon.
| Add the onion and the garlic and allow turn golden.
| Add the tomates, season and cook until the sauce is done.
| Meanwhile, boil the pasta in plenty of salted boiling water for 10 minutes, or until al dente.
| Drain, and place in a serving bowl.
| Add the sauce, tossing it lightly and sprinkle with some Parmesan.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with salad.
===Variations===
Add some chillies or ginger if you want to spice it up a bit.
