===Description===

Be prepared for the summer season with this spiffing Pimm's cocktail, taken from issue 57 of The Chap.

===Ingredients===
| 1 measure Pimm's No.1 Cup
| ½ measure Gin
| 1 measure Bols Creame de Bananes
| 1 measure rosso vermouth
| 1 measure lime cordial
| 3 dashes Angostura bitters
===Method===


| Mix together
| Shake and serve in a martini glass with a garnish of guilty conscience.
