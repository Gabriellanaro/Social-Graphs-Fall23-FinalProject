===Description===


This Thermomix pasta sauce recipe is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 250g minced beef
| 250g minced pork
| 350g milk
| 250g chopped and blended tomatoes
| 100g carrots
| 100g onions
| 50g celery
| 50g olive oil
| 50g mushrooms
| Salt
===Method===


| Add the carrot, onion, mushrooms and celery to the bowl.
| Chop '''15 seconds / Speed 5'''.
| Add the oil and program '''7 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| Next add the meats, the milk and salt.
| Cook '''10 minutes / Temp. Varoma /  Reverse blade /  Speed spoon'''.
| Add the tomato and adjust seasoning.
| Cook '''20 minutes / Temp. Varoma /  Reverse blade /  Speed spoon'''.
===Serving suggestions===
This is a sauce ideally served with tagliatelle, pasta made with wheat flour, egg, olive oil salt and a little water or with gnocchi, which is made with a dough of potatoes and semolina.

===Chef's notes===
Follow the RSPCA Think Pig Checklist to make sure that your pork has come from pigs that have been reared to higher welfare standards.
