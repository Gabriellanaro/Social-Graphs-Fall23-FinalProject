===Description===


Great for snacking on while the barbecue food cooks - just don't overdo it!

===Ingredients===
| 12 vine leaves
| 12 slices of pancetta
| 200g (7 oz) pecorino cheese, thinly sliced
===Method===


| Flatten the vine leaves
| Lay a slice of pancetta on each
| Smother with pecorino cheese
| Roll or fold the leaves
| Cook on an oiled barbecue plate for 2 minutes a side
===Variations===

Add a dash of garlic olive oil on each vine leaf

Maybe a few chili flakes
