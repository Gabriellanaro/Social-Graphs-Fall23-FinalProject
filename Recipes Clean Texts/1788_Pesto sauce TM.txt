===Description===

This Thermie Pesto Sauce recipe is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix'''.

===Ingredients===TM
| 50g fresh basil
| 3 Cloves garlic
| 50g shelled walnuts
| 100g virgin olive oil
| 50g Parmesan cheese
| Salt and pepper
===Method===


| Place the cheese in the bowl and grate for '''10 seconds / Speed progressively 5-10'''.
| Next, add the remaining ingredients and process at '''Speed 6'''.
| Pulverise until you have a homogenous and thick mixture.
===Serving suggestions===
Pesto is a cold, aromatic Italian sauce used to accompany pasta dishes. 
===Variations===
The classic pesto is made with pine nuts but variations work just as well.

To make this even tastier, you can add 1-2 tbsps of cream or evaporated milk.
