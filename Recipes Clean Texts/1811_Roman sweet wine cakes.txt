===Ingredients===

| 225g Spelt wheat flour
| 5ml baking powder
| 5ml Ground Rosemary
| 100g Chopped Almonds
| 5ml Cinnamon
| 60ml Sherry
| 60ml Red grape juice
| Squeeze of honey
| a little bit of milk
===Method===



| Mix all of the ingredients except the milk into a bowl. 
| Add a little bit of milk to the combine the mixture and make a soft dropping consistancy. 
| Put on a baking tray and into an oven at gas mark 5 (190\"C) for approx 30 minutes and here you have it!
