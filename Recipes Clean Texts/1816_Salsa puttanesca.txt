Literally meaning 'whore's sauce', this Roman sauce is made with anchovies, garlic, olives, capers and tomatoes.

===Ingredients===

| 30ml olive oil
| 50g butter
| Garlic, to taste, chopped
| 4 anchovies, rinsed and chopped
| 150g stoned black olives, chopped
| Chilli flakes, to taste
| 1 tablespoon capers
| 2 tablespoons of flat leaf parsley, chopped
| Seasoning
| 200g tinned tomatoes or fresh equivalent if sufficiently ripe, chopped
===Method===



| Heat the butter and the oil in a saucepan and add all of the ingredients except for the tomatoes.
| Saut\u00e9 gently for about 10 minutes.
| Add the tomatoes and simmer for a further 15 minutes.
===Serving suggestions===
Pour the sauce over a warmed bowl of cooked, long pasta such spaghetti and taglitelli.
