===Description===

Simple but tasty, baby carrots won't need boiling for as long as larger sliced carrots. Adjust the cooking time to suit your taste

===Ingredients===
| 450 g (1 lb) Carrots
| 1 teaspoon butter
| Â½ teaspoon sea salt
===Method===

| '''Young carrots'''
| Scrub the carrots
| Drop into boiling water salted water and boil for 7 to 10 minutes depending upon your desire for crunch
| Place in a warm bowl and toss the carrots in the butter
| Add a pinch of salt to taste
|'''Larger, older carrots'''
| Peel the carrots, top and tail them
| Slice into discs about 1 cm thick
| Drop into boiling water salted water and boil for 9 to 12 minutes depending upon your desire for crunch
| Place in a warm bowl and toss the carrots in the butter
| Add a pinch of salt to taste
