===Description===


A colourful and very easy antipasto which can also be served as an accompaniment.

===Ingredients===
| 1 bunch small radishes, trimmed and thinly sliced
| 3 celery sticks, thinly sliced on the diagonal
| 1 green pepper, julienned
| 2 medium carrots, thinly sliced on the diagonal
| 45ml olive oil
| Seasoning
| 50g Parmesan, shaved
===Method===


| Arrange all the ingredients on a serving plate and lightly salt.
| Drizzle with the olive oil.
| Add the Parmesan shavings and grind some black pepper over the top.
===Variations===
Add some Crushed garlic if desired.
