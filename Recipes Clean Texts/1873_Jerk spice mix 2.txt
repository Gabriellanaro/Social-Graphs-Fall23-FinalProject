===Description===

Jamaican jerk spice is a pungent, hot mixture of spices used to flavour meats  before grilling or roasting.

===Ingredients===
| 4 tablespoons of ground allspice
| 1 tablespoon ground dried thyme
| 1 tablespoon paprika
| 1 teaspoon hot chilli powder
| 1 teaspoon garlic powder
| 1 teaspoon onion powder
| 1 teaspoon salt
| Â½ teaspoon black pepper
===Method===


| Mix the ingredients together and store in an airtight container.
| Rub on meat and leave for a few hours in a cool place before grilling, roasting or barbecuing.
===Servings===
Serves 10 - Will prepare meat for 10 people
