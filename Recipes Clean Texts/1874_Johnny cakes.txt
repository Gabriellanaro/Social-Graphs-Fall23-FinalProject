===Description===

These Jamaican 'cakes' are also known as '''journey cakes''' because they are ideal as a travelling snack.

===Ingredients===
| 500 g plain flour
| 2 teaspoons baking powder
| 1 teaspoon salt
| 30 g butter
| Water
| Oil for deep frying
===Method===


| Sift the flour, baking powder and salt into a bowl.
| Mix in the butter and enough water to make a stiff dough.
| Shape them into 5 cm balls and deep fry them in moderately hot oil until they are brown.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with butter for breakfast, or as an accompaniment to fish or meat.
