===Description===

A simple but unusual way of making rice pudding from Colombia.

===Ingredients===
| 110 g raisins
| 1 tablespoon butter
| 500 g pudding rice
| 4 tablespoons sugar
| ½ teaspoon salt
| 680 ml water
| 240 ml strong, sweetened, black  coffee 
===Method===


| Melt the butter in a saucepan and add the raisins.
| When they are plump, add the remaining ingredients and leave to simmer until the rice is cooked and has absorbed the liquid.
