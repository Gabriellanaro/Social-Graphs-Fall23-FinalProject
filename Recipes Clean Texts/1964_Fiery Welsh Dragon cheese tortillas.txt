===Description===

[[Image:Welsh Dragon cheese tortillas.jpg|thumb|300px|right|]]

Hot chili tortillas filled with Welsh Dragon vintage cheddar cheese and salad.  No cooking required, what could make a nicer snack?
===Ingredients===

| 4 large tortillas
| 4 ripe tomatoes thinly sliced
| 1 large avocado thinly sliced
| Shredded iceberg lettuce
| 2 cooked chicken breasts thinly sliced
| 4 tablespoons hot chilli sauce
| 120g grated vintage cheddar
| 1 red onion thinly sliced
| 2 tablespoons of finely chopped coriander

===Method===


| Spread each tortilla with 1 tablespoon of chill sauce
| Top each tortilla with lettuce, sliced onion, tomato, chicken and avocado
| Sprinkle with coriander and grated cheese, then season to taste. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll up tightly, serve immediately
