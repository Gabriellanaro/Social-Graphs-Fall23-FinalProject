===Description===

Another recipe from Mum's scribbled cookery notes, this recipe was one of Dad's favourites too.

===Ingredients===

| 4 slices of wholemeal bread, preferably stale
| 25 g (1 oz) butter
| 1 tablespoon Muscovado sugar 
| 426 ml (3/4 pint) whole milk
| 50 g (2 oz) Dried fruit of choice (sultanas, raisins, currants, chopped dried apricots, Maraschino Cherries)
| 2 free range eggs
| ¼ teaspoons freshly ground cinnamon
| ¼ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg


===Mise en place===

* Preheat the oven to 180\u00b0 C (350\u00b0 F - gas 4), [fan oven 160\u00b0 C & reduce cooking time by 10 mins per hour]
===Method===



| Spread all of the butter evenly on the bread and cut each piece into 4
| Place half of the bread, buttered side up into the base of a 900 ml (1.5 pint) oven-proof dish
| Layer with half of the dried fruit and sprinkle with half of the sugar
| Repeat with the remaining bread, fruit and sugar
| Whisk the eggs, milk and spices into a custard and pour over the layered bread
| Bake for 45 minutes by which time the custard should be set
===Serving suggestions===
Serve hot
