===Ingredients===
| 800 g tomatoes, peeled, de-seeded and diced
| 200 g onions, finely diced
| 100 g Serrano chillies, finely chopped
| 80 g coriander leaves, chopped
| 20 ml lemon juice
| Salt to taste
| 2 Avocados (optional)
===Method===


| Mix all of the ingredients and season to taste.
===Serving suggestions===
Excellent with tortilla chips.
===Variations===
If you cannot find Serrano chiles, you can substitute with Jalape\u00f1os.

You can also add some peeled avocados.
