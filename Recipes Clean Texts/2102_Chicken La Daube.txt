===Description===

Chicken Daube, (Daube de Poulet), is a classic chicken recipe from Mauritius.
===Ingredients===
| 1kg whole chicken, cut into small pieces
| 2 large onions, chopped
| 1/2 a tablespoon of crushed garlic
| 1 teaspoon ginger, crushed
| 3 cloves
| 1 cinnamon stick
| 2 teaspoons ofthyme
| 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
| 500g tinned chopped tomatoes
| 3 medium chillies, chopped
| oil
| salt and pepper to taste 
===Method===


|Fry the onions in the oil for a few minutes then add the garlic and ginger. 
|Fry for 2 minutes before adding the chicken, cloves, cinnamon, thyme, chillies and a little salt. 
|Allow to cook for about 15 minutes before adding the tomatoes. 
|Simmer on low heat for about 30 minutes, or until the chicken is done then adjust the seasoning.
|Garnish with coriander. 
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with plain white rice or black lentils daal.
