===Description===

This is an easy dish from Mauritius.

===Ingredients===
| Oil
| 800g pumpkin, cut into small cubes
| Salt, to taste
| Dried thyme, to taste
| Parsley, to taste, finely chopped
| 1 glass warm milk
| Grated cheese, to taste
| Savoury biscuits, to taste, roughly crushed
===Method===


| Heat the oil in a pan and fry the pumpkin until softened.
| Season with salt and add the thyme
| Transfer the pumpkin to a bowl and add the warm milk.
| Beat the mixture to form a smooth paste and stir in the parsley.
| Place in a baking dish and sprinkle with the cheese and biscuits.
| Brown in the oven for 15 minutes.
