===Description===

This is a hot, delicate cucumber and potato soup, suitable for a cold day.

===Ingredients===TM
| 30g olive oil
| Garlic to taste, peeled
| 100g onion, peeled and chopped
| 500g cucumbers, peeled and chopped
| 300g potatoes, peeled and chopped
| 500g vegetable stock
| Salt to taste
| 100g single cream
===Method===


| Put the oil in the bowl '''6 minutes / Temp Varoma / Speed Spoon'''.
| Add the garlic and onion '''12 minutes / Temp Varoma / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon / MC Off'''.
| Add the cucumbers and potatoes '''4 secs / Speed 5'''.
| Add the stock and salt '''12 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 1'''.
| Add the cream '''30 secs  / Speed progressively 5-10'''.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with crusty bread.
===Variations===
If you wish, you can remove the seeds from the cucumber, but personally I don't bother.
===Chef's notes===
I used half a bulb of garlic, but I feel it could take more without losing its delicacy.
