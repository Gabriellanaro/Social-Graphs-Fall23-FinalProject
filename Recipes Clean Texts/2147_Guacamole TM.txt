===Description===

 |Image = alt=Electus
This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 2 ripe avocados
| 200g tomatoes
| 1 small onion
| ½ lemon
| 1 teaspoon fresh coriander
| A pinch of pepper
| A pinch of salt
| A pinch of sugar
===Method===


| Peel and chop the avocados and onion.
| Wash and chop the tomatoes and remove the seeds.
| Squeeze the juice from the lemon half.
| Put the avocado, tomato, onion, coriander and lemon juice into the bowl.
| Beat at '''Speed 4''' to obtain a thick pur\u00e9e.
| Add the salt, pepper and sugar and blend again at '''Speed 4'''.
===Serving suggestions===
The most popular way to eat guacamole is as an accompaniment to corn tortillas or nachos.
