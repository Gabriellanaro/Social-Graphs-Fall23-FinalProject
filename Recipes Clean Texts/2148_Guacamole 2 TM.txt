===Description===

Another variation of this popular dip

===Ingredients===TM
| 90g spring onions
| 360g tomatoes, peeled and seeded
| Coriander (optional)
| Green chillies (optional)
| Salt
| Pepper
| 430g avocado, peeled and stone removed
| A few dashes of Tabasco
| The juice of half a lemon
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 1 avocado stone
===Method===


| Put the spring onions, chopped into large pieces into the bowl '''20 seconds / Speed 5'''
| Remove and reserve
| Without washing the bowl, add the tomatoes, coriander and chillies (if using), salt and pepper '''1 minute / Speed 5'''
| Incorporate the remaining ingredients '''1 minute / Speed 5'''
| Pour the guacamole into a dish, add the reserved spring onions and mix well
| Place the avocado stone on the guacamole so that it doesn\u0092t go dark
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with nachos.  You can also serve it as a salad with corn tortillas, Mexican tacos or with diced, battered fish
