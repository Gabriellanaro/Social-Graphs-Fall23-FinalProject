===Description===

This dip, some tortilla chips and a big hat will start your Mexican evening off nicely!

===Ingredients===
| 4 ripe avocados
| 4 medium tomatoes
| Â½ small onion, roughtly chopped
| 2 peeled garlic cloves
| Handful  of coriander leaves, chopped (or parsley at a push)
| Juice of a lime
| 3 fresh red chillies
| Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
===Method===


| Prepare the avocados: cut the avocados in half, remove the stones and scoop the flesh out with a tablespoon and place in a food processor
| Add the lime juice to the avocados and blend until smooth
| Add the remaining ingredients and blend until properly mixed
| Cover and chill for 1 hour, then serve
===Serving suggestions===
Serve chilled with tortilla chips
You can also serve it with fried plantains. 
===Variations===
* add 4 tablespoons of sour cream
* add a teaspoon of freshly ground cumin seeds
