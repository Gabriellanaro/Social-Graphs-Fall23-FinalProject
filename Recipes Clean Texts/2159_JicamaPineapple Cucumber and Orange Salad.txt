===Description===

This is a great snack and its also great to serve at parties.

===Ingredients===
| 1 cup jicama
| 1 cup pineapple
| 1 cup cucumber
| 1 cup orange pieces
| lemon juice
| salt
===Method===


| Wash, peel and cut up into cubes jicama, pineapple, cucumber, and orange.  Add lemon juice and salt to taste.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve on toothpicks
===Variations===
Sprinkle with chili powder if desired.
