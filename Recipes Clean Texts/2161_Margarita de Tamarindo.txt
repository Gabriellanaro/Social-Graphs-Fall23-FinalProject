Margaritas are a great summer drink and are nowadays mixed with anything. If you love flavoured margaritas try this quick easy recipe.

===Ingredients===
| 1 part Tequila
| 1 part Tamarindo
| 1 part ice cubes
===Method===


| Add the ingredients to a blender & mix well
| Pour into glasses have have been rimmed with tajin.
===Chef's notes===
A mixture of Chilli powder and fine sea salt is probably an acceptable substitute you cannot obtain tajin.
