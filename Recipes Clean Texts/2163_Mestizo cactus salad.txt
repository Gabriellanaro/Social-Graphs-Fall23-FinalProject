===Ingredients===

| 844g (1 jar) tender cactus La Coste\u00f1a
| ¼ olive oil
| ¼ coriander bunch
| ¼ white onion
| 2 tablespoons of Apple vinegar La Coste\u00f1a
| 1 tablespoon Dried Mexican oregano
| 1 whole tomato
| 100g queso fresco or feta cheese (optional)
| salt
| 1 small Lettuce
| pinch of ground black pepper

===Method===


| Chop the coriander
| Cut the onion & the tomato into fine slices
| Place the cactus into a bowl
| Mix the tender cactus La Coste\u00f1a, coriander, apple vinegar  La Coste\u00f1a, black ground pepper, olive oil and salt
===Serving suggestions===
Place a lettuce leaf on a plate

Place the cactus over the lettuce with the tomato and feta cheese as a garnish
