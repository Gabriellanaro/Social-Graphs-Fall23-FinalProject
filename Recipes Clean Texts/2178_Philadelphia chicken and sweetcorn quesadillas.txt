===Description===

Easy to make creamy quesadillas using philadelphia light with garlic and herbs.



===Ingredients===

| 1 large cooked chicken breast, thinly sliced
| 3 tablespoons sweetcorn
| 3 spring onions, finely sliced
| 1 dsp tomato puree
| 2 large tortillas
| 50 g Philadelphia Light with Garlic & Herbs
===Method===



| Heat chicken, sweetcorn, spring onion and tomato puree with 2 tablespoons of water in a small pan, stirring occasionally.
| Spread one of the tortillas with the Philly. Spoon the chicken mixture over one tortilla and top with the other.
| Heat a large frying pan and add the filled tortilla. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.
| Turn over with a large spatula and cook the other side until golden brown.
===Serving suggestions===
Slice into 4 wedges and serve with a green salad.
