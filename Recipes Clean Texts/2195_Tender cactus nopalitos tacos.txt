===Description===

This tender cactus recipe is courtesy of MexTrade.co.uk and originates from [http://www.tamal.co.uk/ Tamal UK]

Located in Maltby Street market and Duke of York Square, [http://www.tamal.co.uk/ Tamal UK] is the perfect way of getting in touch with mexican food. Specializing in Tamales this Mexican street stall is one of the favourites of Mexican people. Order them online or visit the fabulous food market.
===Ingredients===

| 825g La Coste\u00f1a Tender Cactus Nopalitos (1 Jar)
| ½ cup of thin sliced or finely chopped red onion
| 3 medium tomatoes seeded and chopped
| ¼ cup finely chopped coriander leaves (more if you like)
| ½ tablespoons olive oil
| Juice of 1 fresh lime
| ¼ teaspoon dried Mexican oregano
| 1-2 La Coste\u00f1a Jalape\u00f1os Chilies, finely chopped (optional)
| 1 Cup La Coste\u00f1a Refried Pinto beans 
| 75g Shredded cheese (mozzarella, cheddar, halloumi) (optional)
| 8 Corn tortillas

===Method===


| Drain La Coste\u00f1a nopalitos.
| Place the nopalitos in a salad bowl and add the onion, tomatoes, coriander and chilies.
| Whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, oregano and pour all over. Toss to blend well.
| Heat La Coste\u00f1a Refried beans on a stove or in microwave.
| Heat tortillas on a flat pan then wrap in a kitchen towel to keep them warm.
| Place them inside a bamboo steamer or any other plastic container with lid.
| Place about 2 tablespoons of La Costena Refried beans on the tortilla and top with another 2 tablespoons of the vegetable mixture.
| Top with some cheese if you like.
