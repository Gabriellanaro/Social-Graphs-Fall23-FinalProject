===Description===

Crisp on the outside, fluffy in the middle, these potato pancakes make an nice accompaniment or a tasty snack when served with salsa and soured cream.

===Ingredients===
| 6 potatoes 
| a little milk - if the mixture is too dry.
| 1 cup of grated cheese
| 2 large free range eggs
| Vegetable oil for frying
| Salt
| Plain flour
===Method===


| Wash the potatoes.
| Boil the potatoes whole in their skin until cooked. 
| Rub the skin off. 
| Mash the potatoes and mix with the grated cheese,milk, eggs and salt.
| Make small tortitas, add a little flour if the mixture is too wet.
| Fry them on both sides until brown and crispy.
| Serve with any salad of your choice.

''recipe by Eduardo Navarro''
