This is a simple, but delicious creamy ice\u2013cream and the ingredients have been kept to a minimum.

===Ingredients===
| 600 ml double cream
| 125 g caster sugar (or to taste)
| 3 teaspoons cinnamon, ground 
| Pinch of salt
===Method===


| If you are using an ice-cream maker, follow the manufacturer\u2019s instructions.
| To make by hand, place all of the ingredients in a container and mix well.
| Place it in the freezer and every half-hour so, remove from freezer and mix again.
| Repeat this process until the ice-cream is at the desired consistency.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with fruit salad or basboosa.  Also goes well with sorbets and apple pie.
===Variations===
Add a few drops of orange flower water if desired.
