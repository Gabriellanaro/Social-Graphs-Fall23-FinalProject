===Description===

This goes well with kidneys and liver or would make a nice vegetable dip.

===Ingredients===
| 500 g thick Greek yogurt (Total is good)
| 5 cm (2\") stick of cinnamon or 1 teaspoon of powdered cinnamon
| 6 tablespoons water
| Salt and freshly ground black pepper
===Method===


| In a coffee grinder, powder the cinnamon or use ready powdered cinnamon 
| Tip the yogurt and the water into a shallow bowl 
| Sprinkle the cinnamon over the yogurt and whisk so it's evenly distributed
| Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper
===Serving suggestions===

Serve as a dip for julienned carrots and red bell peppers
