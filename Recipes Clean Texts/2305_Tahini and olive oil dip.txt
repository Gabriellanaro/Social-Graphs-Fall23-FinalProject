===Description===

If you like peanut butter, you'll like this.

===Ingredients===
| 1 clove garlic, crushed to a smooth paste, with a pinch of sea salt, in a pestle and mortar
| 3 tablespoons tahini (light, preferred)
| 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
| 1 tablespoon lime juice
| Up to 10 tablespoons cold water
| 1 tablespoon of toasted sesame seeds (optional)
| Sea salt and ground black pepper to taste
===Method===


| In a small bowl, whisk the olive oil, garlic and tahini
| Add lime juice and half the water and whisk vigorously
| Whisk in extra water until the consistency is similar to yogurt
| Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds
===Serving suggestions===

Serve with crispbreads
