===Description===

This is a lovely crisp pastry, perfect for meat pies or quiches and now I use nothing else now due to protests from my hubby!

===Ingredients===
| 500g Plain flour
| 250g Butter
| 125g Mature Cheddar (yellow)
| 3-5tbsp cold water
| extra flour for dusting


===Mise en place===
* Food processor with plastic blade required
===Method===


| Sieve flour, cut butter into 1cm cubes and grate cheddar.
| Add everything to food processor and pulse until mixed into chucky crumbs.
| Add water one tablespoon at a time and pulse food processor until ball of dough is formed.
| Use immediately or freeze for future use
===Serving suggestions===
This makes 6 individual pie crusts and lids or
2x quiche crusts or
1x large pie crust and lid
===Chef's notes===
Using a orange coloured cheese will make the cheese noticeable in the pastry
