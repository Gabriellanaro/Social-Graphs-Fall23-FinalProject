===Description===


Use your vegetable steamer to prepare this spicy Moroccan carrot recipe.

===Ingredients===
| 500g carrots, halves and cut into thin rounds or julienned
| 2 tablespoons Belazu extra virgin olive oil 
| 1 teaspoon Belazu Rose Harissa 
| 1 tablespoon of lemon juice 
| Handful chopped coriander, plus extra for garnish
===Method===


| Place the carrots in a steamer set over a pan of boiling water and cook for 7-10 minutes until just soft.
| Mix together the remaining ingredients, then toss the warm carrots through the dressing.
| Sprinkle with extra chopped coriander to serve.
(''BBC Good Food'')
