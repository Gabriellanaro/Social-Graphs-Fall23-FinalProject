===Description===

This cheese sauce for the Thermomix is taken from ''''[http://www.forumthermomix.com/index.php?topic=6120.0 Monkeyboyzmum's recipe]''' on the Thermomix Forum.

===Ingredients===TM
| 180g Cheddar cheese or your own favourite
| 45g flour
| 50g butter
| 1 teaspoon mustard powder
| ½ teaspoon salt
| Pinch cayenne
| 500g milk
===Method===


| Grate the cheese '''5 seconds / Speed 8'''.
| Add the remaining ingredients and cook '''7 mins / Temp. 90\u00b0 / Speed 4'''.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with pasta or vegetables.
===Variations===
Add other spices as desired.
===Chef's notes===
The sauce will keep warm in a dish in the Varoma whilst you cook your pasta or vegetables in the internal steaming basket.
