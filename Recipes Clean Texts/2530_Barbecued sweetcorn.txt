===Description===


A tasty way to cook sweetcorn.

===Ingredients===
| 4 Sweetcorn cobs
| 30 g Butter
| Salt and freshly ground black pepper
===Method===


| Soak the corn cobs in cold water for 1 hour
| Grill for 5 minutes on a hot barbecue, turn to prevent burning
===Serving suggestions===

Serve with melted butter and sprinkle with salt and pepper
