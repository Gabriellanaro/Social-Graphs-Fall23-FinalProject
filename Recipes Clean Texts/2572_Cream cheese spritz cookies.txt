===Description===

I've put this recipe here in case anyone has lost user manual for their Oxo good grips cookie press.

This is a an Oxo tested recipe.
===Ingredients===

| 230 g (2 sticks) unsalted butter direct from the fridge
| 85 g (2 oz) Philadelphia Original cream cheese, softened
| 200g (1 cup) castor sugar
| 1 egg yolk
| 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
| 315 g (2.5 cups) plain flour (all-purpose flour)
| ¼ teaspoon fine salt
| ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
===Mise en place===

* Preheat your oven to 180\u00b0 C (350\u00b0 F - gas 4), [fan oven 160\u00b0 C & reduce cooking time by 10 mins per hour]

===Method===


| In a large mixing bowl, cream together cubed butter and cream cheese
| Add sugar and the egg yolk, beating until light and fluffy
| Mix in the vanilla extract
| Fit your chosen cookie press disk into the Oxo good grips cookie press.
| Press cookies out onto an ungreased baking sheet
| Bake for 12 to 15 minutes in the centre of the oven or until the cookies have turned golden brown
| Turn the baking tray round after 6 minutes
===Recipe source===

* Oxo good grips cookie press user manual
===Chef's notes ===
This recipe makes 24 mini cookies
