This is a cheats way to make frito pie on the hoof!

If you want cook the real thing, there is a recipe here.

===Ingredients===
| 1 99\u00a2 Grab-Bag of Fritos. (corn tortilla chips)
| '''other brands of tortilla chips are available''' \ud83d\ude1c
===Method===


| Buy a 99\u00a2 Grab-Bag of Fritos.
| Buy a basic 99\u00a2 cup of chili from Wendy's (or wherever you like to buy chili)
| Carefully open the bag without ripping the sides
| Pour the chili over the top of the chips in the bag.
| Pour on the hot sauce from the little packets, and a little more grated cheese.
| Carefully eat it right out of the bag with a plastic fork, and enjoy.
