===Description===

A grasshopper is a sweet, mint-flavoured, after-dinner drink. The name of the drink is derived from its green colour, which is provided by the cr\u00e8me de menthe. The drink reputedly originated at Tujague's, a landmark bar in the French Quarter of New Orleans, Louisiana, and gained popularity during the 1950s and 1960s throughout the American South.

===Ingredients===

| 30 ml (1 part) Cr\u00e8me de menthe (green)
| 30 ml (1 part) Cr\u00e8me de cacao (white)
| 30 ml (1 part) Fresh cream

===Method===


| Pour ingredients into a cocktail shaker with ice. 
| Shake briskly and then strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
