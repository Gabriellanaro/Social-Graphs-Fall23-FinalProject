===Description===

This recipe is a neat cookery cheat from South Caernarfon Creameries and makes smokey boston beans the quick way.  Adding the Cannellini beans to the baked beans also adds a taste variation. Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese finished this recipe perfectly.
===Ingredients===
| 1 tin cannellini beans rinsed and drained
| 1 tin baked beans in tomato sauce
| 100g Chorizo finely chopped
| 100g Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese, grated
| 1 small jar chargrilled red peppers drained and chopped
| 1 tablespoon tomato puree
| 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
| 2 teaspoons of chipotle paste

===Method===


| Mix all ingredients together well and heat through until piping hot and the cheese has melted
| Serve with crusty bread
===Variations===
Add a little extra grated Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese to the beans and grill until melted.
