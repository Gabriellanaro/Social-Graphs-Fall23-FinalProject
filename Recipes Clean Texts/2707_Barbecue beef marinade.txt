A great beef marinade for barbecues.

===Ingredients===
| 4 tablespoons dark soy sauce (light soy will do, though it's not as sweet)
| 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
| 1 teaspoon salt
| ½ teaspoon five spice powder (optional)
| ½ teaspoon brown sugar
| Freshly grated black pepper to taste
===Method===


| Create a marinade by mixing all ingredients together
| Marinate the beef for 3 or 4 hours before grilling or barbecuing.
===Servings===
Serves 4 - Sufficient to marinate beef for 4 people
===Variations===

Add good pinch of chili powder, chilli sauce or some harissa paste for a hotter marinade.
