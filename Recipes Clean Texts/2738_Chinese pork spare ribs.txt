===Ingredients===
| 750g (1.5 lb) belly pork strips or spare rib chops
| 5 tablespoons hoisin sauce, it's quick and easy to make
| 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
===Method===


| Cut the belly into 2\" pieces.
| In a bowl add the ribs and cover well with the hoisin sauce
| Place in a fridge and marinate for at least an hour
| Place in a preheated the oven to 220c ''temperature guide'' and cook for 35-45 minutes
| Toast the sesame seeds in a small pan over a low flame
| Place on a warm dish and serve
| Sprinkle with sesame seeds
===Variations===
The ribs can be cooked on a barbecue for about 10-15 minutes instead of the oven.
===Chef's tip===
To save a little time on the washing up, use Bake-O-Glide.
