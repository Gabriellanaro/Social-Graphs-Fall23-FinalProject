A stunningly simple, yet delicious sauce to spice up noodle and vegetable dishes.

===Ingredients===
| 4 tablespoons sesame seeds
| 2 tablespoons peanut butter
| 2 tablespoons light soy sauce
| 1 tablespoon sesame oil
| 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
| 1 tablespoon tomato puree
| 2 tablespoons sugar
| 2 tablespoons sherry or rice wine
| 150 ml (1/4 pint) vegetable stock)
===Method===


| Heat the sesame seeds in a wok until they release that wonderful toasted aroma
| Allow to cool a little and grind to a powder in a coffee grinder
| Add to a small pan and mix with the remaining ingredients
| Heat and stir until well mixed
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with noodles and chopped spring onions
