===Description===

A nice dip for frys and vegetables.

===Ingredients===
| 4 fat Cloves of garlic, peeled and crushed
| 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
| 2 tablespoons light soy sauce
| 2 teaspoons brown sugar
| 2 teaspoons chili oil
===Method===


| Mix all the ingredients together, mix well and allow to mingle for an hour before re-mixing and serving
