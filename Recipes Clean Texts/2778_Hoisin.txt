Although you can but hoisin sauce cheaply from Chinese or ethnic outlets, it's fun and easy to make.

===Ingredients===
| 3 tablespoons crushed yellow bean sauce
| 1 tablespoon plain flour
| 1 tablespoon sugar
| 2 tablespoons cider or white wine vinegar
| 1 teaspoon chili sauce
| 1 tablespoon sesame oil
| 1 clove garlic, crushed
| 2 tablespoons rice wine or sherry
===Method===


| Place all of the ingredients together in a bowl and whisk until mixed.
===Serving suggestions===

Can be stored in a refrigerator until required
