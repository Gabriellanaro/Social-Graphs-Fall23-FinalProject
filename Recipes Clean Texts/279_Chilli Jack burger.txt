===Description===

[[Image:Dragon Monterey Jack cheese.jpg|thumb|300px|right|[http://www.dragonwales.co.uk/products/monterey-jack/ Dragon Monterey Jack cheese]]]
A tasty chilli burger made with Dragon Monterey Jack cheese, produced in Wales by South Caernarfon Creameries.
===Ingredients===

| 500g Beef mince
| 2 tablespoons Sweet chilli sauce
| 1 medium sized egg beaten
| 1 red chilli finely chopped (optional for extra heat)
| 4 x 28g slices Dragon Monterey Jack cheese
| 4 x Burger buns split in half horizontally
| 4 large gherkins thinly sliced
| A Light dusting of plain flour

===Method===


| Mix the mince with the chopped chilli and 1 tablespoon of chilli sauce
| Add beaten egg and mix thoroughly, form into 4 evenly sized burgers and dust with plain flour
| Grill or bbq burgers for 6-10 minutes until cooked through
| Place a slice on Monterey Jack on each burger and grill until melted and bubbling
| Spread one slice of bun with chilli sauce and arrange gherkins slices on base of bun, place the burger inside bun and serve immediately
