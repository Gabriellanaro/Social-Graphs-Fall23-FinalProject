Although this requires marinading overnight, it is a simple and undemanding way of cooking pork and it gives excellent results

===Ingredients===
| 1 kg (2lb) boned roasting pork (loin is good), thickly sliced
| 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
| 6 tablespoons light soy sauce
| 2 tablespoons rice wine or dry sherry
| 1 tablespoon brown sugar
| 1 tablespoon honey
| ½ teaspoon five spice powder
===Method===


| Arrange the sliced pork in a shallow dish 
| Make a marinade with the other ingredients and pour over the sliced pork
| Refrigerate overnight, basting when you can
| In a large roasting tin, add ½\" of water, place a rack over this and arrange the slices on the rack
| Roast in a preheated oven at 180\u00b0 C (350\u00b0 F) for about an hour, basting frequently with the marinade
| Cover, keep warm and rest for 10 minutes
| Slice and arrange on a platter and serve
===Serving suggestions===

Serve hot with plain rice or noodles.

Ham sauce for noodles would go nicely with this.
