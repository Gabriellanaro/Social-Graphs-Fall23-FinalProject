===Description===

[[Image:Caramelised-red-onion-sausages.png|300px|thumb|right|Debbie & Andrew's Caramelised Red Onion Pork Sausages - perfect for this salsa dip]]
Andy BBQs some key ingredients to add a smoky twist to a Mexican classic. Chunky, tangy and bursting with flavour, get your guests doing the sausage salsa this summer with this BBQ must-have! Perfect with the sweetness of our Caramelised red onion Sausages.

This recipe is reproduced courtesy of debbie&andrew's sausages
===Ingredients===

| 4 ripe tomatoes
| 2 red chillies
| 4 spring onions
| 1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed
| 1 teaspoon tomato puree
| Pinch of smoked paprika
| Juice of 1 to 2 limes
| Pinch of golden caster sugar
| Small bunch coriander, roughly chopped
| Lime wedges, to serve
| Salt and freshly ground pepper

===Method===


| Pop your chillies, tomatoes and spring onions on the BBQ for 2-3 minutes for caramelisation and charcoal notes.
| Chop them as finely as you can and mix well with the remaining ingredients. Stunningly simple!
