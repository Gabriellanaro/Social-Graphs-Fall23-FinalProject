===Description===

Make this simple dipping sauce in minutes with just a few ingredients.  Goes well with prawns, shrimp and other seafood.
===Ingredients===
| 1 teaspoon sesame oil
| 2 teaspoons hoisin sauce
| 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
| 1 tablespoon water
| 1 teaspoon brown sugar
===Method===


| Add all ingredients to a small bowl
| Mix well
===Serving suggestions===

Serve as a dip with seafood dishes, especially prawns
