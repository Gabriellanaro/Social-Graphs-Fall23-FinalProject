===Description===

A quick and simple dip that is ideal for pork dishes.

===Ingredients===
| 120 ml (4 fl oz) light soy sauce
| 1 teaspoon sesame oil
| 1 clove garlic, crushed well
| 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
===Method===


| Add all ingredients to a small bowl
| Mix well
===Serving suggestions===

Serve as a dip with pork dishes
