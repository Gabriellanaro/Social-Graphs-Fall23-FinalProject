===Ingredients===
| 450g (1 lb) tender spring greens
| 1 teaspoon sea salt
| 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
| 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
| Â½ teaspoon sugar
| 1 tablespoon water 
===Method===


| Wash the greens and cut into 2.5 cm (1\") lengths, discarding any stalks
| Cover with cold water and salt and leave for 10 minutes
| Heat a wok or large frying pan and add oil
| When hot, add the garlic and stir-fry for a minute or two. Don't allow the garlic to burn
| Add the greens and sprinkle with sugar
| Add the tablespoon of water and heat for 3 to 4 minutes until the leave change to a transparent green
| Arrange on a warm dish and serve
