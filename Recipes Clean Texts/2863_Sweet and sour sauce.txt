This sauce goes well with much more than just Chinese dishes and it's so quick to make.

If you're looking for something a little different, try the very subtle Sweet and sour sauce with black vinegar.

===Ingredients===
| 250 ml (8 fl oz) water
| 100g (4 oz) sugar
| 120 ml (4 fl oz) white wine vinegar or malt vinegar
| 2 tablespoons tomato puree
| 1 tablespoon cornflour
| 1 tablespoon soy sauce
===Method===


| Bring ¾ of the water to the boil
| Add the sugar and stir until dissolved
| Add the vinegar and stir for a minute
| In a saucer, mix the remaining water, tomato pur\u00e9e and cornflour into a smooth paste
| Mix into the hot pan and stir continuously until thickened
| Pour into a gravy dish
===Serving suggestions===
Great with anything!
===Variations===
Add some chopped chillies for a hot sauce.
