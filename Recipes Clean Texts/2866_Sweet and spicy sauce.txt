===Description===


Great for carrots or cabbage.

===Ingredients===
| 120 ml (4 fl oz) water
| 100g (4 oz) sugar
| 3 teaspoons white wine vinegar or malt vinegar
| 2 tablespoons tomato puree
| 1 tablespoon cornflour
| Pinch of salt
| 3 tablespoons sugar
===Method===


| Mix all ingredients in a pan and simmer until the sauce thickens
===Serving suggestions===

Pour over cooked vegetables such as cabbage or carrots

===Variations===

Add some chopped chillies for a hot sauce
