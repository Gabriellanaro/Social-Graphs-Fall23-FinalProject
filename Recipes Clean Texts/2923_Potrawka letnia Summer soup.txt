===Description===

Potrawka letnia is a sort of Polish gazpacho.

===Ingredients===
| 750 g beetroot, cooked, peeled and sliced
| 1 tablespoon lemon juice
| 480 ml buttermilk or yogurt
| 120 ml sour cream 
| Salt to taste 
| 1 small cucumber, peeled and roughly chopped
| 1 hard-boiled egg, peeled and roughly chopped 
| 2 tablespoons chopped chives
===Method===


| Place beetroot in blender and blend until smooth. 
| Add the remaining ingredients and blend again.
| Serve chilled.
===Serving suggestions===
Would make an excellent pick-me-up drunk from a glass for breakfast.
