===Description===

[[Image:Ziemniaki z kwasna smietana (Potatoes in sour cream) recipe.jpg|thumb|300px|right|Ziemniaki z kwasna smietana (Potatoes in sour cream)]]
A simple and traditional Polish recipe for potatoes.

===Ingredients===
| 6 medium potatoes, peeled
| 2 medium onions, peeled and chopped
| 50g butter
| 200ml sour cream
| 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped or chives, snipped
| Seasoning
===Method===


| Boil the potatotes for 5 minutes.
| Drain and slice.
| Saut\u00e9 the onions in the butter until soft.
| Add the potatoes, sour cream, dill or chives and seasoning.
| Cook over a low heat, covered, until the potatoes are tender - about 20 minutes.
| Serve hot.
===Chef's notes===
I am not sure about the sour cream quantity - you may need more or less than 200ml.

I made this for 2 people and used about half the potatoes, a large onion, about 25 g butter with an equal amount of olive oil. 150 g sour cream and loads of dill as I love it and it goes so well with potatoes.  I used a little extra milk to just cover the potatoes.  This will be made again! --Chef 18:20, 16 May 2010 (BST)
===Serving suggestions===
This went very well with garlic lamb kebabs.
