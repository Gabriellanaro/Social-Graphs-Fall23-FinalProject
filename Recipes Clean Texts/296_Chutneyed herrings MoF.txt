===Description===

From the Ministry of Food leaflet \"All about Herrings\" - reprinted in 1948!

===Ingredients===
| 2 level teaspoons salt
| 4 herrings, cleaned and boned
| 3 level tablespoons chutney
===Method===


| Rub salt on the inside of the herrings and spread with chutney
| Fold back into shape, place on a greased ovenproof dish and cover with greased paper
| Bake in a moderate oven (180\u00b0 C / 350\u00b0 F / Gas 4) for 25- 30 minutes
