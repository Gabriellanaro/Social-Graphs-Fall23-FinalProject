===Description===

These Serbian / Croatian sweetmeats are often served on festive occasions
===Ingredients===

| 300g ground walnuts
| 130g brown sugar
| 150g chopped almonds
| 150g ground almonds
| 200g plain chocolate, melted
| 160ml water
| 1 teaspoon ground cloves
| 1 tablespoon orange zest
| Ground almonds or sugar for rolling

===Method===


| Dissolve the sugar in the water over a medium heat
| Remove from the heat and add the remaining ingredients
| Combine well and refrigerate for 1 hour
| Shape into small balls, about 15mm in diameter
| Roll in the ground almonds or the sugar
| Keep in the fridge until ready to serve
