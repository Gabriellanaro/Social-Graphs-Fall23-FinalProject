===Description===

Vasina Torta (\u0412\u0430\u0441\u0438\u043d\u0430 \u0442\u043e\u0440\u0442\u0430, Vasa's torte) - the most loved cake in Serbia.

'''the base'''
===Ingredients===
| 6 free range eggs
| 6 tablespoons of sugar
| 6 tablespoons of ground almonds, or nuts of your choice
| 2 tablespoons of flour
| 10g baking soda
| 10ml fresh orange juice
'''filling'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 300g ground almonds, or nuts of your choice
| 0.5 litre milk
| 200g margarine
| 80g chocolate
| a little oil
| 4 egg yolks
| 4 egg whites beaten to firm peaks
| 4 tablespoons of sugar (add to your taste)


===Mise en place===
* Preheat the oven to 200\u00b0 C (400\u00b0 F - gas 6)
===Method===


| Mix the ingredients for the base, and put it in a tray.
| Bake it for about 20 minutes at 200\u00b0C.
| After it's baked, pour some orange juice over it.
| Mix the nuts, milk, margarine and some sugar, and bring them to the boiling point.
| In another pot, melt the chocolate with a little oil.
| In a third pot, mix the egg yolks with the sugar. Then mix all three parts of the filling, boiling it for about 10 minutes.
| Pour this over the base and wait for everything to cool.
| Finally, add the whipped cream and chocolate shavings.
