===Description===


This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 8 anchovy fillets, drained
| 150g mushrooms, sliced
| 3 Cloves garlic
| 300g red wine
| 100g olive oil
| 250g chopped tomatoes
| 1 teaspoon oregano
| Salt
| Pepper
===Method===


| Pour the oil into the bowl and heat '''3 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| Next, add the garlic, anchovies and mushrooms and for another '''3 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 3'''.
| Add the remaining ingredients and cook '''15 minutes / Temp. Varoma /  Reverse blade /  Speed Spoon'''.
| Allow to cool a little, transfer to a container and refrigerate.
===Serving suggestions===
The sauce can be used to marinate meat before cooking or added during cooking.  The sauce will keep for 10 days..
