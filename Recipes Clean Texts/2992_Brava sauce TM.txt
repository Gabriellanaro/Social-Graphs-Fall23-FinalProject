===Description===

An easy Spanish sauce for Patatas Bravas, made in the Thermomix
===Ingredients===TM
| 40g olive oil
| 1 medium onion peeled and sliced
| Garlic to taste
| 2 compacted teaspoons flour
| 1 teaspoon smoked sweet paprika
| 1\u00bd teaspoons smoked hot paprika (or to taste)
| 2 teaspoons sherry vinegar
| 1 x 400g tin of tomatoes
| 1 teaspoon sugar
| 1 tablespoon stock paste (or 2-3 Oxo cubes)

===Method===


| Put the oil, onions and garlic into the bowl 18 minutes / 100\u00b0C / Speed Spoon / Reverse Blade / MC Off
| Add the remaining ingredients 30 minutes / 90\u00b0C / Speed Spoon / MC On
| Then blend 40 secs / Speed 5-7-9 
| Taste, and season if necessary
===Serving suggestions===
Traditionally served with fried cubes of potato, but is also excellent as a dip, in which case, allow to cool to room temperature
