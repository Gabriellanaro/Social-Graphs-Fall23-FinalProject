===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 700g onions
| 30g olive oil
| 1 teaspoon sugar
| Black pepper to taste
| A pinch of cinnamon
| 30g sweet wine
| 40g butter
| Salt
===Method===


| Peel and cut the onions.
| Place them in the bowl and chop '''4 seconds / Speed 4'''.
| Add the oil and butter.
| Put the butterfly on the blades.
| Cook '''10 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| Next, add the wine, salt, pepper, cinnamon and sugar.
| Cook for a further '''25 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 1'''.
===Serving suggestions===
This is ideal to accompany meat dishes, fish, salads, hamburgers, aperitifs and cheese.
