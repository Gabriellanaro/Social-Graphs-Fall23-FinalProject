===Description===

A somewhat different way of serving carrots as an accompaniment.

===Ingredients===TM
| 800g carrots
| 100g Serrano ham in one piece
| 750g water
| 25g butter
| 2 vegetable stock cubes
===Method===


| Peel and wash the carrots.
| Cut half of them with a very sharp knife to get your desired size.
| Make balls with the rest of the carrots with the help of a melon baller.  Reserve.
| Put the ham, cut into pieces, in the bowl '''7 seconds / Speed 5'''.
| Remove and reserve.
| Add the water, butter and the stock cubes to the bowl '''5 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| Put the internal steaming basket into the bowl with the carrots '''15 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| Drain the carrots and serve with the chopped ham.
===Serving suggestions===
Use this recipe as an accompaniment to meat or fish or rolled up ham.
