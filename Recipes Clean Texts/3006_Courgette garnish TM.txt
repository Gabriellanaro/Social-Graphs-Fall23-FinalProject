===Description===

This recipe can be made using courgettes or marrows.

===Ingredients===TM
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 3 cloves garlic
| 400g courgettes (or marrow), cut into fingers
| Salt and pepper, to taste
===Method===


| Put the oil in the bowl '''4 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''
| Add the garlic, chop '''6 seconds / Speed 5''' and then '''6 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''
| Put the '''Butterfly''' on the blades, add the courgettes or marrow, salt and pepper '''15 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed Spoon'''.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
