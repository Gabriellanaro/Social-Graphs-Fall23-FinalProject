===Description===

A delicious soup made in a Thermomix

===Ingredients===TM
| 200g almonds
| 1 litre chicken stock
| Salt
| Pepper
| Nutmeg
| 300g single cream
| 100g water
| The juice of half a lemon
'''For the sofrito'''
* 200g leeks (white part only)
* 1 clove garlic
* 70g butter
* 30g extra virgin olive oil
===Method===


| Tip the almonds into a very dry bowl '''10 seconds / Speed 6'''
| Tip them into a bowl and reserve
| Next, put all of the sofrito ingredients into the TM bowl '''5 seconds / Speed 5''', then '''2 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1''', [scrape down the bowl and lid] then '''8 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''
| Add the stock and the almonds '''1 minute / Speed 7'''.
| Add the salt, pepper and nutmeg '''20 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 3'''.
| Add the cream, the 100g water and the lemon juice '''7 minutes / 90\u00b0 / Speed 3'''.
| Check seasoning (you can add a little more water if you like)
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
