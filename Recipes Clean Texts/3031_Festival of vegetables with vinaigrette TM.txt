===Description===

A wonderful vegetable dish from Spain

'''For the vinaigrette'''
===Ingredients===TM
| 100g green peppers
| 100g red peppers
| 100g onion
| 1 egg, hard-boiled
| 100g extra virgin olive oil
| 30g vinegar
| 1 small bunch of parsley, chopped
| Salt
'''For the bowl'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 600g water
'''For the Varoma'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 1kg vegetables, cut into slices (aubergines, potatoes, artichokes, carrots etc)
'''For the Varoma tray'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 200g garden peas
| 300g courgettes
===Method===


| Put all the vinaigrette ingredients into the bowl '''4 seconds / Speed 4'''.
| Remove and reserve.
| Without washing the bowl, add the water '''6 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 2'''
| Place all of the vegetables in the Varoma basket (the softer ones in the tray) '''20 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 2'''
| Sprinkle a little salt on the vegetables and serve with the vinaigrette in a sauce boat so that everyone can add it to their own taste
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs
