===Description===

This recipe requires you to make your own butter first.

===Ingredients===TM
| 600g double cream (35% fat minimum)

'''Fines herbes butter'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 30g chives, chopped
| 10g celery salt

'''Tarragon butter'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 20g capers
| 10g fresh tarragon, chopped

'''Anchovy butter'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 6 semi-preserved anchovy fillets

===Method===


| Make the butter according to this recipe using 600g cream.
| Once made, the butter can be flavoured to your taste.
| Add your chosen ingredients to the butter in the bowl and mix '''5 seconds / Speed 5'''.
===Serving suggestions===
The flavoured butters can be used as a sauce in hot plates of pasta, baked potatoes, meat or fish.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
