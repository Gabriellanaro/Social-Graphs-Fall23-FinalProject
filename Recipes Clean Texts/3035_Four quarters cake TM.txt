===Description===

Known in Spain as 'Bizcocho cuatro cuartos' it takes its name from the fact that it contains equal parts of the the four main ingredients: flour, sugar, butter and eggs.  It can be made with with various flavourings such as lemon, vanilla, cinnamon etc.  It is similar to the English Pound Cake.

===Ingredients===TM
| 130g sugar
| The peel of 1 lemon
| 3 free range eggs
| 130g butter at room temperature
| 130g plain flour
| 3 teaspoons baking powder
| A pinch of salt


===Mise en place===
* Preheat the oven to 180\u00b0 C (350\u00b0 F - gas 4) 
===Method===


| Tip the sugar into the very dry bowl '''20 seconds / Speed 5-10 progressively'''
| Add the lemon peel and again '''20 seconds / Speed 5-10 progressively'''
| Next, add the eggs '''2 minutes / 37\u00b0 / Speed 3'''
| Add the butter and beat '''30 seconds / Speed 3'''
| Incorporate the flour, baking powder and salt '''10 seconds / Speed 2½'''
| Finish by folding the mixture delicately with the spatula
| Pour into a greased and floured cake tin and bake in the oven for about 25-30 minutes
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
