===Description===

You can use this picada in many things, amongst which is a vinaigrette for salads by adding extra virgin olive oil, vinegar and a little soya sauce.

===Ingredients===TM
| 3 cloves garlic
| A handful of clean, dry parsley leaves


===Method===


| Put everything in the bowl 5 seconds / Speed 5
| Once the two ingredients are mixed, pour them into a container, cover with oil to preserve them and use in stews, sofritos etc.
===Servings===
Serves 4 people
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
===Chef's notes===
This recipe makes about 30g of picada.
