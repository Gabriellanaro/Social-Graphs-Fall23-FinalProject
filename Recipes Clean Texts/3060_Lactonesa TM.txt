===Description===

Lactonesa is a type of mayonnaise made with milk instead of eggs

===Ingredients===TM
| 150g milk
| 1 clove garlic [optional]
| Salt
| 300g oil
| The juice of Â½ a lemon
| A few glugs of vinegar (optional)
===Method===


| Weigh the oil and reserve.
| Put the milk, garlic and salt into the bowl '''1 minute / 37\u00b0 / Speed 5''' (if you don\u0092t want the garlic, program '''30 seconds / 37\u00b0 / Speed 5''')
| Program '''3 minutes /  37\u00b0 / Speed 5''' and drop the oil, little by little, onto the lid, without removing the MC.  (The oil will fall little by little into the bowl)
| Next, add the lemon or vinegar and mix for a '''few seconds''' at the '''same speed'''.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
===Variations===
You can add a little colouring or mustard to give it colour.
