===Description===

This recipe literally means 'Fried milk' and is very popular in Spain

===Ingredients===TM
| 1 litre milk
| 150g flour
| 150g sugar
| 1 teaspoon cornflour
| 1 egg, beaten
| Oil for frying
| 5g extra sugar
| 2 teaspoons powdered cinnamon
===Method===


| Put all of the ingredients into the bowl and mix '''10 seconds / Speed 4'''
| Put the butterfly on the blades '''10 minutes / Temp. Varoma /Speed 2½'''
| Continue '''2 minutes / no temperature / Speed 2½'''
| Pour the mixture into a dish to the thickness of a finger
| When it is cold, cut into squares, pass through cornflour and beaten egg and fry in plenty of oil until golden
| Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
===Variations===
You can also coat it with flour, breadcrumbs and egg; or flour, crushed biscuits and egg
