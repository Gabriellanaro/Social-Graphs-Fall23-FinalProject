===Description===

This is another Spanish version for Thermomix of this famous sauce

===Ingredients===TM
| 3 egg yolks
| The juice of Â½ an orange
| 80g tomato ketchup
| 2 tablespoons brandy
| A few dashes of Tabasco
| Salt
| 300g oil
===Method===


| Put all of the ingredients into the bowl except for the oil, and mix '''10 seconds / Speed 4'''
| With the MC in place, set to '''Speed 4''' and pour the oil, little by little, onto the lid
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
===Variations===
If you like, you can add half a teaspoon of horseradish
