===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===
| 200g milk
| 400g olive oil
| A few drops of lemon juice
| 25g onion
| 1 teaspoon vinegar
| White pepper
| Salt

===Method===

| Peel and cut the onion, tip it into the TM and chop '''5 seconds / Speed 5'''.
| Reserve and wash the TM.
| Put the milk in the TM and heat for '''3 minutes / 37\u00b0 / Speed 4'''.
| Next, add the chopped onion, salt and pepper.
| Beat '''37\u00b0 / Speed 5'''.
| Without stopping the machine, add the oil to the lid, little by little.
| When the mayonnaise has thickened a little, add the vinegar and lemon.
| You use roughly double the amount of oil to milk, but if you want a paler colour, you will have to reduce the amount of oil.
===Serving suggestions===
This is a variation of traditional mayonnaise.  It can be used to accompany many dishes:  boiled potatoes, steamed vegetables, griddled or steamed fish, griddled seafood and rice and pasta salads.
