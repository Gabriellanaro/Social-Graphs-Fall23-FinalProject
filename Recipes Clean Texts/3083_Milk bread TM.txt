===Description===

A Spanish version of milk bread for Thermomix

===Ingredients===
| 250g milk
| 50g butter
| 50g sugar
| 30g fresh baker's yeast

| 450g strong flour
| 1 teaspoon salt
===Mise en place===
* Preheat the oven to 200\u00b0

===Method===

| Put the milk, butter and the sugar into the bowl '''2 minutes / 37\u00b0 / Speed 2'''
| Add the yeast and mix '''5 seconds / Speed 4'''.
| Incorporate the flour and salt '''15 seconds / Speed 6''' and then '''3 minutes / Speed Dough'''
| Shape the dough into the desired shape (also you can use a mould), make some slashes and glaze the surface with milk.
| Leave until double its volume
| Put the bread in the oven
| The cooking time depends on the size of the bread, between 15-30 minutes
| After taking out of the oven, you can glaze it again with milk or egg white
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
