===Description===

A Thermomix version of this popular Spanish tapa

===Ingredients===TM
| 4 cloves garlic
| Parsley leaves
| 400g mushrooms, chopped
| 1 cayenne chilli pepper
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| Salt 
| Pepper
===Method===


| Put the garlic and the parsley into the bowl '''4 seconds / Speed 5'''
| Put the butterfly on the blades and add the remaining ingredients '''12 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon'''
| Correct the seasoning and check that the mushrooms are well done, if not, cook for a '''few minutes more'''
| If there is too much liquid, remove the mushrooms, leaving in the liquid, reduce it '''Temp. Varoma / 5 minutes approximately'''
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs
===Chef's notes===
You can double the quantity.  You only have to double all of the ingredients and cook for 3 minutes more
