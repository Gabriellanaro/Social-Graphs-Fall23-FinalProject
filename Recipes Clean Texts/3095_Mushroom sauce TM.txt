===Description===

A Spanish recipe for mushroom sauce, which also includes prawns

===Ingredients===TM
| 150g prawns
| 100g white wine
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 1 tablespoon butter
| 1 clove of garlic
| 2 leeks (only the white part)
| 250g mushrooms
| 200g single cream
| Sal, to taste
| Pepper, to taste
===Method===


| Put the shells and the heads of the prawns into the bowl (reserve the bodies) together with the white wine and make a broth '''4 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 3½'''.
| Strain through a fine sieve and reserve.
| Thoroughly wash the bowl and lid.
| Next, tip the oil, butter, garlic and the leeks into the bowl '''3 seconds / Speed 5''' to chop them all.
| Using the spatula, push down the ingredients inside the bowl '''5 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 3½'''.
| Afterwards, chop '''15 seconds / Speed 5''' and push down the ingredients inside the bowl.
| Add the mushrooms in thick slices, the cream, salt, pepper and the reserved prawn broth '''10 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon'''.
| One minute before the end of the cooking time add the prawn bodies.
| Check that the mushrooms are tender.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve this sauce with any type of fish.  If you like, you can add the juice of half a lemon.
