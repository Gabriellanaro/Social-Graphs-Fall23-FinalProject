This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 50g butter
| 30g dijon mustard
| 210g single cream
| Black pepper
| Salt
===Method===


| Put the butter in the bowl and program '''3 minutes / 80\u00b0 / Speed 2'''.
| Next, add the mustard and program '''1 minute /  90\u00b0 / Speed 1'''.
| Finally, incorporate the cream, a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
| Run '''5 minutes / 90\u00b0 / Speed 3'''.
| Allow to cool and keep in the fridge.
===Serving suggestions===
It is recommended that you use this sauce to accompany red and white meat.
===Variations===
There is a variation which includes capers and stoned and chopped olives.  They are added after the cream and mixed in for a few seconds at '''Speed 3'''.
