===Description===


Literally 'potatoes for the poor', this is a simple Spanish potato recipe and the following recipe is for cooking in a Thermomix.  The conventional method of making this dish will be added shortly.

===Ingredients===TM
| 100g olive oil
| 1 large onion, peeled
| 800g potatoes, peeled and sliced
| Garlic, to taste, sliced
===Method===


| Heat the oil in the bowl for '''3 minutes''' at '''100 degrees''' on '''speed 1'''.
| Meanwhile sprinkle the potato slices with a little salt on both sides.
| Add the onion and chop for '''6 seconds''' on '''speed 4\u00bd''';.
| Scrap down the inside of the bowl.
| Reduce the '''speed''' to '''1''' and allow to cook for '''5 minutes / MC Off'''.
| Place the butterfly whisk in the bowl.
| Add the potatoes, and cook on '''speed 1''' at '''100 degrees''' for '''20 minutes'''.
| 5 minutes before the end of the cooking time, add the garlic.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve as an alternative to boiled or chipped potatoes.
