===Description===

A nice and spicy potato dish made in a Thermomix and is often served as a tapa.

===Ingredients===TM
| 900g potatoes, cubed
| 1 large onion, cut into quarters
| Garlic, to taste
| 50g olive oil, plus extra for frying the potatoes
| 100ml white wine
| 400g tinned tomatoes 
| 2 guindilla peppers
| Salt
===Method===


| Put the onion and garlic in the bowl, and chop '''7 seconds / Speed 7'''.
| Add the oil MC off, '''10 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 2Â½'''.
| Add the wine, MC on, '''4 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 3'''.
| Add the tomatoes and salt, '''10 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 2'''.
| Incorporate the guindillas and blend '''40 seconds / Speed 1-9 progressively''', for a smooth sauce.  Use a lower speed for less time if you prefer it to have some texture.
| In the meantime, fry the potatoes in olive oil, empty them into a dish and pour over the sauce.
