===Description===

These are fried potatoes which can be served as an accompaniment or used in tortillas.

===Ingredients===TM
| 100g olive oil
| 2 cloves garlic, sliced
| 600g potatoes for frying, cut into slices 3-4mm thick
| 100g water
| Salt, to taste
| A bunch of parsley leaves, chopped
| 1 teaspoon vinegar
===Method===


| Place the Butterfly on the blades, put the oil and garlic in the bowl and program 4 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon.
| Add the potatoes, water, salt and parsley 14 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon.
| When it has finished, add the vinegar and stir with the spatula.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
===Variations===
Without adding the vinegar and parsley you can use these potatoes in tortillas:  remove the potatoes from the bowl and reserve.  Put 6 eggs in the bowl with a teaspoon of salt and mix 14 seconds / Speed 4.  Pour the eggs over the reserved potatoes, mix with the help of the spatula, and cook it in a frying pan.
