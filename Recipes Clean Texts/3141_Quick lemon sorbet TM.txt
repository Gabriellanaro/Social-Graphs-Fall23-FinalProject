===Description===

A Spanish Thermomix recipe for an easy lemon sorbet

===Ingredients===TM
| 3-4 lemons, peeled with pith and pips removed
| 200g sugar
| 800g ice cubes
===Method===


| Put all the ingredients into the bowl '''2 minutes / Speed 5-10 progressively''', keeping the '''first 30 seconds''' at '''Speed 5'''.
| Check that the texture is that of a thick cream.
| If the sorbet is liquid, add more ice cubes and repeat the process at the same speed.
| Pour into glasses.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
