===Description===

A Spanish recipe for cooking cabbage in a Thermomix.

===Ingredients===TM
| 100g raisins
| 700g red cabbage, washed and core removed
| 2 large cloves garlic, finely sliced
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 2 Golden Delicious apples, cored and cut into small pieces
| 150g water
| Salt
| Pepper
===Method===


| Soak the raisins in water until plump.
| Divide the cabbage into two.
| For each batch, break up into pieces the size of an egg and put into the bowl '''4 seconds / Speed 5'''.  Remove and reserve.
| Do the same with the other half.
| Put the garlic, oil, apples, and raisins into the bowl '''15 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon'''.
| When it finished add the water, cabbage, salt and pepper '''15-20 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon'''.
| Check that it is cooked and to your taste (it should be a little al dente), but if not, cook for a few minutes longer.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
