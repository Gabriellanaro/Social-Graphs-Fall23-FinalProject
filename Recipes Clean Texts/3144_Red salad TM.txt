===Description===

This salad takes its name from the fact that it includes cooked beetroot

===Ingredients===TM
| 250g celeriac, peeled
| 200g cooked beetroot
| 150g Skinless smoked sausages
| 70g green olives, stoned
| 50g pickled gherkins
| 2 tablespoons capers
| 100g cooked sweetcorn
| 50g sunflower seeds
| 4 whole endives, well washed and dried
| 250g mayonnaise
===Method===


| Chop all of the ingredients separately '''Speed 4-5'''(the time will depend on the hardness of the vegetables) except for the capers, sweetcorn, sunflower seeds and endives.  The olives and capers can be chopped together
| Next, mix all the ingredients in a salad bowl stirring in the mayonnaise and arrange the endive leaves around the contour to form a flower
| Serve cold
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs
===Variations===
You can substitute the celeriac with turnips, carrots or cooked potatoes
