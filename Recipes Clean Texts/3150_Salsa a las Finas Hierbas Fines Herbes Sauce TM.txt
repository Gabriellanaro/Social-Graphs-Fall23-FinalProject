===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 1 cooked egg yolk
| ½ onion
| 1 small handful of fresh parsley
| 1 tablespoon capers
| 2 tablespoons fresh oregano
| ½ tablespoon fresh tarragon
| 100g olive oil
| 25g Balsamic vinegar
| Black pepper
| Salt
===Method===


| Peel the onion, cut it and put it together with the rest of the ingredients in the bowl.
| Blend '''1 minute / Speed 3''' then for a further minute, gradually increasing from '''Speed 3-10'''.
| Keep in the fridge.
===Variations===
The mixture of herbs is what makes this sauce delicious and which gives it an intense green colour.  It is a perfect accompaniment for meat, fish or salads.

It is served cold.
