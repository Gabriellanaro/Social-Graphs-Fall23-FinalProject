===Description===

This is a translation and light adaptation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 1 small sweet onion
| A pinch of black pepper
| A pinch of salt 
| ½ teaspoon sugar
| ½ teaspoons sweet, smoked paprika 
| 1 teaspoon ground mustard seeds
| 150g olive oil
| ½ teaspoon ginger
| 3 tablespoons cider vinegar)
===Method===


| Peel and cut the onion and chop it in the bowl '''6 seconds / Speed 6'''.  
| Add the remaining ingredients in the order given.
| Blend the mixture '''5 seconds / Speed 6''' or until it has achieved the desired consistency.
===Serving suggestions===
It is very suitable for meat, fish or any other dish.  In some countries this is a very common sauce.  
===Variations===
To vary the flavours omit the paprika and add chopped parsley, finely chopped red pepper, garlic, etc.
