===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)

===Ingredients===TM
| 1 large onion
| 40g butter
| 40g olive oil
| 200g single cream
| 200g dulce de membrillo (Spanish quince paste)
| 100g seasonal mushrooms
| Pepper
| Salt
===Method===


| Thoroughly wash the mushrooms and cut them into slices.  Reserve.
| Place the onion, butter and oil in the bowl.
| Chop '''10 seconds / Speed 3'''.
| Cook '''20 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| When the 20 minutes are up, add the cream and dulce de membrillo and blend '''15 seconds / Speed 10'''.
| Add the mushrooms and blend '''5 seconds / Speed 8'''.
| Afterwards, season with salt and pepper, and program '''12 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 1'''.
===Variations===
It is an exquisite sauce to serve with cold meat, roast chicken, stuffed turkey and in general with all types of game.
