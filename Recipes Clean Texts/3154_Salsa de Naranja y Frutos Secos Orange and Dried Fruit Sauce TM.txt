===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 200g orange juice
| 70g dried apricots
| 50g seedless raisins
| 70g stoneless dried prunes
| 400g Pedro Xim\u00e9nez wine (sweet, dark sherry or M\u00e1laga wine)
| 360g cooking cream (I think this means double cream as single can curdle if cooked on a high heat)
| Salt
| Pepper
===Method===


| Put the juice, apricots, raisins, prunes and wine in the bowl '''30 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| Nest, add the cream, a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
| Blend '''20 seconds / Speed 10'''.
| Cook '''8 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 2'''.
| Remove from the bowl and pour into a serving dish.
===Serving suggestions===
This sauce is served hot.  It is suitable for poultry and game dishes.  
===Variations===
The Pedro Xim\u00e9nez wine can be substituted with any other sweet wine with similar characteristics.
