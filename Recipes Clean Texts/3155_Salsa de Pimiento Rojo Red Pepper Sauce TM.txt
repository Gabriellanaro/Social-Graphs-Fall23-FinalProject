===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 250g jarred or tinned red peppers
| 1 clove garlic
| 50g olive oil
| 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
| 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
| Salt
| Pepper
===Method===


| Drain the peppers thoroughly and put them in the bowl, together with the garlic oil, parsley, salt and pepper.
| Cook '''3 minutes / Temp. Varoma /  Reverse blade /  Speed spoon'''.
| Blend at '''Speed 3''', increasing to '''Speed 6'''  to obtain a light, smooth sauce.
| When you have finished, pour into a bowl and garnish with the thyme.
| Season with a little salt.
===Serving suggestions===
Red pepper sauce can be served at room temperature or cold.  It is suitable as an accompaniment to any type of vegetables and salads.
===Serving===
Servings: 6 - Makes 200 ml
