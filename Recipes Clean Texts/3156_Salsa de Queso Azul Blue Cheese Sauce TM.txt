===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 150g single cream
| 100g fresh cheese
| 1 tablespoon butter
| 1 tablespoon cornflour
| 100g blue cheese
| 1 teaspoon brandy
| Salt
| Pepper
| 2 tablespoons milk (optional)
===Method===


| Put all of the ingredients, except the milk, in the bowl.
| Cook '''6 minutes / 90\u00b0 / Speed 4'''.
| If the sauce is very thick, add the 2 tablespoons of milk and mix for a '''few seconds / Speed 4'''.
| Once you have obtained the desired consistency, turn out into a bowl and serve.
===Serving suggestions===
This exquisite sauce goes well with many dishes, but above all with T-bone,  fillet and sirloin steaks.  Also it is a good dressing for some salads.  At the same time it can be eaten with potatoes or pasta.  
===Chef's notes===
Some types of blue cheese which you can use in this sauce are Cabrales, Roquefort, Danish Blue and Gorgonzola.
