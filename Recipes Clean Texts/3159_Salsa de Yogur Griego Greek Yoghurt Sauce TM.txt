===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 125g Greek yogurt
| 1 tablespoon mustard
| 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
| 1 teaspoon chopped chives
| A dash of Tabasco
| Salt
===Method===


| Add the yogurt, mustard, parsley, chives and Tabasco.
| Add salt to taste and blend '''5 seconds / Speed 6'''.
| Next, program '''6 minutes / 60\u00b0 / Speed 4'''.
| Serve warm.
===Serving suggestions===
This sauce is very versatile; it works very well with meat, fish and vegetables.  For serving with raw vegetables you can add 2 or 3 tbsps whipped cream and a little more salt.  For boiled or steamed vegetables you can add mayonnaise and various herbs and spices to it such as tarragon, rosemary or coriander.
