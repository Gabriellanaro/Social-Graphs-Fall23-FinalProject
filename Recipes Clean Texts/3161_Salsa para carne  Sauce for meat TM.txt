===Description===

This is a Spanish recipe for Salsa para carne (a thick Sauce for meat).

===Ingredients===TM
| 300g onions
| 150g mushrooms
| 2 cloves garlic
| 100g carrots
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 200g red wine
| 1 meat stock cube
===Method===


| Put all of the ingredients into the bowl except for the wine and the stock cube '''4 seconds / Speed 5'''
| Using the spatula, scrape down the inside of the bowl.
| Program '''20 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| Add the wine and stock cube '''5 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 4'''
| If you want a smoother sauce blitz '''1 minute / Speed 7''' and check the seasoning
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
===Variations===
You can substitute the red wine with cream, yogurt, cider, white wine etc
