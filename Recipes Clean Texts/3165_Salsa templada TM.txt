===Description===

This is a warm Spanish sauce for salads and vegetables

===Ingredients===TM
| 70g walnuts
| 150g extra virgin olive oil
| 50g butter
| 50g soya sauce
| 1 tablespoon mustard
| A dash of Tabasco
===Method===


| Chop the nuts in the bowl '''3-4 seconds / Speed 4'''
| Remove and reserve
| Next, put the rest of the ingredients into the bowl '''4 minutes / 60\u00b0 / Speed 4'''
| Add the reserved nuts '''1 minute / 60\u00b0 / Speed 1'''.
===Serving suggestions===
Use this sauce for salads of endives, raw spinach, asparagus, chicory, cabbage, lettuce etc.  Also for cooked vegetables.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
