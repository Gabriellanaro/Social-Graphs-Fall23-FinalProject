===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 160g oil
| 40g butter
| 30g soya sauce
| 3 tablespoons mustard
| 25g walnuts
| 25g pine nuts
| 25g almonds
| A dash of Tabasco
===Method===


| Chop the nuts in the bowl '''5 seconds / Speed progressively 5-9'''.  Remove and reserve.
| Put all the ingredients, except for the nut mixture into the bowl and cook '''4 minutes / 60\u00b0 / Speed 4'''.
| Add the nut mixture and cook for a further '''1 minute / 60\u00b0 / Speed 1'''.
===Serving suggestions===
As its name indicates, the sauce should be served warm.  You can use it as a dressing for salads such as chicory, asparagus, cabbage,  lettuce etc.  It is also good with cooked vegetables.
