===Description===

Sofrito is used as the basis of many Spanish dishes and 2 versions are given here.

'''For sofrito 1'''
===Ingredients===TM
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 150g onions
| 1 clove garlic
'''For sofrito 2'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 100g extra virgin olive oil
| 300g onions
| 2 cloves garlic
| 3 leeks (only the white part)
| 3 ripe tomatoes or 200g chopped tomatoes
===Method===
'''For sofrito 1'''

| Tip all of the ingredients into the bowl '''2 seconds / Speed 4'''
| Then '''5 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''
| If you want the ingredients chopped very fine, then use '''Speed 4-5'''
'''For sofrito 2'''

| Tip all of the ingredients into the bowl '''4 seconds / Speed 5'''
| Then '''10 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''
| If you want the ingredients chopped very fine, process '''15 seconds / Speed 6'''
===Servings===
Recipe 1, 200g. Recipe 2, 600g.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
