===Description===

Another version of garlic soup!

===Ingredients===TM
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 40g garlic [peeled]
| 1 heaped tablespoon paprika
| 1500g water
| Salt, to taste
| 4 free range eggs
| 3 slices of finely sliced bread
===Method===


| Put the oil in the bowl '''5 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| When the machine stops add the garlic '''4 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 3½'''.
| Using the spatula, scrape down the lid and the walls of the inside of the bowl.
| Add the paprika and mix '''Speed 3 / a few seconds'''.
| Incorporate the water '''20 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 1'''.
| When the time is up, add the eggs and the slices of bread.
| Leave it to rest for some minutes and serve hot.
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
===Chef's notes===
If you want to make half the quantity cook for '''15 minutes''' only, after adding the water.
