===Description===

A Thermomix macaroni soup recipe from Spain.

Comments in square brackets are mine and not in the original recipe.

Tablespoons are European ones, ie, 3 teaspoons

'''For cooking the pasta'''
===Ingredients===TM
| 750g water
| Salt
| 1 tablespoon olive oil
| 250g macaroni
'''Other ingredients'''
===Ingredients===NB
| 1,000g water
| 2 chicken stock cubes
| 2 tablespoons tapioca
| 50g grated cheese
===Method===


| Tip the water, salt and olive oil into the bowl and set '''7 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 2'''.
| Add the pasta through the hole in the lid '''10 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon'''.
| After this time, strain off the macaroni using the internal basket and reserve.
| Discarding the original water, pour the 1,000g of water into the bowl with the stock cubes '''8 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| Add the tapioca '''5 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''.
| Next, add the macaroni '''1 minute / 100\u00b0 / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon'''.
| Serve in soup bowls and sprinkle the grated cheese over the top.
===Recipe source===
* Translated from a recipe in Cocina espa\u00f1ola con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones).
===Variations===
[Use different types of tubular pasta instead of macaroni, but you may need to adjust the cooking time]
