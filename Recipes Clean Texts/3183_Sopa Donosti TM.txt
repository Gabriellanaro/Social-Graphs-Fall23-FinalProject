===Description===

A Thermomix recipe for crayfish soup from Spain.

===Ingredients===TM
| 12 crayfish
| '''For the sofrito'''
| 150g tomatoes
| Â½ leek
| 25g shallots
| 25g butter
| 25g olive oil
'''Other ingredients'''
* 750g water
* 25g brandy
* 25g white wine
* Salt
* 150g rice
* 1 truffle, grated

===Method===


| Remove the heads from the crayfish and keep them.
| Peel the tails and reserve.
| Peel the tomatoes, remove the seeds and chop them.
| Wash the leek and chop it.
| Peel the shallots and chop them.
| Add the sofrito ingredients to the bowl with the crayfish heads '''5 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 5'''.
| Add the water, brandy, wine and salt '''5 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 4'''.
| Strain the stock and wash the bowl well.
| Pour the stock back into the bowl with the rice '''10 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Reverse blade / Speed Spoon'''.
| One minute before the end, add the reserved crayfish tails.
| When the time is up, check for salt and serve, accompanied with the grated truffle.
