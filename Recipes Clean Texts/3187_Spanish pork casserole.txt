===Description===

A nice quick one pot casserole recipe using shoulder of pork, always good for an economic dish.
===Ingredients===

| 1 kg boneless pork shoulder, minced
| 15g / \u00bd oz lard, ghee or olive oil
| A \u00bd teaspoon of Taste Sensation Italian seasoning
| A good pinch of freshly ground black pepper
| 1 large onion, peeled and chopped
| 3 cloves of garlic, peeled and crushed
| 1 glass of red wine
| 1 chicken stock cube stirred into 200 ml boiling water
| 1 tablespoon sweet paprika powder
| A good pinch of dried mixed herbs
| 1 bay leaf
| 3 tablespoons of Taste Sensation Mixed Sweet Red and Green Bell Pepper Granules
| 1 can of chopped plum tomatoes
| 100g / 4 oz button mushrooms, sliced
| 2 teaspoons cornflour

===Method===

| Heat the lard, oil or ghee in a large frying pan and add the black pepper and Taste Sensation Italian seasoning
| Stir-fry the onions and garlic for 5 minutes
| Add the onions, garlic and ghee with all of the remaining ingredients to the slow cooker
| Slow cook on on medium for about 3 hours
| Taste for seasoning and serve
