===Description===

Another Spanish recipe for Tartare sauce

===Ingredients===TM
| 10g pickled gherkins
| 10g baby onions
| 10g capers
| Parsley
| 1 hard boiled egg
| 1 raw egg
| The juice of Â½ a lemon, or vinegar
| 25g water
| Salt, to taste
| 300g oil
===Method===


| Chop the gherkins, onions, capers and parsley in the bowl '''3-4 seconds / Speed 4-5'''.
| Remove and reserve.
| Chop the hard boiled egg '''4 seconds / Speed 4'''.
| Remove and reserve.
| Without washing it, put the raw egg, lemon juice, water and salt into the bowl '''10 seconds / Speed 4'''.
| With the MC in place on the lid program '''Speed 4''' and pour the oil on the lid, little by little.
| Once the mayonnaise has been made, add the chopped pickles [and the egg] and mix well with the spatula.
