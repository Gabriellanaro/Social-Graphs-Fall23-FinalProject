===Description===

A Thermomix recipe for fresh tomato ketchup (don't expect it to taste like Heinz!)

===Ingredients===TM

| 20g olive oil
| 1 large onion, chopped into chunks
| 1 green pepper, chopped into chunks
| Garlic, to taste
| 1kg ripe tomatoes, chopped into chunks
| 35g muscovado sugar
| 70g white wine or cider vinegar
| ½ teaspoon salt
| ½ teaspoon sweet paprika
| ½ teaspoon mustard powder
| A pinch of ground cinnamon
| A pinch of ground cloves
| 1 guindilla pepper (optional)
===Method===



| Put the oil in the bowl '''5 minutes / Temp Varoma / Speed Spoon'''.
| Add the onion, green pepper and garlic '''15 minutes / Temp Varoma / Reverse Blade / Speed Spoon / MC Off'''. 
| Add the tomatoes '''45 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 1'''.
| Add the remaining ingredients '''60 minutes / 100\u00b0 / Speed 1''', until you have a thick pur\u00e9e.
| Allow to cool slightly and blitz '''1 minute / Speed 5-10 progressively'''.
| To thicken it, if necessary, cook for a further '''10-20 minutes / 90\u00b0 / Speed 3 / MC Off'''.
| Pour into sterilised jars or a clean Lock&Lock container.
| Allow to cool and cover well.
===Recipe source===
* Lightly adapted from a PepeKitchen recipe at '''[http://www.directoalpaladar.com/recetas-de-salsas-y-guarniciones/receta-de-ketchup-casero Directo al Paladar]'''
===Chef's notes===
I used 1 whole bulb of garlic.
