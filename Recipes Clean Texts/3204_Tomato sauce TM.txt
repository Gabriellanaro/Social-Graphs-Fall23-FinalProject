===Description===

A basic recipe for tomato sauce

===Ingredients===TM
| 50g extra virgin olive oil
| 2 cloves garlic
| 100g onions
| 1kg chopped tomatoes
| 30g sugar
| Salt and pepper
===Method===


| Put the oil in the bowl '''3 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1'''
| Add the garlic and onion '''10 seconds / Speed 5'''
| Then '''7 minutes / Speed Varoma / Speed 3½'''
| Scrape down the lid with the spatula '''a few seconds / Speed 5'''
| Add the remaining ingredients '''30 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 1''', placing the internal steaming basket over the lid to avoid splashing
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
