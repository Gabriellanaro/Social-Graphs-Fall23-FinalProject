===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 100g cashew nuts
| 180g extra virgin olive oil
| 30g vinegar
| Pepper
| Salt
===Method===


| Put the nuts in TM and grind '''3 seconds / Speed 7'''.
| Add the remaining ingredients and mix '''1 minute / Speed 1'''.
| Stir to mix the ingredients.
| Serve at room temperature.
===Serving suggestions===
This is a vinaigrette suitable for dressing salads (rocket, lettuce, Swiss chard, leeks, peas) and to accompany pasta, fish and seafood.
===Variations===
The cashews can be substituted with pistachios, toasted pine nuts, walnuts or other nuts.  It combines well with other sauces, such as cheese.
