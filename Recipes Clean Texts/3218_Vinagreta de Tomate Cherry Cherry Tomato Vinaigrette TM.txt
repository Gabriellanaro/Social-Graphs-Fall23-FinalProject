===Description===

This is a translation of a recipe in '''Salsas y Ali\u00f1os con Thermomix (Susaeta Ediciones)'''

===Ingredients===TM
| 100g cherry tomatoes
| 100g virgin olive oil
| 1 tablespoon ground ginger
| 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
| Black pepper
| Salt
===Method===


| Wash the tomatoes and cut them.
| Place in the TM and blend them '''2 minutes / Speed 3'''.
| Nest, add the rest of the ingredients and blend again at '''Speed 5''', until the vinaigrette is well bound.
===Serving suggestions===
Cherry Tomato Vinaigrette is an ideal dressing for all types of salad, but in particular, those which contain chicken, mozzarella, cream or cottage cheese or salt-cod.
