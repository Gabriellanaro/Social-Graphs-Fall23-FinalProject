===Description===

This is a Spanish recipe for vinaigrette which is made in the Thermomix.

===Ingredients===TM
| 200g extra virgin olive oil
| 50g Sherry vinegar
| Salt
===Method===


| Put the ingredients in the bowl and emulsify '''25 seconds / Speed 5-6'''
===Recipe source===
* This is a translation of a recipe in '''''Thermomix TM31 - Imprescindible para tu cocina''''' which is the book supplied with Spanish TMXs.
===Variations===
If you want a flavoured vinaigrette add flavours as desired:  dill, basil, coriander, tarragon, hazelnuts etc.  First crush at '''Speed 7''' and then emulsify at '''Speed 5-6''' for a few seconds.
