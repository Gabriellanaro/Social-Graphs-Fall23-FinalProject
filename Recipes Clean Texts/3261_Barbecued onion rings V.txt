===Description===

[[Image:Barbecued onion rings ingredients.jpg|thumb|right|250px|Barbecued onion rings, the ingredients]]
A nice side dish.

===Ingredients===
| 500g 1 kg (1 lb) Red onions
| Sprig fresh thyme
| Sprig fresh rosemary
| 2 tablespoons garlic olive oil
| Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
===Method===


| Heat barbecue (hotplate is ideal if you have one)
| Roughly chop the herbs
| Slice the onions 1 cm (Â½\") rings, don't separate the rings, that will happen anyway
| Toss the all ingredients in a bowl, coating the onions well
| Place the onions on the barbecue and cook for 20 minutes, turning often
| Remove any woody herbs and serve
