===Description===

Cachapas are a part of traditional Venezuelan cuisine. Like arepas it is a popular \"outdoors\" food, particularly in roadside stands.

This version is the corn (maize) variety

===Ingredients===
| 10 ears of corn
| 2 tablespoons sugar (avoid if the corn is too sweet)
| ¼ cup milk
| A pinch of salt
| Corn oil, for sauteing
| 112g strong cheese, grated [4 oz]
===Method===


| Remove kernels form husk of corn using a sharp knife
| Pure\u00e9 in a blender
| Mix in the sugar, milk, and salt
| Heat a griddle pan to medium heat, lightly coat with corn oil
| Spoon mixture onto hot griddle to form \"pancakes\" of any desired size
| Cook for 2 minutes on each side, and serve with grated cheese
