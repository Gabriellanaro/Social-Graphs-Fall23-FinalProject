===Description===

A very way to make a fabulous dip for French fries, taco chips, vegetables; well almost anything.  So tasty!
===Ingredients===

| 2 heaped tablespoons Taste Sensation Nacho Cheese Seasoning
| 4 tablespoons Greek-style yogurt
| Pinch of Taste Sensation Smoked Paprika Powder

===Method===


| Add 4 tablespoons of Greek-style yogurt to a mixing bowl
| Stir in 2 heaped tablespoons of Taste Sensation Nacho Cheese Seasoning
| Mix really well
| Spoon into a serving bowl
| Sprinkle with a pinch of Taste Sensation Smoked Paprika Powder - ''just for show!''

Image:My Taste Sensation Nacho Cheese seasoning.jpg|My packet of nacho seasoning

===Serving suggestions===
Serve as a dip for potato chips, taco chips, French fries, raw vegetables, etc.
