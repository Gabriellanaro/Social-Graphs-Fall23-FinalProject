===Description===

This beef stew on corn tostadas recipe is courtesy of MexTrade.co.uk and originates from [http://www.lupita.co.uk/ Lupita], London.

Lupita is known as one of the most popular restaurants serving authentic Mexico City cuisine in London. Lupita is a true reflection of the incredible flavours, colours and aromas found throughout Mexico City.
===Ingredients===

| 500 g cooked shredded beef brisket
| 700 g plum tomatoes
| 200 g of La Coste\u00f1a green tomatillo
| 150 g of La Coste\u00f1a Chile Chipotle in Adobo
| 1 Large Onion, peeled and sliced         
| 1 whole onion peeled and cut in half
| 45 ml of corn oil
| 1 garlic clove, minced
| 1 sprig of marjoram
| 1 sprig of thyme
| 1 teaspoon of dry mexican oregano
| \u00bd teaspoon of Cracked black pepper
| \u00bd table spoon of sea salt
====to serve====
===Ingredients===NB
|16 small Corn Tostadas
|Tender mix leaf salad
|200 ml Cr\u00e8me Fraiche
|100 g of Mexican crumble Queso Fresco
|1 Avocado hass on slices

===Method===


| Roast the plum tomatoes and 1 onion in half on a flat non-stick pan until everything is soft and the skin is blackened.
| Place in a mortar or a blender and add La Coste\u00f1a Green Tomatillo and Chipotle in Adobo.
| Blend perfectly with the pestle or the blender until you obtain a thick sauce and reserve.
| Separately heat the corn in a skillet and saut\u00e9 the onion slices stirring constantly until you get a golden colour. Add the minced garlic and gently saut\u00e9 all together with the marjoram, thyme and Mexican oregano.
| Pour the mixture of roasted tomatoes, La Coste\u00f1a Chipotle and tomatillos and medium fry for 10-15 minutes.
| Season with cracked black pepper and add half of the salt. Stir and check the flavour.
| Place the tostadas on a plate and top with the beef Tinga stew.
| Add Cr\u00e8me Fraiche to decorate. 
| Sprinkle with crumbled Mexican Queso Fresco
| Garnish with the tender mixed salad leaves and a few avocado slices.
