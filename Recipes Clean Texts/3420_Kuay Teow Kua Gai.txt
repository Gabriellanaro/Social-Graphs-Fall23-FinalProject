Stir-fried rice noodles with chicken, Fried rice noodles with chicken or Kuay Teow Kua Gai (Thai : \u0e01\u0e4b\u0e27\u0e22\u0e40\u0e15\u0e35\u0e4b\u0e22\u0e27\u0e04\u0e31\u0e48\u0e27\u0e44\u0e01\u0e48) is a Chinese-influenced dish with Noodles or Kuay Teow that is popular in Thailand. Kuay teow was later modified by Thais to dried noodles with chicken. Then, they call this style \"Kuay Teow Kua Gai\". Kuay Teow Kua Gai normally is served as soaked dried rice noodles stirred with a simple combination of ingredients, such as chicken, squid and lettuce. You can add other additional ingredients as sliced tomatoes, egg, deep-fried doughstick, garlic  and spring onion. It is seasoned with oyster sauce, light soy sauce and fish sauce. It also may be seasoned with sauce prik (''nam prik - chilli sauce''), chili flakes, vinegar, sugar and tangy tomato sauce.

To make this dish special is arrange the Kuay Teow Kua Gai on the top of lettuce.

===Ingredients===
|\u00bd kilo of rice noodle (Depends on you)
|\u00bd cup of sliced chicken
|\u00bd cup of squid 
|3 chicken eggs 
|Sliced Tomato
|1 teaspoon of dry cabbage leaves
|1 tablespoon of chopped spring onions
|2-3 tablespoons of frying oil
|1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
|1 tablespoon of fish sauce
|2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
|1 tablespoon of soy sauce
|1 tablespoon of white sugar
|Chinese cabbage (depends on you)

===Method===

| Add the oil to your wok
| Add the onions and vegetables and stir fry for 3 minutes
| Add the meat or fish and stir fry for a few more minutes
| Pour in the liquid ingredients and mix well
| ''You can add the egg and scramble it in the pan, then mix it up, or just add it and stir-fry it as it cooks''
