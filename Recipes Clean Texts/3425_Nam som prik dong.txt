Nam som prik dong (Thai Vinegar with Chili)

===Ingredients===
| 1 measure sliced red Thai chillies ''to:'' 2 measures white wine vinegar
| Good pinch of salt
===Method===


| Use clean, sterilised glass jars with plastic lids, metal lids will rust
| Add 1 measure sliced fresh red chillies to 2 measures white wine vinegar
| Add a good pinch of salt
| Mix well
| Refrigerate, shaking now and then
| Should keep for 3 months if refrigerated
===Serving suggestions===

Serve with fish dishes
