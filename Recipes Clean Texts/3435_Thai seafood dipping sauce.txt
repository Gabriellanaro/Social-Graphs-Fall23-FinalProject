===Description===

Perfect for finger dipping seafood such as prawns or crawfish

===Ingredients===
| 1.5 tablespoons lime juice
| Pinch of lime zest
| 1 teaspoon caster sugar
| 3 tablespoons nam pla
| 1 small fresh red chili, deseeded and sliced to rings
===Method===


| Stir the sugar into the lime juice and whisk to dissolve
| Add the nam pla and chili rings
===Serving suggestions===

This would also go well with chicken
