===Description===


In Turkish it is called ''samersakli yogurt'' and is a good dip for spicy food

===Ingredients===
| 200g Greek yogurt
| 2 tablespoons milk
| 1 small garlic clove
| Good pinch sea salt
===Method===


| In a mortar and pestle, grind the garlic to a paste with the sea salt
| Add the milk to the garlic and mix
| Pour the milk into the yogurt and mix well
| Pour into a bowl and serve
===Serving suggestions===

Serve chilled.  It is the usual accompaniment for dolmas.

===Variations===

Sprinkle with chive flowers if in season
