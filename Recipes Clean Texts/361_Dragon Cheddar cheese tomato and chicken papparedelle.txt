===Description===

A great cheese, chicken and pasta recipe from South Caernarfon Creameries, the award winning Welsh cheese makers.
===Ingredients===

| 500g [1lb] pappardelle or tagliatelle pasta
| 2 tablespoons olive oil
| 2 cloves garlic
| 8 plum tomatoes
| 2 chicken breasts thinly sliced (for vegetarian version omit chicken and add some roasted peppers)
| 1 tablespoon thyme leaves
| 200g [7oz] Dragon Mature Cheddar cheese, grated
| Freshly ground black pepper
===Mise en place===

* Set your oven  to 180\u00b0 C (350\u00b0 F - gas 4), [fan oven 160\u00b0 C & reduce cooking time by 10 mins per hour]

===Method===


| Cut tomatoes in half and arrange on a baking sheet. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and sprinkle with the thyme leaves. Bake for 20 minutes.
| Stir fry chicken in 1 tablespoon olive oil until cooked through
| Cook pasta in boiling salted water until al dente (follow packet instructions)
| Drain pasta, place in a baking dish. Stir through chicken and arrange tomatoes on top.
| Sprinkle over grated cheese and place under a hot grill to melt cheese.
| Season with black pepper and serve with a green salad.
