===Description===

This recipe from  South Caernarfon Creameries quickly turns read-rolled puff pastry into a delicious snack or aperitif.
===Ingredients===

| 320g ready rolled puff pastry
| 190g jar of green pesto
| 2 red peppers deseeded and sliced
| 100g Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese, grated
| 1 tablespoon Olive oil
| Fresh basil leaves
===Mise en place===

* Preheat your oven to 220\u00b0 C (425\u00b0 F - gas 7), [fan oven 200\u00b0 C & reduce cooking time by 10 mins per hour]

===Method===


| Toss peppers in olive oil and roast for 10 minutes on a baking sheet (also great with courgettes and red onions)
| Cut pastry into 4 squares and place on a baking sheet that has been dampened with cold water
| Spread each pastry square with pesto
| Arrange roasted peppers over the top and sprinkle with cheddar
| Bake for 12-14 minutes until pastry is risen and topping is lightly browned

Image:Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese.jpg|thumb|300px|right|[http://www.dragonwales.co.uk/products/cheddar/mature/ Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese]
Image:Dragon reduced fat mature Welsh cheddar cheese.jpg|thumb|300px|right|[http://www.dragonwales.co.uk/products/cheddar/mature-reduced-fat/ Dragon reduced fat mature Welsh cheddar cheese]

===Serving suggestions===
Serve with a green salad or cut up as nibbles to serve with drinks
===Variations===
For a lower fat alternative, use Dragon reduced fat mature Welsh cheddar cheese
