===Description===

This cheese and tomato scone recipe comes courtesy of South Caernarfon Creameries, the addition of tomatoes makes an unusual and interesting change.
===Ingredients===

| 250g self-raising flour
| 1 level teaspoon baking powder
| 50g Dragon salted butter
| 1 egg beaten & 100ml milk mixed together
| 100g Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese
| 2 tablespoons of chopped sun-dried tomatoes in oil (drained weight)
| 1 teaspoon mixed herbs
| Freshly ground black pepper
===Mise en place===

* Preheat your oven to 220\u00b0 C (425\u00b0 F - gas 7), [fan oven 200\u00b0 C & reduce cooking time by 10 mins per hour]

===Method===


| Sieve the flour with baking powder into a large mixing bowl, rub in the butter until mixture resembles breadcrumbs
| Sprinkle in herbs and black pepper, stir through sun dried tomatoes and cheddar
| Gradually add egg & milk mixture until you have a dough, if this seem too wet do not add all liquid
| Turn onto floured surface and knead until smooth
| Flatten out to approx. 3cm deep and using a round cutter stamp out scones
| Bake on a tray lightly dusted with flour for 12-15 minutes until golden brown and cooked through

===Serving suggestions===
Serve warm and spread with Dragon Cheddar cheese and leek pate or just with butter.
===Variations===
Use Dragon reduced fat mature Welsh cheddar cheese if you are watching your calories!
