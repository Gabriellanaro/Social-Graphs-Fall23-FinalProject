===Description===

Welsh Cheddar cheese pancakes made with tangy Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese, produced by South Caernarfon Creameries.
===Ingredients===

| 200g plain flour
| 2 teaspoons baking powder
| 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
| \u00bc teaspoon salt
| 300ml milk
| 1 large egg
| 75g grated Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese
| Butter or olive oil for frying

===Method===


| Combine the flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in a bowl, then add the egg and gradually add the milk in between mixing
| Stir in the grated cheese.
| Heat a frying pan to a medium-high heat and lightly butter.
| Pour the batter into the pan in even circles. Cook until bubbles form then flip and cook for 2-3 minutes.

===Serving suggestions===
Serve with a little extra grated Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese for an extra cheesey pancake!
===Variations===
Use Dragon reduced fat mature Welsh cheddar cheese if you are watching your calories!
