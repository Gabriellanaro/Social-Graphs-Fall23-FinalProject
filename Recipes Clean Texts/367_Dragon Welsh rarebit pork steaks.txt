===Description===
===Description===
This reciepe from South Caernarfon Creameries combines tangy Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese melted over tender pork chops, a fabulous combination.
===Ingredients===

| 4 x 130g pork steaks, trimmed
| 60g grated Dragon mature cheddar
| 1 dessertspoon wholegrain mustard
| 1 tablespoon cr\u00e8me fra\u00eeche or soured cream
| Dash of Worcestershire sauce
| Freshly ground black pepper
===Mise en place===

* Preheat your oven to 200\u00b0 C (400\u00b0 F - gas 6), [fan oven 180\u00b0 C & reduce cooking time by 10 mins per hour]

===Method===


| Place pork steaks into a lightly oiled baking dish and bake for 15 minutes
| Mix together the mustard, cheddar and cr\u00e8me fra\u00eeche or soured cream, add seasonings and mix thoroughly
| Spread the cheese mixture over the steaks and bake again until the topping is golden and bubbling and the meat is cooked through

===Serving suggestions===
Serve with green beans and new potatoes
===Variations===
For a lower fat alternative, use Dragon reduced fat mature Welsh cheddar cheese
