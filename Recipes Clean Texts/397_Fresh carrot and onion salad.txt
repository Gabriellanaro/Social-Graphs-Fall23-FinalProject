===Description===

We love this recipe; and probably have this two or three times a week.

If you have a food-processor, it only takes a few minutes to prepare.  Do try it, it's fantastic! ðŸ¥—ðŸ¥•
===Ingredients===

| 3 small carrots
| 1 medium white onion
| 2 tablespoons of olive oil
| 1tbs cider vinegar
| 1 teaspoon honey
| Pinch of pink sea salt
| Pinch of freshly ground black pepper

===Method===


| Top, tail and peel the carrots
| Remove the skin from the onion
| Finely slice the onion
| Grate the carrot on the finest setting
====dressing====

| Mix all of the dressing ingredients together, ensuring the honey is properly dissolved
| Pour over the salad and mix well
| Chill, if required, and serve
===Variations===
Fresh lemon juice and a little zest of lemon makes a good substitute for the cider vinegar.
