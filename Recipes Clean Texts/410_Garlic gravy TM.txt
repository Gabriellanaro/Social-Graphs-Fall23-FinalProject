===Description===


I spotted this recipe on [http://www.lidl.co.uk/en/6888.htm Lidl's website] and just had to adapt it for Thermomix.
===Ingredients===

| 3 clove of garlic,peeled
| 1 tablespoon of butter
| 1 tablespoon of plain flour
| 150ml red wine, boiled and reduced to one-third (50ml)
| 1 tablespoon tomato pur\u00e9e
| 400ml vegetable stock
| 1 tablespoon soy sauce

===Method===


| Add the garlic to the bowl '''3 secs / Speed 6'''
| Scrape down the bowl
| Add the butter '''10 minutes / 100\u00b0 C / Speed 1 / MC Off'''
| Add the flour '''4 minutes / 90\u00b0 C / Speed 1'''
| Add the remaining ingredients '''10 minutes / 100\u00b0 C / Speed 4'''
===Serving suggestions===
Use in place of standard gravy
