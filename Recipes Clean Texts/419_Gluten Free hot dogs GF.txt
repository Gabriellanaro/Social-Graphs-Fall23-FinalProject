===Description===

[[Image:Caramelised-red-onion-sausages.png|300px|thumb|right|Debbie & Andrew's Caramelised Red Onion Pork Sausages]]
[[Image:Perfect-pork.png|300px|thumb|right|Debbie and Andrew's Perfect Pork sausages]]
Gluten free hot dogs made with debbie&andrews sausages which are made from the very best cuts of pork which come British Red Tractor assured farms. perfect for picnics and barbecues - even good for bonfire night!
===Ingredients===

| 1 pack of debbie&andrew\u0092s Harrogate 97% Pork or Caramelised red onion pork sausages
| 1 pack of Udi\u0092s gluten free Classic Finger Rolls
| 2 brown onions - peeled and sliced
| A knob of butter

===Method===


| In a non stick large frying pan, drizzle some olive oil and fry off onions over a medium/high heat until golden and sticky, this should take around 12 minutes. Set aside in a bowl.
| In the same non stick large frying pan, fry off debbie&andrew\u0092s Caramelised red onion pork sausages over a medium/high heat until golden brown, about 10 minutes.
| With a bread knife, slice your buns directly down the centre without cutting the whole way through. Toast under the grill, then butter. Place your sausages inside and cover in onions and your favourite sauce.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with your favourite tomato sauce or ketchup
