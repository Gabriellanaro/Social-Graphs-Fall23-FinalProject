A simple way to make hard boiled eggs a little more interesting.

===Ingredients===
| 4 Free range eggs
| 4 anchovy fillets, chopped finely 
| 25g (1oz) softened butter
| 8 Cooked peeled prawns
| Parsley to garnish
| Salt & pepper
===Method===


| Hard-boil the eggs for 8 minutes in boiling water, refresh in cold water then peel and slice in half lengthways
| Remove the yolks, place in a bowl and mash with a fork
| Finely chop 2 of the walnuts
| Add the chopped anchovies and butter to the egg yolks. Mix well
| Season mixture with salt and pepper
| Fill the egg white halves with the mixture, dress with a prawn on each
| garnish with chopped parsley and serve
