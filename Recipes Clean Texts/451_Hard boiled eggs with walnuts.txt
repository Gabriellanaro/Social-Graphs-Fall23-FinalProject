===Description===

An interesting take on hard boiled eggs.

===Ingredients===
| 4 Free range eggs
| 12 walnut halves
| 4 anchovy fillets, chopped finely 
| 40g (1.5oz) softened butter
| Juice from ½ lemon
| Parsley to garnish
| Salt & pepper
===Method===


| Hard-boil the eggs for 8 minutes in boiling water, refresh in cold water then peel and slice in half lenghtways
| Remove the yolks, place in a bowl and mash with a fork
| Finely chop 4 of the walnut halves
| Add the chopped walnuts, chopped anchovies, butter and lemon juice to the egg yolks. Mix well
| Season mixture with salt and pepper
| Fill the egg white halves with the mixture, placing a walnut half on top of each one
| garnish with chopped parsley and serve
