===Description===

From the Ministry of Food leaflet \"All about Herrings\" - reprinted in 1948!

===Ingredients===
| 6 oz cooked, flaked herring
| ½ oz margarine, melted
| 1 level teaspoon salt
| Pinch of pepper
| ½ level tablespoon finely chopped onion
| 1 level tablespoon chopped parsley
| 2 teaspoons vinegar
===Method===


| Mash the ingredients together with a fork to form a smooth paste
| Use as required for sandwich filling or hot on toast
| If preferred the onion may be fried in the melted margarine before adding to the mixture
