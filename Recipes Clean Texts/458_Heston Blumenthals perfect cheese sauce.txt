===Description===

[[Image:Perfect cheese sauce ingredients.jpg|thumb|300px|right|Heston Blumenthal's perfect cheese sauce, the ingredients]]
This is Heston's the cheese sauce used with Heston Blumenthal's perfect cauliflower cheese.  It is just as good with pasta, any vegetables and anywhere a rich cheese sauce is needed.

'''for the cheese sauce'''
===Ingredients===
| 500ml chicken stock, reduced to 200ml and kept warm
| 300ml white wine, reduced to 30ml
| 120g grated gruy\u00e8re cheese
| 20g cornflour
| 20g soft cheese
| Knob of butter
| Drops of truffle oil


===Mise en place===
* Reduce the white wine from 500 ml to 30 ml, or cheat and use dry sherry!
* Reduce the chicken stock from 500 ml to 200 ml
===Method===


| Add the reduced stock and wine to a pan over a medium heat
| Mix in the grated Gruy\u00e8re and cornflower and mix well until the cheese is incorporated into the stock
| Remove the pan from the heat and add a few drops or truffle oil
| Use as required
