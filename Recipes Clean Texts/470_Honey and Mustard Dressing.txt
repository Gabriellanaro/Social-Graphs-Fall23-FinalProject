===Description===

Andy Bates shares his secret for the essential salad dressing. Hint: it's all about the magic ratio in this classic sweet and savoury summer dressing.  Great Gluten free with a sausage salad!

This recipe is reproduced courtesy of debbie&andrew's sausages.
===Ingredients===

| 1 tablespoon clear honey 
| 1 tablespoon wholegrain mustard
| 3 tablespoons olive oil
| 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
| Salt and freshly ground black pepper

===Method===


| Combine all the ingredients and whisk. Yep. That\u0092s it!
