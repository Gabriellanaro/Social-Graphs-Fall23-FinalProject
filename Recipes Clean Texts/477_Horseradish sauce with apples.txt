===Description===

This unusual horseradish sauce recipe goes well with salty meats.

===Ingredients===
| 150g Fresh horseradish
| 150g Cooking apples
| 284 ml wine vinegar
| Pinch of sugar
===Method===


| Peel and grate an equal quantity of horseradish and cooking apples.
| Place in serving bowl or a jar, if storing, and cover with white wine vinegar.
| Add sugar to taste
