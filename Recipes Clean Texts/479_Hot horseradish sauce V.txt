===Description===

This horseradish sauce is hot, and is also served hot.

===Ingredients===
| 125 ml white sauce
| ¼ teaspoon mustard powder
| 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
| 3 tablespoons freshly grated horseradish
===Method===


| Mix all of the ingredients together
| Heat and serve!
