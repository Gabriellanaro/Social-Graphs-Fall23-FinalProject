===Description===

A garlicky sauce from Armenia.

===Ingredients===
| 300 ml Greek style yogurt
| 1 or more cloves garlic, crushed
| ½ teaspoon salt
| ½ teaspoon dried mint or 2 tablespoons fresh mint, finely chopped
| ½ teaspoon or more chilli powder
===Method===


| Mix all of the ingredients (except the chilli powder) and put into a serving bowl or jug.
| Sprinkle the chilli powder over the top.
===Serving suggestions===
Goes well with fried vegetables and kebabs.
