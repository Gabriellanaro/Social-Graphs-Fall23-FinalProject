===Description===

A rather stunning and definitely unusual wreath-shaped salad recipe from South Caernarfon Creameries, the award-winning Welsh cheese makers.
===Ingredients===

| 1 large bag of salad leaves
| 1 crisp eating apple, cored and cut into thin slices and tossed in lemon juice
| 1 large pomegranate deseeded
| 50g dried cranberries
| 1 heaped tablespoon pumpkin seeds or chopped nuts
| 120g crumbled Dragon mature Welsh cheddar cheese
====dressing====
===Ingredients===NB
| 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
| 1 tablespoon aged balsamic vinegar
| 1 teaspoon runny honey
| sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

===Method===


| Arrange the salad leaves on a large flat platter in the shape of a wreath
| Carefully place the apple slices on the salad leaves
| Sprinkle over the cheese and walnut halves, top with pomegranate seeds and cranberries
| Mix together the salad dressing and drizzle over the salad. Season to taste
| Serve immediately
