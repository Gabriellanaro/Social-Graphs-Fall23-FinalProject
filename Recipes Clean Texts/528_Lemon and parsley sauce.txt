===Description===

An extremely quick and delicious accompaniment for fish dishes.

===Ingredients===
| 50g butter
| 1 lemon
| Bunch of fresh parsley leaves, finely chopped
| sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
===Method===


| Melt the butter in a small pan
| Add a twist or two of black pepper
| Stir in the juice and zest of 1 lemon
| Season to taste
| Sprinkle in the chopped parsley
===Serving suggestions===
Pour over the fish and serve
