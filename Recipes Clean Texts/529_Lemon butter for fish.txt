===Description===


A simple lemony butter sauce that is perfect for delicate white fish and for dipping globe artichokes.

===Ingredients===
| 2.5 tablespoons white wine
| 100 g (3.5 oz) butter, cut into cubes
| 2 tablespoons lemon juice
| ½ teaspoon zest of lemon
| Salt and freshly ground black pepper
===Method===


| Leave the butter in a warm place to soften
| Gently heat the wine in a small pan and reduce by 50%
| Set to lowest heat and whisk in the butter, little by little
| Remove from the heat, whisk in lemon juice and zest
===Serving suggestions===

Serve warm
