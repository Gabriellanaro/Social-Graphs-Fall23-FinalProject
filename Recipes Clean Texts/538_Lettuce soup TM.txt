===Description===

This Thermomix recipe has been adapted from a recipe in '''[http://www.ukthermomix.com/recindex.php Fast and Easy Cooking]''', the book which comes with UK Thermomixes.  It can easily be adapted for cooking on the hob.

===Ingredients===TM
| 4 spring onions, cut into 2cm lengths
| Garlic to taste, peeled
| 30g butter
| 500g vegetable stock
| 250g potatoes, peeled and cut into 1cm cubes
| 8-10 outside leaves of a large lettuce, roughly torn (I used Iceberg)
| 100g cream or yogurt
| Seasoning
| Mint or parsley to garnish
===Method===


| Place the spring onion and the garlic in The TM bowl and chop '''4 seconds / Speed 4Â½'''.
| Scrape doen the sides and lid of the TM bowl and lid.
| Add the butter and saut\u00e9 '''10 minutes /100\u00b0C/ Speed 1 / MC Off'''. 
| Add the stock and the potatoes and cook '''15-17 minutes / 100\u00b0 C / Speed 1''', making sure that the potatoes are properly cooked.
| Add the lettuce, cream seasoning and pur\u00e9e '''1 minute / 90\u00b0C / Speed 8''' until smooth.
| If the temperature has dropped below 90\u00b0C, heat through on '''Speed 1''' until that temperature has been achieved.
| Serve garnished with the mint or parsley.
