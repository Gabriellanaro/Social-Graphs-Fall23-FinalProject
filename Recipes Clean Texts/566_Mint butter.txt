Mint butter is a good alternative to mint sauce when you need an accompaniment to lamb or mutton.

Make it in large quantities when mint is in season and freeze in batches.

===Ingredients===
| 100g (4 oz) butter
| 2 tablespoons fresh mint leaves, washed and finely chopped
| Pinch of each; salt, pepper and sugar
===Method===


| Work the mint and seasoning into the butter
| Shape by rolling in foil
| Cut into usable portions
| Wrap well, use immediately or refrigerate or freeze for later use.
