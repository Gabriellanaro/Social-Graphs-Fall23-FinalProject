===Ingredients===
| 200g (7oz) white breadcrumbs
| 125g (4Â½oz) butter
| 2 Cloves of garlic (or to taste), peeled and crushed
| 1 onion, peeled and finely chopped
| 1 sprig of rosemary, chopped leaves only
| 1 sprig of thyme, chopped leaves only
| 6 fresh sage leaves, chopped
| The zest from 1 lemon
| 50g (2oz) pine nuts
| 3 tablespoons of fresh parsley, chopped
| 1 teaspoon sea salt
| freshly ground black pepper
===Method===


| Melt butter in a frying pan and saut\u00e9 onion and garlic for five minutes or until softened - don't allow the garlic to burn.
| Mix in the herbs for one minute then add breadcrumbs and mix well.
| Add the zest, pine nuts.  Season and cook over medium heat for about seven minutes until crumbs start to brown and crisp.
| Remove from the heat, stir in the parsley and serve.
