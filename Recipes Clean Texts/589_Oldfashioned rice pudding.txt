You either love or loathe this; it's the original, thick skinned, spicy flavoured, creamy rice pudding.

===Ingredients===
| 110g (4 oz) short-grain rice (pudding rice)
| 568 ml (1 pint) full-fat milk
| 410g tin of evaporated milk (14.5 oz)
| 40g (1.5 oz) sugar, jaggery or honey
| 1 whole nutmeg, grated
| 25g butter


===Mise en place===
* Preheat the oven to 150\u00b0 C (300 \u00b0F - gas 2)

===Method===


| Evenly distribute the rice and  sugar in a suitable oven proof serving dish
| Mix the evaporated milk and the milk together and pour over the rice
| Grate the nutmeg over the surface and dot with shavings of butter
| Place in the oven for 30 minutes
| After 30 minutes, remove and stir well
| Cook for a further hour without stirring
===Serving suggestions===

Serve hot with spoonfuls of strawberry jam
