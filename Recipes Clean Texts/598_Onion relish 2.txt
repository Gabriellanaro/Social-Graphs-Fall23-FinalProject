===Description===

Another onion relish recipe, this one courtesy of British Onions.

===Ingredients===
| 900g/2lb Onions, sliced
| 3 cloves garlic, crushed
| 4 tablespoons Soft brown sugar
| 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
| Â½ teaspoon Salt
| 1 teaspoon Black peppercorns
===Method===


| Put all the ingredients into a deep pan with 100ml/ 4 floz of water.
| Bring to the boil, and then reduce to simmer.
| Cover & cook for 30 to 40 minutes until the onions are soft.
| Remove the lid and bring to the boil stirring until it thickens.
| Store in sterilised jars.
