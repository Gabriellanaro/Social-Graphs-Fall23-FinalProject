===Description===

Really nice with fish, vegetables, potatoes, ham and gammon steaks.

===Ingredients===
| ½ pint Basic white sauce
| 3 tablespoons freshly chopped parsley


===Method===


| Make the white sauce as directed
| Stir the freshly chopped parsley into the sauce
===Serving suggestions===
Serve hot or cold
===Dried parsley===
If using dried parsley instead of fresh, use half the amount, 1.5 tablespoons.
