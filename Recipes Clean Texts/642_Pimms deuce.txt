===Description===

Be prepared for the tennis season with a Pimm's deuce.

===Ingredients===
| 1 part Pimm's No.1
| 1.5 parts orange juice
| 1.5 parts cranberry juice
| A slice of orange
| A strawberry
===Method===


| Take a highball glass or a jug
| Fill it with ice
| Add the Pimm's and juices
| Garnish with the fruit
