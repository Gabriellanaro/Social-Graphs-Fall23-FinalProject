Be prepared for the summer season with this gingery Pimm's cocktail.

===Ingredients===
| 1 part Pimm's No.1 Cup
| 3 parts ginger ale
| A slice of lemon
| A sprig of mint
===Method===


| Take a jug or long drink glass and fill it with ice.
| Add the ingredients
| Serve
