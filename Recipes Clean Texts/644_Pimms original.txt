===Description===

Be prepared for the summer season with the original Pimm's cocktail.

===Ingredients===
| 1 part Pimms No.1
| 3 parts chilled lemonade
| Sprig of Mint
| Slices of Cucumber
| A wedge of Orange 
| A few Strawberries
===Method===


| Take a long glass or jug and add ice
| Add the ingredients
| Stir and serve
