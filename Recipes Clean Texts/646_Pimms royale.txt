===Description===

Be prepared for the summer season with a Pimm's royale.

===Ingredients===
| 25 cl Pimms No.1
| 25 cl Cava or Champagne
| A strawberry
===Method===


| Take a Champagne flute
| Add 25 cl of Pimm's No.1
| Top up with champagne
| Add a strawberry
