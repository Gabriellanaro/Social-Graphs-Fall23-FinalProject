Delicious with sweet or savoury items, these oatcakes are simple to make.

===Ingredients===
| 60 g (2oz) medium oatmeal, plus extra for sprinkling
| 60 g (2oz) wholemeal flour.
| Â½ teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda.
| A pinch of salt.
| 5 tablespoons of boiling water.
| A tablespoon of lard.


===Mise en place===
* Preheat the oven to  204\u00b0 C (400\u00b0 F - Gas 6)
===Method===


| Grease a baking tray and line with greaseproof paper
| Melt a tablespoon of lard in a bowl and add the fry ingredients, mix well
| Add 5 tablespoons of boiling water and mix. Using a palette knife fold the mixture until a dough starts to form
| Sprinkle a little oatmeal on a board, cut the dough into two and roll each half out into rounds, about 5 cm (1/2\") thick
| Score into farls
| Bake in the oven for about 10 minutes or until golden brown
| Cool on a rack before eating
