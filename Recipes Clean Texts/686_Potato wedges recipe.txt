The healthier alternative to chips.  Kids love them and don't realise they're missing their chips.  Served with a salsa or sour cream they also make a very good starter.

===Ingredients===
| 2 large baking potatoes, unpeeled
| 2 tablespoons olive oil
| 2 tablespoons peanut oil (groundnut oil)
| Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
===Method===


| Preheat the oven to  220\u00b0 C (400\u00b0 F - Gas 6)
| Cut the potatoes into wedges 2.5 cm (1\") wide
| Add the potato wedges and both oils to a bowl and with your hands, mix really well to coat the wedges
| Place on the preheated baking tray and cook for  20 to 30  minutes or until golden - check them regularly
| Drain on kitchen paper, sprinkle with seasoning
===Serving suggestions===
Serve immediately

===Variations===
The following are the best potato varieties to make great tasty baked potatoes:
* Cara
* Estima
* King Edwards
* Marfona
* Maris Piper
* Vivaldi
