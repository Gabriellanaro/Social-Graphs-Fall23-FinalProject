===Description===

An old favourite from the days of the Hostess Trolley \ud83d\ude38

===Ingredients===
| 125g equal mixture of white cabbage and crisp lettuce, shredded - enough to line 4 individuals dishes or bowls
| ½ red onion, finely sliced
| ¼ large cucumber, peeled and diced
| 130ml mayonnaise, preferably home made
| 2 tablespoons tomato ketchup
| Tabasco, to taste
| 1 tablespoon brandy
| 200g-250g shelled, cooked, king prawns
| Cayenne pepper
| Lemon slices, to garnish
===Method===


| Line 4 bowls or dishes with the cabbage and lettuce mixture.
| Sprinkle the onion and cucumber over the top.
| Make the Marie Rose sauce by thoroughly mixing the mayonnaise, tomato ketchup, Tabasco and brandy.
| Spoon over the prawns and sprinkle the Cayenne pepper over the top,
| Garnish with a lemon slice and serve.
===Serving suggestions===
This is traditionally served with thin slices of wholemeal bread and butter.
===Recipe source===
* Adapted from a recipe in '''''The Prawn Cocktail Years''''' by Simon Hopkinson and Lindsey Bareham ISBN 0718149807
