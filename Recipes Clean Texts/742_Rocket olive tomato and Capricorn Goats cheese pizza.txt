===Description===

A goats' cheese vegetarian pizza from those nice people at The Lubborn Creamery.

===Ingredients===
| 1 x 100g Capricorn Somerset goats' cheese
| 2 ready-made pizza bases
| 125g tomato passata
| Â½  red onion, sliced into rings
| 80g sun-blushed tomatoes
| A  handful of rocket leaves
| A handful of pitted black olives
| Olive oil
| sea salt
| Freshly ground black pepper
===Method===


| Cover the pizza bases with a layer of tomato passata and add the rocket.
| Add the sun-blushed tomatoes and scatter the red onion rings over the top.
| Dice the Capricorn Somerset goats' cheese and sprinkle over, then finish off with a handful of black olives.
| Season with salt and pepper.
| Place the pizza in the middle of a preheated oven and cook at 180\u00b0C for 6-8 minutes.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with extra rocket as desired and a drizzle of olive oil.
===Variations===
Make your own pizza base - it's so simple, especially if you own a bread maker.
