===Description===

A nice and simple barbecue hot-dog recipe made with gluten free sausages from debbie&andrews sausages so is also suitable for coeliacs.
[[Image:Harrogate-pork-sausages.png|300px|thumb|right|Debbie and Andrew's Harrogate 97% Gluten Free Pork Sausages]]

===Ingredients===

| 1 thin french baguette, cut into 6 pieces, or gluten free rolls of your choice such as Udi's GF White Rolls
| 2 onions, peeled and sliced
| 1 tablespoon butter, plus more to spread
| 1 pack Debbie and Andrew's Harrogate 97% Gluten Free Pork Sausages
| Ketchup and mustard to serve

===Method===


| Melt the butter in a small frying pan and cook the onion until beyond golden and almost brown.
| Remove from the pan and put to one side.
| On a pre-heated BBQ, cook sausages, turning regularly for 10-12 minutes until cooked through and browned evenly.
| Meanwhile, slice each piece of baguette in half (but not all the way through) and butter generously, or butter your gluten free bread choice.
| Fill each piece of bread with a sausage and a few buttery onions.
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with ketchup and mustard.
