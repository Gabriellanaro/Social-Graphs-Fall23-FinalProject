===Description===

A nice easy one pot casserole recipe using shoulder of pork, always good for an economic dish.
===Ingredients===

| 1 kg boneless pork shoulder, minced
| 15g / \u00bd oz lard, ghee or olive oil
| A \u00bd teaspoon of Taste Sensation Italian seasoning
| A good pinch of freshly ground black pepper
| 1 large onion, peeled and chopped
| 3 cloves of garlic, peeled and crushed
| 1 tablespoon sweet paprika powder
| A good pinch of dried mixed herbs
| 1 bay leaf
| 3 tablespoons of Taste Sensation Mixed Sweet Red and Green Bell Pepper Granules
| 1 can of chopped plum tomatoes
| 100g / 4 oz button mushrooms, sliced
| 2 teaspoons cornflour
===Method===

| Fry the onions for 5 minutes in the oil
| Add the garlic and fry for a further 3 minutes, don't let it burn
| Mix the remaining ingredients together and stir well
| Slow cook for 1 hour on high
| Serve
