===Description===

This is the kind of recipe that you get cravings for, which is fine because it's not difficult to make.  A small packet of flaked smoked salmon is still relatively inexpensive.
[[Image:Remember the greaseproof.jpg|thumb|right|300px|Twice now, I've forgotten to put greaseproof paper under the baking beans.  Perhaps this picture will remind me next time!]]

===Ingredients===
| 1 Basic flan case
| 135 g (4Â½ oz) flaked smoked salmon
| 2 free range eggs
| 250 ml (0.43 pint) of full cream milk
| Salt and freshly ground black pepper
| 2 tablespoons of freshly chopped parsley
| 1 fresh tomato, sliced for garnish


===Mise en place===

* Preheat the oven to 200\u00b0 C (400\u00b0 F - Gas 6)

===Method===


| Follow the recipe for the Basic flan case or use a ready-made flan case
| Add the eggs, milk, parsley and seasoning to a bowl
| With an electric whisk, combine the ingredients so they are well mixed.
| Blind bake the case @ 200\u00b0 C (400\u00b0 F - Gas 6) for 10 minutes.
| Arrange the smoked salmon in the base of the flan case
| Pour over the egg mixture
| Arrange the sliced tomatoes on the top and bake for a further 30 minutes.
===Serving suggestions===

Serve warm or cold
