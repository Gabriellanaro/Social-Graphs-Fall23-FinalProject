===Ingredients===
| 700g Rooster potatoes, washed and cut into thick wedges 
| 4 tablespoons Olive oil 
| ¼ teaspoons Ground cumin 
| ¼ teaspoons Ground coriander 
| ¼ teaspoons Paprika
===Method===


| Pre - heat oven to 225 C / Gas 7
| Place a roasting tray into the oven and leave till tray is very hot.
| Pour the oil into the tray, arrange the potatoes in a single layer onto the tray, return the tray to the oven and roast the potatoes till golden brown (about  20 minutes), turning once or twice.
| During the last 5 minutes of cooking sprinkle the spices over the chips.
| Remove potatoes from the oven, sprinkle with sea salt and serve with garlic mayonnaise or your favourite dip.
