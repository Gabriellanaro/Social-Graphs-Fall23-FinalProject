===Description===

Ice-cream made with fresh strawberries.  If your ice-cream maker has no built-in freezer, place your bowl in the freezer for a minimum of 12 hours before using.

===Ingredients===

| 600g fresh strawberries, hulled and cut in half (cut into quarters if particularly large)
| 60ml freshly squeezed lemon juice
| 330g caster sugar
| 330ml full fat milk
| 600ml double cream
| 1\u00bd teaspoons vanilla extract

===Method===


| Place the strawberries in a bowl and mix with the lemon juice and 110g of the sugar and leave for 2 hours to allow the sugar to dissolve
| Pour into a tall jug and add the remaining ingredients except for the cream
| Blend until the mixture is smooth using a stick blender
| Add the cream and blend briefly
| Allow to chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour
| Pour into your ice-cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions
| Transfer into a container and keep in the freezer
