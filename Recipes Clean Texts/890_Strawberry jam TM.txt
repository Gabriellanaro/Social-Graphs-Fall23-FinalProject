===Description===

This jam is very easy to make in a Thermomix
===Ingredients===TM
| 500g strawberries, washed and patted dry
| 500g sugar
| Juice of 1 lemon
| Large knob of butter
===Mise en place===

* Sterilise 2 x 500g jam jars

===Method===


| Chop the strawberries in the bowl by pressing the Turbo button for 2 seconds (if the fruits are very small, omit this step)
| Scrape down the sides of the bowl and add the remaining ingredients
| 25 minutes / Temp. Varoma / Speed 2 / MC Off (but place internal steaming basket on the lid to prevent splashing)
| Test for setting ability by putting a teaspoon of jam on a saucer and freezing for about 2 minutes.
| The mixture is set if the jam wrinkles when removing from freezer
| Pour into dry, sterlised jars, place a waxed disk on the jam and seal
| It will keep for up to one year
===Serving suggestions===
Serve on toast or use as a filling for cakes, tarts etc.
===Variations===
Other soft fruit such as raspberries, blackberries etc can be used instead
