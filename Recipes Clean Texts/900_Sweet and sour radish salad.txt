===Description===

An interesting addition to your salad repertoire which gives a nice spicy bite with an oriental flavour.

===Ingredients===

| 8 small red radishes
| 2 teaspoons table salt (10 ml)
| 2 tablespoons Muscovado sugar
| 2 tablespoons Cider vinegar
| 1 teaspoon Sesame oil
| 2 teaspoons of Light soy sauce
===Method===



| Wash, top and tail the radishes
| Using the tines of a fork, scratch the surface of the radishes.
| Arrange on a large plate and sprinkle with half of the salt, leave for 10 minutes
| Turn the radishes over and repeat the process
| Wash the radishes and discard the salt
| Dry with paper towels
| Mix the remaining ingredients and whisk
| Pour over the radishes and all to marinate for two hours or more
===Serving suggestions===
Serve as part of a selection of salads
