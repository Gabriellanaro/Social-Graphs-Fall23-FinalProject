===Description===

Ideal for fried and grilled fish, certain meats and salads.

===Ingredients===
| 142 ml (5 fl oz - ¼ pint) mayonnaise
| 1 tablespoon chopped gherkin or capers
| ½ teaspoon finely chopped shallot (or onion)
===Method===


| Stir the chopped gherkin or capers into the mayonnaise
===Serving suggestions===

Chill and use as required
