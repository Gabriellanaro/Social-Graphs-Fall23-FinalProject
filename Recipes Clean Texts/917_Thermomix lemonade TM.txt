===Description===

Proper homemade lemonade taken from '''Fast and Easy Cooking''' from Thermomix.

===Ingredients===TM
| 70g granulated sugar
| 2 unwaxed lemons, washed
| 8-10 ice cubes
| Water
===Method===


| Weigh the sugar into the TM bowl and grind '''5 seconds / Speed 10'''.
| Whilst the sugar is settling, cut the lemons into quarters and remove any superficial pips.
| Place into the TM bowl, with the ice cubes.
| Pulse '''Turbo / 3-4 seconds'''.
| Add water to the 1.25 litre mark.
| Stir '''5 seconds / Speed 4'''.
| Place the internal steaming basket in the TM bowl, pushing it down carefully.
| Strain the lemonade into a jug or individual glasses while holding the basket in place.
===Variations===
You can use sparkling mineral water instead of still.
