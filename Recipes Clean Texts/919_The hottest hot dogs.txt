===Description===


Hot-dogs made with the best quality sausages and crusty French bread are a far cry from the floppy, tasteless objects sold from vendors vans.  Give these a try!

===Ingredients===
| 6 high quality sausages
| 1 Crusty French baguette
| Salad leaves
| 90g  Belazu Smoked Chilli Jelly (1 jar)

===Method===


| Grill the good quality sausages until cooked.
| Stuff the sausages between slices of crusty French bread with some salad leaves.
| Top with Belazu Smoked Chilli Jelly.
