===Description===

I found this recipe in a copy of The Times in its Food and Drink section.

It looked quite fun and was easy to make.
===Ingredients===

| 250 g self-raising flour
| 200g caster sugar
| 450 g can of pineapple
===Mise en place===

* Preheat your oven to 180\u00b0 C (350\u00b0 F - gas 4), [fan oven 160\u00b0 C & reduce cooking time by 10 mins per hour]

===Method===


| Spray a springform cake-tin with olive oil and lien the base with a circle of greaseproof paper
| Add the pineapple chunks to an electric mixer with a metal blade and blend to a pulp
| If you do not have access to a food mixer you can buy ready crushed pineapple in cans from some supermarkets
| Mix  the crushed pineapple, flour and sugar to a bowl and mix until well combines
| Pour the mixture into your cake tine and bake in the middle of the oven for 60 minutes or until golden brown
| Remove from the oven and leave to cool in the tin for 15 minutes then transfer to a cooling rack until cooled
===Serving suggestions===
Serve with ice-cream
