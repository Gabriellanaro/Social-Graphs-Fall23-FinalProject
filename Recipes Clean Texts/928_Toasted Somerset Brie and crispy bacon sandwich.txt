===Description===


This recipe for a perfect lunchtime snack is reproduced by kind permission of The Lubborn Creamery

===Ingredients===
| 4 Rashers of de-rinded streaky bacon
| 4 Large slices quality white bread
| 135g Ripe Somerset brie
| Butter


===Mise en place===
* Either preheat sandwich toaster or grill. 
===Method===


| Grill the bacon until crispy and remove.
| Using kitchen paper, wipe out grill pan.
| Cut crusts off the sliced bread and butter both sides.
| Cut the ripe brie into strips and arrange them on two of the slices of bread.
| Using scissors cut the crispy bacon rashers into 3 and arrange on top of the cheese.
| Cover with remaining slices of bread and press down at edges.
| Place sandwiches on the grill rack and grill 5 cm from the
| heat until the sandwiches are golden brown - about 1 to 2 minutes both sides.
| Cut into quarters and serve immediately.
===Variations===
Alternative fillings are seedless grapes or ham and tomato.  
===Chef's notes===
Follow the RSPCA Think Pig Checklist to make sure that your pork has come from pigs that have been reared to higher welfare standards.
