===Description===


Another recipe from British Onions

===Ingredients===
| 450g (1lb) Turkey Mince
| 25g Onion, finely chopped
| 1 clove garlic, crushed
| 10g/Â¼oz Breadcrumbs
| 1 Handful Parsley
| 1 Egg, beaten
| Salt & freshly ground black pepper
| 2 tablespoons olive oil
| 4 Ciabatta Rolls
| 2 tablespoons Cranberry relish
| Handful rocket leaves
===Method===


| Mix the turkey, onion garlic, breadcrumbs, parsley egg, salt & pepper together in a bowl. 
| Shape into 4 burgers.
| Heat the oil in a frying pan & fry the burgers on both sides until browned & cooked through.
| Cut the ciabatta rolls in half & toast. 
| Divide the rocket between the rolls, then top with a bun & finally spoon on the cranberry relish.
===Variations===
These could also be grilled or barbecued
