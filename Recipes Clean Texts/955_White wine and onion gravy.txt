A vegetarian onion gravy that uses white wine.

===Ingredients===
| 2 tablespoons olive oil
| 2 onions, peeled and finely sliced
| 2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
| 8g sugar
| 1.5 tablespoons plain flour
| 250 ml dry white wine
| 500 ml vegetable stock
| 2 bay leaves
===Method===


| Heat the oil in a medium pan, add the onions and saut\u00e9 over a medium heat for 7 minutes or until softened.
| Add the garlic and sugar and heat for a further few minutes.
| Stir in the flour and cook for a minute or two until combined.
| Turn up the heat, add the wine and stir constantly for a few minutes with a whisk.
| Add the bay leaves and the stock and heat for a further 5 minutes or until it reached your desired consistency.
| Remove the bay leaves and serve in a gravy boat.
