===Description===

One for those winter nights, taken from issue 57 of The Chap.

===Ingredients===
| A warmed teapot
| 1 part Pimm's No.1 Cup
| 3 parts warm apple juice
===Method===


| Pour into warmed mugs or teacups and add slices of apple and orange.
| Alternatively, pour the lot down the sink and wait until the weather picks up.
