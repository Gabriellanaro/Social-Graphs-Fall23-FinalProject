===Description===

This simple and easy chilled Bulgarian soup is a legacy from the Ottoman empire.

===Ingredients===
| 1.2 litres yogurt
| 1 cucumber, grated, salted and drained for 30 minutes
| Garlic, to taste, crushed, with a little salt
| 75 g walnuts, crushed
| A few sprigs of mint and dill, finely chopped
===Method===


| Rinse the cucumber and stir into the yogurt with the garlic and walnuts.
| Pour into serving bowls and garnish with the chopped herbs.
===Variations===
Although not authentic, you could add a little chopped chilli to the yogurt.
