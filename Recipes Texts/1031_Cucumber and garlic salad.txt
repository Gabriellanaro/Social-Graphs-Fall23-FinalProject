crushed]]\n}}\n\n===Method===\n{{RecipeMethod\n\n| Peel the [[Cucumbers|cucumbers]]\n| Cut them in half lengthways and using a tablespoon or an [[Ice-cream scoop|ice-cream scoop]], remove the [[Cucumbers|cucumbers]] into a bowl and sprinkle with the [[Salt|salt]] then sit them in a [[Colander|colander]] for 10 minutes to allow them to drain\n| Rinse them under cold running water\n| Pat them dry with a [[Paper towel|paper towel]] or dry in a [[salad spinner]]\n| Add the [[Dried|dried]] [[Cucumbers|cucumbers]] to a bowl and sprinkle with the [[Sugar|sugar]] and [[White wine
