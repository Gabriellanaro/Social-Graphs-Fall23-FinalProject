The perfect light summer [[Lunch|lunch]] or [[Starter|starter]].\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n\n| 1 ripe Galia or Honeydew [[Melon|melon]]\n| 1 [[Cucumber|cucumber]]\n| 1 stalk of [[Lemongrass|lemongrass]]\n| 1 heaped teaspoon of freshly [[Grated|grated]] [[Ginger|ginger]]\n| A good pinch of finely chopped fresh [[Chilli|chilli]] (more or less to taste)\n| 200ml [[Coconut milk|coconut milk]]\n| A large handful of Thai or regular [[Basil|basil]]\n| A small handful of fresh [[Mint|mint]]\n| 1-2 [[Limes|limes]], [[Juice|juice]] and [[Zest|zest]]\n}}\n\n===Method===\n{{RecipeMethod\n\n| Halve your [[Melon|melon]]. [[Scoop]] out the [[Seeds|seeds]]. Spoon the flesh from the skin straight into a [[Blender|blender]] or [[Food processor|food processor]].\n| Peel your [[Cucumber|cucumber]]. Roughly chop. Add to the [[Melon|melon]].\n| Bash your [[Lemongrass|lemongrass]] stalk till it flattens and softens a bit. Peel the outer layer away. Thinly slice starting at the base. Finely chop till you have about 1 heaped tsp.\n| Add the [[Lemongrass|lemongrass]] to the [[Blender|blender]]/[[Food processor|food processor]] along with the [[Ginger|ginger]], [[Chilli|chilli]], [[Coconut milk|coconut milk]], [[Basil|basil]], [[Mint|mint]] and the [[Zest|zest]] and [[Juice|juice]] of 1 [[Lime|lime]].\n| Whizz till smooth. Taste. Add more [[Lemongrass|lemongrass]], [[Ginger|ginger]], [[Chilli|chilli]], [[Herbs|herbs]] or [[Lime juice|lime juice]] as needed. Swirl in a bit more [[Coconut milk|coconut milk]], too, if needed (depends on the [[Melon|melon]]\u0092s sweetness).\n| Once it's so good you can't stop eating it, pop it in the [[Freezer|freezer]] to chill quickly or the [[Fridge|fridge]] if you\u0092re serving it later. Lovely garnished with a swirl of [[Coconut milk|coconut milk]] drizzled on top and some fresh [[Basil leaves|basil leaves]].\n}}\n{{Template:PeelingGinger}}\n{{RecipeLine}}\n[[Category:Recipes]]\n[[Category:Chinese recipes]]\n[[Category:Oriental cuisine]]\n[[Category:Vietnamese recipes]]\n[[Category:Snacks and light bites]]\n[[Category:Soup and stock recipes]]\n[[Category:Spicy recipes]]\n[[Category:Starters]]\n[[Category:Fruit recipes]]\n[[Category:Abel and Cole veg box recipes]]\n[[Category:Uncooked]]\n{{Template:Abel and Cole veg box recipes attribution}}\n[[Category:Recipes from other sites]]\n[[Category:Thai recipes]]\n[[Category:Unusual recipes]]\n\n<!-- footer hashtags --><code 'hashtagrev:032020'>#coconutmilk #lemongrass #melon #ginger #chilli #zest #thaimelonsoup #mint #foodprocessor #basil #blender </code><!-- /footer_hashtags -->"
