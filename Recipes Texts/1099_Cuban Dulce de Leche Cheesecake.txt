Tip into the tin, press down firmly with the back of a spoon to cover the base, then [[Bake|bake]] for 10 mins. [[Bake]] for 10 mins, then lower the heat to 110C/90C fan/ gas \u00bc and [[Bake|bake]] for a further 30 mins.
