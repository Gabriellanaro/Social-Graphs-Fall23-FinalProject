heat and leave the [[Prunes|prunes]] to soak for 30 minutes.\n| Drain and dry.\n| Pre-heat the [[Oven|oven]] to 180\u00b0 C (350\u00b0 F - gas 4), [fan oven 160\u00b0 C].\n| Using a long skewer, make pieces, alternately, and push them through the [[Meat|meat]], using the long handle of a wooden spoon.\n| Tie the loin securely at 2.5cm intervals.\n| Place in a [[Roasting tin|roasting tin]] and cook
