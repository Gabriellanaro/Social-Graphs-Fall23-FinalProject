It has now been translated into English and is known as ''I know how to Cook'' (ISBN 0714848042).  \n\nThe recipe given below has been slightly adapted from Ginette's original.\n\nThis needs to be started the day before.\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| 3 tablespoons [[Olive oil|olive oil]]\n| 90g [[Butter|butter]]\n| 1.5kg [[Chicken|chicken]] pieces, bone in (capon was traditionally used)\n| 1 [[Red onion|red onion]], chopped\n| 40g [[Plain flour|plain flour]]\n| 50ml [[Brandy|brandy]]\n| 500ml gutsy [[Red wine|red wine]]\n| Seasoning\n| 2 bulbs [[Garlic|garlic]], peeled and finely [[Diced|diced]] or chopped\n| 190g [[Button mushrooms|button mushrooms]], left whole, ot cut in half if on the large side\n| 100g [[Pickled|pickled]] silverskin [[Onions|onions]]\n| 100g [[Smoked bacon|smoked bacon]], [[Diced|diced]]\n}}\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| The day before you want to serve, heat the [[Oil|oil]] and 50g of the [[Butter|butter]] in a heavy pan or sauteuse.\n| Add the [[Chicken|chicken]] pieces and the [[Onion|onion]] and cook over a medium heat until the chicken is [[Browned|browned]] on both sides.\n| Sprinkle in the [[Flour|flour]] and stirring constantly, cook for a further few minutes.\n| Pour in the [[Brandy|brandy]] and the [[Wine|wine]], increase the heat, and cook until the aroma of [[Alcohol|alcohol]] has disappeared.\n| Season, add the [[Garlic|garlic]] and cook gently for one hour, turning over the [[Chicken|chicken]] pieces half way through.\n| Cool and refrigerate overnight.\n| The next day, gently reheat over a low temperature.\n| Meanwhile, add the remaining 40g [[Butter|butter]] to a [[Frying pan|frying pan]] and saut\u00e9 the [[Onions|onions]] and [[Bacon|bacon]] until the [[Bacon|bacon]] is [[Brown|brown]].\n| Add to the [[Chicken|chicken]], leaving as much [[Butter|butter]] as possible in the [[Frying pan|frying pan]].\n| Next, add the [[Mushrooms|mushrooms]] to the [[Frying pan|frying pan]] and cook until softened.\n| Stir them into the [[Chicken|chicken]] and serve.\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nServe with [[Mashed|mashed]] or straw [[Potatoes|potatoes]].\n===Variations===\nYou can add other [[Vegetable|vegetable]] such as [[Carrots|carrots]] or [[Celery|celery]] if desired.\n===Chef's notes===\nIt was found be be very buttery, so I would recommend reducing the quantity of
