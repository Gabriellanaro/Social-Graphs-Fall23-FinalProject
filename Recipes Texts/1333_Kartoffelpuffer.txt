This will depend on the type of [[Potatoes|potatoes]] used.\n| Divide the mixture into 8 and shape each piece as if you were making burgers.\n| Allow to rest in the [[Fridge|fridge]] for about 30-45 minutes.\n| Heat the [[Oil|oil]] in a [[Frying pan|frying pan]] and cook the kartoffelpuffer on both sides until they are [[Brown|brown]] and the insides are cooked.\n}}\n===Chef's notes===\nIf the [[Potatoes|potatoes]] are very wet, drain of some of the excess water.  Do not be tempted to squeeze it out.
