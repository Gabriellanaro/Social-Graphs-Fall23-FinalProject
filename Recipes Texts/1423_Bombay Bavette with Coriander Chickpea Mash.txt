Add enough [[Oil|oil]] to create a 1cm pool of [[Oil|oil]]. Add your sliced [[Onions|onions]] and a pinch of [[Salt|salt]]. Sizzle over medium-high heat till golden and crisp. Place a fine [[Sieve|sieve]] over a [[Glass|glass]] bowl or pan. Drain the [[Onions|onions]]. (Use the oniony oil to make a [[Salad dressing|salad dressing]] another day.)\n| Place the [[Frying pan|frying pan]] back on the heat. Once hot, add your steaks, ensuring you mop up as much of the [[Spice|spice]] and [[Oil|oil]] as possible before pressing them into the pan. Cook over medium heat for 5 mins, or till warmed through. Drain.\n| Add the [[Zest|zest]] and a good squeeze of [[Juice|juice]] from your [[Limes|limes]]. Crush the [[Chickpeas|chickpeas]] with a [[Potato masher|potato masher]] or fork till you have a rough [[Mash|mash]]. Swirl in the [[Rest|rest]] of your [[Curry powder|curry powder]] and the [[Coriander|coriander]]. Taste. Season as needed. 7.
