The use of [[mustard seeds]] and [[curry leaves]] as ingredients comes from southern India.  \"Tadka\" refers to the process of heating the spices in oil and pouring over the cooked lentils.\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| 200 g yellow [[Split peas|split peas]]\n| 1 litre water or [[Vegetable Stock|vegetable stock]]\n| 15 ml [[Oil|oil]]\n| 1 tablespoon [[ghee]]\n| 1 large [[Onion|onion]], thinly sliced\n| [[Garlic]] to taste, [[Diced|diced]] (I used 2 bulbs)\n| 4 Indian [[Green chillies|green chillies]], chopped\n| 2 teaspoons black [[Mustard seeds|mustard seeds]]\n| &frac12; Experiment with different [[Spices|spices]]. You can also substitute [[Split peas|split peas]] for [[chana]] dhal for a different flavour.\n===Chef's notes===\nTo reduce the need for skimming, use bottled water.
