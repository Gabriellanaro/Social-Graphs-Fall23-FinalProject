It is made from sliced [[Marinated|marinated]] [[Chicken|chicken]] [[Meat|meat]], and served with a [[satay sauce]].\n\nThis variant is unique for the fact that each skewer contains one large piece of [[chicken]], rather than several small slices. The [[Meat|meat]] is marinated in [[Spices|spices]] and sweet [[Soy sauce|soy sauce]], in a process called \"bacem\" and is served with [[Rice|rice]] or lontong (rice cake). The [[Grill|grill]] is made from terracotta earthenware with a hole in one side to allow ventilation for the coals. After three months of use, the earthenware grill disintegrates, and must be replaced.\n\n\n'''[[skewers]]'''\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| Wooden skewers soaked in water for 1 hour\n| 450 g (1lb) boneless [[Chicken|chicken]], skin removed and [[Jointing a chicken|cut into pieces]]\n}}\n'''[[Marinade|marinade]]'''\n{{RecipeIngredientsNB\n| 2 [[Cloves of Garlic, peeled and crushed|cloves of garlic, peeled and crushed]]\n| 1 cm (1/2\") [[Galangal|galangal]], [[Grated|grated]]\n| &#189;
