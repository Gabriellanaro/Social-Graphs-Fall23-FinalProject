Heat a small non-stick [[Frying pan|frying pan]], the same size that you want the [[Wafers|wafers]] to be.\n| Once the pan is hot enough to melt the [[Cheese|cheese]], sprinkle the cheese evenly over the surface of the pan in a thin layer and add a good dusting of [[sesame seeds]].\n| Once the [[Cheese|cheese]] has crisped up, invert the pan over a suitably shaped item such as a [[Soup|soup]] ladle and tap the cheese [[Pancake|pancake]] out over it and allow it to cool and harden.\n| Mix the [[Ricotta cheese|ricotta cheese]], [[Smoked salmon|smoked salmon]] trimmings, chopped [[Parsley|parsley]], [[Salt|salt]] and [[Freshly ground black pepper|freshly ground black pepper]] together in a bowl and add a tablespoons of the mixture into each nest.\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nArrange [[Fresh herbs|fresh herbs]] over the top and add a drop of [[Lemon juice|lemon juice]] and a tiny amount of freshly [[Grated|grated]] [[Lemon zest|lemon zest]].\n===Variations===\nIf you cannot obtain [[D\u017eiugas cheese]], a good quality [[Parmesan]] will work equally well.\n===Chef's notes===\nThe [[Tefal one egg wonder 12 cm mini frying pan]] is
