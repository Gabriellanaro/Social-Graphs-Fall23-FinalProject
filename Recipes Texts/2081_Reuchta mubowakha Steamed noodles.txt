However, if you have one, then do use it!\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| 1 heaped tablespoon [[smen]] (clarified butter) or 20 g [[Butter|butter]]\n| 1 large [[Onion|onion]], roughly chopped\n| Garlic, to taste, finely chopped\n| 900 g [[Lamb|lamb]], [[Goat|goat]] or [[Beef|beef]], cut into 5 cm cubes\n| 2 large [[Tomatoes, peeled|tomatoes, peeled]] and roughly chopped\n| 2 tablespoons [[Tomato|tomato]] pur\u00e9e diluted in 5 tablespoons water\n| 1.5 [[Litres|litres]] [[Meat stock|meat stock]]\n| 175 g [[Chickpeas|chickpeas]], soaked overnight, or 2 x 400g tinned chickpeas, drained\n| 1 teaspoon [[Salt|salt]]\n| &frac12; teaspoon [[Paprika|paprika]]\n| &frac12; teaspoon gound [[Black pepper|black pepper]]\n| &frac12; teaspoon [[Baharat|baharat]]\n| &frac12; teaspoon [[Saffron|saffron]], diluted in 3-4 tablespoons water\n| 450 g reuchta (thin, flat [[Noodles|noodles]]), or [[Spaghetti|spaghetti]]\n}}\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Melt the [[smen]] or [[Butter|butter]] in a large saucepan, add the [[Onion|onion]] and [[Fry|fry]] until soft.\n| Add the [[Meat|meat]], [[Tomatoes|tomatoes]] and [[Tomato|tomato]] pur\u00e9e and cook for 5 minutes.\n| Add the [[Stock|stock]], drained [[Chickpeas|chickpeas]] and the [[Spices|spices]] and stir well.\n| Bring to the [[Boil|boil]] and place a [[Muslin|muslin]] lined [[Colander|colander]] over the top of the pan.\n| Break the [[Noodles|noodles]] or [[Spaghetti|spaghetti]] into 5 cm lengths and place them in the [[Colander|colander]] with a little of the [[Meat|meat]] [[Sauce|sauce]].  If using a proper [[couscoussi\u00e8re]], add the noodles 15-20 minutes before the end of the cooking time, moistening them regularly with the [[Sauce|sauce]].\n| Cover the [[Colander|colander]], lower the heat and [[Simmer|simmer]] for 20 minutes.\n| Remove 1 or 2 ladlefuls of the [[Sauce|sauce]] from the pan and again, pour it over the [[Noodles|noodles]], making sure that all pieces are moistened.\n| Cover the [[Colander|colander]] again and [[Simmer|simmer]] for a further 40 minutes until the [[Meat|meat]] and [[Chickpeas|chickpeas]] are tender, moistening the [[Noodles|noodles]] every 10 minutes.  If using tinned [[chickpeas]], add them 10 minutes before the end of the cooking time.\n| Add more water or [[Stock|stock]] to the [[Stew|stew]] if necessary.\n| To serve, spread the [[Noodles|noodles]] in a large serving dish and pour the [[Meat|meat]] and [[Chickpea|chickpea]] [[Stew|stew]] over them.\n}}\n===Variations===\nIf you don't want the hassle of cooking the [[Noodles|noodles]] over the [[Stew|stew]], simply [[Boil|boil]] them in plain water.
