Bring to the [[Boil|boil]] then strain the flavoured milk into a jug.\n| In a clean pan, melt the [[Butter|butter]] then add the [[Flour|flour]] and cook for 2 mins, stirring, to cook out the rawness of the [[Flour|flour]].\n| Remove from the heat and gradually [[Blend|blend]] in the flavoured [[Milk|milk]] to make a thick [[Sauce|sauce]].
