Season with [[Salt|salt]] and [[Pepper|pepper]].\n| Heat [[Oil|oil]] in a [[Deep frying|deep frying]] pan. Season [[Chicken|chicken]] breasts on both side with [[Salt|salt]] and [[Pepper|pepper]]. Add to pan and [[Fry|fry]] over a medium-high heat for 1-2 minutes or until golden, the turn over and lightly [[Brown|brown]] other side. Transfer to a plate and set aside. \n| Add [[Celery|celery]], [[Carrot|carrot]] and [[Onion|onion]] to the pan and [[Fry|fry]] over a low heat for 5 minutes or until soft but not [[Browned|browned]]. Add half the [[Preserved lemon|preserved lemon]], 2 tablespoons of the [[chermoula]], the [[Tomatoes|tomatoes]] and [[Stock|stock]]. Return the [[Chicken|chicken]] to the pan, then bring to the [[Boil|boil]]. Reduce heat to low and [[Simmer|simmer]] for 10 minutes.\n| Add [[Potatoes|potatoes]] to pan and cook for a further 6-8 minutes or until potatoes and [[Chicken|chicken]] are cooked through. Stir in [[Olives|olives]], [[Herbs|herbs]] and the remaining [[chermoula]] and [[Preserved lemon|preserved lemon]] into the [[Sauce|sauce]]. Adjust the seasoning if necessary. Lift chicken into warmed deep plates and spoon over sauce.
