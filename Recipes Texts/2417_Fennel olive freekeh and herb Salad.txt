If there are any fronds at the top, reserve for garnish \n| 2 [[Leeks|leeks]], washed and thinly sliced \n| 2 [[Courgettes|courgettes]], thinly sliced or spiralised \n| 1 tablespoon [[Coriander seeds|coriander seeds]], lightly crushed \n| A small handful each of fresh [[Mint leaves|mint leaves]], [[Coriander leaves|coriander leaves]] and curly [[Parsley|parsley]], finely chopped \n| 150g [[Green olives|green olives]], quartered \n| 100g [[Blanched|blanched]] [[Almonds|almonds]], [[Toasted|toasted]] and roughly chopped \n| 1 small hot [[Chilli|chilli]], very finely chopped \n| [[Juice]] of half a [[Lemon|lemon]] \n| 1 tablespoon [[White wine|white wine]] or [[Rice vinegar|rice vinegar]] \n| [[Extra virgin Olive oil|extra virgin olive oil]] \n| [[Salt]] and [[Pepper|pepper]]\n}}\n===[[Mise en place]]===\n\n* Preheat your [[Oven|oven]] to 180\u00b0 C (350\u00b0 F - gas 4), [fan oven 160\u00b0 C & reduce cooking time by 10 mins per hour]\n\n===Method===\n{{RecipeMethod\n\n| Turn the [[Oven|oven]] on to 180C/gas mark 4. Set the freekeh soaking in cold water. \n| Put the [[Fennel|fennel]], [[Leeks|leeks]] and [[Courgettes|courgettes]] on a backing tray in one layer. Season with [[Salt|salt]] and [[Pepper|pepper]], give them a good drizzle of [[Olive oil|olive oil]] and sprinkle with the crushed [[Coriander seeds|coriander seeds]]. Put them in the [[Oven|oven]] when it\u0092s up to temperature, setting your timer for 20 minutes \n| When the freekeh has been soaking for 20 minutes, drain and put into a [[Saucepan|saucepan]] with 750mls of water. Put on a medium heat and bring gently to a [[Boil|boil]]. Lower the heat and [[Simmer|simmer]] for about 12 minutes, or just tender and chewy. Drain and set aside \n| When the veggies have been [[Baking|baking]] for 20 minutes, check them for texture and flavour. They should be cooked, but still have a good bite to them, with the [[Leeks|leeks]] in particular nice and crispy around the edges. As soon as they\u0092re done, sprinkle with a little of the [[Vinegar|vinegar]] and set aside. When they\u0092're cool, gently mix the chopped [[Herbs|herbs]] in. Set aside \n| In a bowl, put the [[Olives|olives]], [[Almonds|almonds]], [[Chilli|chilli]] and [[Lemon juice|lemon juice]]. Stir well, and set aside \n| When you\u0092re ready to serve, combine the freekeh, the [[Veggie|veggie]] mix and the [[Olive|olive]] mix.
