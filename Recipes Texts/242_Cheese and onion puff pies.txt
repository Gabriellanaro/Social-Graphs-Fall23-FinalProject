sliced [[Onions|onions]] and 1 tablespoon of the [[Rosemary|rosemary]] and [[Fry|fry]] gently for 10 minutes, stirring until just beginning to turn golden.\n| Meanwhile, bring a saucepan of water to the [[Boil|boil]], add the sliced [[Potatoes|potatoes]] and cook for 4-5 minutes until just tender. \n| Drain and return to the pan then leave to cool for 5 minutes.\n| Add the cr\u00e8me fraiche, [[Cheese|cheese]], [[Garlic|garlic]] and some [[Salt|salt]] and [[Pepper|pepper]] to the [[Potatoes|potatoes]] and gently mix together then add the [[Onions|onions]] and mix once more, being careful not to break up the [[Potato|potato]] slices.\n| Cut the [[Pastry|pastry]] into 6, then roll one piece out on a lightly floured surface until a rough shaped 18cm/7inch square. \n| Lay one of the slices of [[Ham|ham]] on top then [[Pile|pile]] one sixth of the [[Potato|potato]] and [[Onion|onion]] mix on top.\n| [[Brush]] the edges of the [[Pastry|pastry]] with a little [[Egg|egg]] then gradually bring up the edges, pleating the [[Ham|ham]] and pastry up and over the filling to enclose completely, pinch the edges together in the centre and trim off the excess pastry. \n| Put the parcel on to an oiled [[Baking|baking]] sheet and repeat to make six parcels.\n| [[Brush]] the outside of the parcels with beaten [[Egg|egg]]. \n| Cut the remaining [[Onion|onion]] into 6 slices then put a slice on top of each [[Pastry|pastry]] parcel. \n| [[Brush]] with the remaining [[Oil|oil]] then sprinkle with the remaining [[Rosemary|rosemary]] and a little [[Salt|salt]] and [[Pepper|pepper]].\n| [[Bake]] in a preheated [[Oven|oven]] set to 200\u00b0C/400\u00b0F/Gas 6 for 30-35 minutes until golden [[Brown|brown]] and the [[Pastry|pastry]] is cooked
