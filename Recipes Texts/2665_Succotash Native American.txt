It was sometimes cooked in a [[Casserole|casserole]] form, often with a light pie crust on top as in a traditional pot pie. In some parts of the American south, any mixture of [[Vegetables|vegetables]] prepared with lima [[Beans|beans]] and topped with [[Lard|lard]] or [[Butter|butter]] is called succotash. Succotash is a traditional dish of many Thanksgiving celebrations in Pennsylvania and other states. In Indiana, Succotash is made with [[Green beans|green beans]] and corn instead of lima [[Beans|beans]].\n\nOther uses of the word \"[[Succotash|succotash]]\" include its use as the catch phrase of the Warner Bros. cartoon cat Sylvester;
