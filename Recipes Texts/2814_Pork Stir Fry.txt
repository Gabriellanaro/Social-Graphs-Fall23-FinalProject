Set aside.\n| Heat the [[Oil|oil]] in a [[Wok|wok]]. Add the [[Cashews|cashews]] and cook for a minute until golden [[Brown|brown]]. Remove the [[Nuts|nuts]] and tip on to kitchen paper to drain. When cool enough to handle chop finely.\n| Add half of the [[Spring onion|spring onion]] and cook over a high heat for 3-5 minutes until just softened then lift them out and add to the [[Cashews|cashews]].\n| Add the [[Pork|pork]] and [[Carrots|carrots]] to the [[Wok|wok]] and [[Stir fry|stir fry]] for 3-4 minutes, add the [[Broccoli|broccoli]], [[Mange tout|mange tout]], [[Red pepper|red pepper]] and [[Cabbage|cabbage]] stir [[Fry|fry]] for another 4-5 minutes until the [[Vegetables|vegetables]] are tender yet still crunchy.\n| Stir the [[Cornflour|cornflour]] mixture to blend the ingredients, pour into the [[Wok|wok]] and [[Stir-fry|stir-fry]] for a couple of minutes until the [[Sauce|sauce]] thickens. Stir in the [[Spring onion|spring onion]]. Serve the [[Stir fry|stir fry]] sprinkled with the [[Nuts|nuts]], the remaining [[spring onions]], set on a bed of [[Noodles|noodles]] or [[Rice|rice]].\n}}\n===Variations===\nCook the cashews in sesame oil, draining any excess into the soy sauce mix.\n===Chef's notes===\n{{Template:ThinkPig}} \n{{RecipeLine}}\n[[Category:Recipes]]\n[[Category:Pork recipes]]\n[[Category:Meat recipes]]\n[[Category:Main courses]]\n[[Category:Oriental cuisine]]\n[[Category:Stir fried]]\n\n<!-- footer hashtags --><code 'hashtagrev:032020'>#porkstirfry #nuts #cashews #wok #cornflour #pork #noodles #mangetout #muscovadosugar #orange #stirfry </code><!-- /footer_hashtags -->"
