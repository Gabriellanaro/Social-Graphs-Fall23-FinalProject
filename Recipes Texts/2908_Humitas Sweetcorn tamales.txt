[[Sweetcorn|sweetcorn]] into a rough [[Mash|mash]].\n| Place in a bowl and mix in the remaining [[Corn|corn]] ingredients.\n| Leave for at least 30 minutes to thicken.\n}}\n'''For the humitas:'''\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Take 2 [[Corn husks|corn husks]] and lay them so that half of one lies on the half of the other.\n| Place 2 good tablespoons of the [[Corn|corn]] mixture in the centre of the husks.\n| To a small [[Frying pan|frying pan]], on a low heat, add the aj\u00ed, [[Olives|olives]], [[Onion|onion]], [[Achiote|achiote]] [[Oil|oil]] and [[Salt|salt]].\n| [[Fry]], stirring constantly, for a couple of minutes.\n| Remove from heat and allow to cool.\n| For each humita, make a dent in the centre of the [[Corn|corn]] mix and add 1 [[Chicken|chicken]] piece and one slice of [[Egg|egg]].\n| From the [[Fried|fried]] mixture, add a slice of aj\u00ed, 2 [[Olive|olive]] halves and a few [[Onion|onion]] slices.\n| Put a further 2 tablespoons of the [[Corn|corn]] mixture on the top and press the whole thing into a rectangle.\n| Fold one [[Corn husk|corn husk]] over the mixture and the other husk over that.\n| Tie one of the open ends with the nylon thread or raffia, about 5 cm from the end.\n| Do the same with the other end and wrap the humita lengthwise with the thread.\n| When all of the humitas have been made, place them in a wide pan (or two separate ones) so that they all lie on the base of the pan.\n| Add enough hot water to just cover the humitas, cover, and [[Boil|boil]] for 20 minutes.\n| Unfold the humitas and serve warm.\n}}\n===Chef's notes===\nCorn
