Add to the [[Rice|rice]] and [[Meats|meats]].\n| When the [[Beans|beans]] are done, add the [[Rice|rice]] and [[Boil|boil]] gently for 20 minutes.\n| Just before serving, remove the [[Chorizo|chorizo]], slice, return to the pan and warm through.\n}}\n===Variations===\nThis traditional [[Spanish]] dish can vary in many ways depending on the region and the chef.  The [[Chorizo|chorizo]] can be substituted for [[Pork|pork]] chops, but if the [[Stew|stew]] becomes more fatty, add more [[Paprika|paprika]] and water.  You can also add other [[Meats|meats]] such as [[Morcilla|morcilla]] or [[serrano ham]]. \nThere is also a Cuban (vegetarian) version called [[Platillo Moros y Cristianos]].\n===Chef's notes===\nDo cook the [[Chorizo|chorizo]] whole and slice at the end.
