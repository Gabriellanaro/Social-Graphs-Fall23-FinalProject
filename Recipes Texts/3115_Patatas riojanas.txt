[[Oil|oil]], together with the [[Garlic|garlic]], bay leaf and a little [[Salt|salt]].\n| Add the [[Potatoes|potatoes]], chops and [[Chorizo|chorizo]].\n| Cover with water and add the [[Chilli|chilli]] and [[Paprika|paprika]].\n| Allow to cook gently for about 40 minutes until most of the liquid is absorbed.\n| Serve sprinkled with chopped [[Parsley|parsley]].\n}}\n===Variations===\nNot all recipes include [[Pork|pork]] so if you wish to leave them out, add some extra [[Chorizo|chorizo]].\n\nFor a spicier version, use hot [[Smoked
