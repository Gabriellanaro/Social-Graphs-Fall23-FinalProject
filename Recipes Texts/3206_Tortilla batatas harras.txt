This is a [[Tortilla Espa\u00f1ola|Spanish omelette]] with [[Chillies|chillies]], [[Garlic|garlic]] and [[Coriander|coriander]].\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| 130 ml [[Olive oil|olive oil]]\n| 1 kg [[Potatoes|potatoes]], preferably ones which are not floury, eg Cara, Nadine, peeled and sliced\n| 2 medium [[Onions|onions]], peeled and sliced\n| 2 bulbs [[Garlic|garlic]], peeled and finely [[Diced|diced]]\n| 4 [[Chillies|chillies]], finely chopped\n| 70 g [[Coriander leaves|coriander leaves]], finely chopped \n| 8 large [[Eggs|eggs]]\n| [[Salt]] and [[Black pepper|black pepper]] to taste\n}}\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Heat the [[Oil|oil]] in a medium sized, deepish [[Frying pan|frying pan]] at a medium temperarure.\n| When hot, add the [[Potatoes|potatoes]], [[Onions|onions]], [[Garlic|garlic]] and [[Chillies|chillies]].\n| Cover and reduce the heat, stirring from time to time until the [[Potatoes|potatoes]] are cooked and slightly [[Browned|browned]] (about 20 minutes).\n| Meanwhile, beat the [[Egg|egg]] and seasonings thoroughly in a bowl.\n| Drain off most of the excess [[Oil|oil]] and add the [[Potato|potato]] mixture to the [[Eggs|eggs]] and stir in the [[Coriander leaves|coriander leaves]].\n| Pour the whole lot back into the pan - there should be enough [[Oil|oil]] left to coat the bottom of the pan.\n| Increase the heat to medium again and cook the [[Omelette|omelette]] until the bottom and sides have set.\n| Turn out the [[Omelette|omelette]] onto a plate and slide it back into the pan upside down, so that the other side can be cooked. Alternatively, you can simply leave the omlette in the pan and place it under the [[Grill|grill]] until the top has set.\n| Once cooked, turn it out onto a plate and leave to cool. It is best served at room temperature.\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nServe with [[Salad|salad]] or in a [[Baguette|baguette]].\n===Variations===\nUse as much or as little as you like of the [[Garlic|garlic]], [[Chillies|chillies]] and [[Coriander|coriander]].
