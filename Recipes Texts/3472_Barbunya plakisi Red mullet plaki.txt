C (400\u00b0 F - gas 6).\n| Heat a little [[Oil|oil]] in another saucepan and [[Fry|fry]] the [[Garlic|garlic]] for about a minute.\n| Add the [[Tomato|tomato]] pur\u00e9e and [[Chilli flakes|chilli from their saucepan into the pan containing the [[Garlic|garlic]], together with the [[Stock|stock]].\n| Bring to the [[Boil|boil]] and pour over the [[Vegetables|vegetables]].\n| [[Bake]] for about 35 minutes or until the [[Fish|fish]] is cooked.\n| Serve individually or on a serving plate and garnish with the [[Lemon wedges|lemon wedges]] and chopped [[Parsley|parsley]].\n}}\n===Serving
