(optional)\n}}\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| To make the [[Noodle dough|noodle dough]], put the sieved [[Flour|flour]], [[Salt|salt]] and [[Eggs|eggs]] into a [[Blender|blender]], with the water to make a soft, pliable ball of [[Dough|dough]].\n| Knead by [[Hand|hand]], briefly and roll out into a thinnish sheet.\n| To make the filling, mix the [[Meat|meat]], [[Gravy|gravy]], [[Parsley|parsley]] and the [[Egg|egg]].\n| Cut the [[Dough|dough]] in half and place teaspoons of the [[Meat|meat]] mixture, well and evenly spaced over one half of the dough.\n| Using a [[Pastry|pastry]] [[Brush|brush]] and water, dampen between each [[Pile|pile]] of mixture.\n| Place the other half of the [[Dough|dough]] over the top and lightly press it down around each [[Pile|pile]] of mixture.\n| Cut into squares and [[Poach|poach]] gently in [[Broth|broth]] or water for 10-15 minutes.\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nThese can be served in the [[Broth|broth]] as a [[Soup|soup]] or drained and  served with some [[Butter|butter]] and a hard, [[Grated|grated]] Alpine [[Cheese|cheese]] such as [[Austrian Gruy\u00e8re cheese|Austrian Gruy\u00e8re]].\n===Chef's
