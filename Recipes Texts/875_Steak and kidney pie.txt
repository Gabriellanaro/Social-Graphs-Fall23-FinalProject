Making it with with ready-rolled [[puff pastry]] saves loads of your time.\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| 600 g (1lb 5 oz) diced [[stewing]] steak, diced\n| 280 g (10 oz) [[beef kidney]], trimmed and finely sliced\n| 2 tablespoons [[plain flour]]\n| 150ml/5fl oz [[beef stock]] mixed with a dash of [[red wine]]. Half an Oxo cube would do at a push\n| Salt and [[freshly ground black pepper]] to taste\n| Pinch of [[mustard]]\n| 1 large [[onion]], peeled and chopped\n| 2 tablespoons freshly chopped [[parsley]]\n| 1 tablespoon [[tomato pur\u00e9e]]\n| 225g/8oz ready rolled [[puff pastry]]\n\n}}\n\n===[[Mise en place]]===\n* Preheat the oven to 200\u00b0 C (400\u00b0 F - Gas 6 - Moderately hot)\n** If your electric oven has the options of upper, lower, both elements, use the lower element.\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Trim any excess fat from the steak and the kidneys\n| Season the [[flour]] and add the steak and kidney and coat well\n| shake off any excess flour\n| Layer the [[onion]], meat and [[parsley]] into a buttered pie dish\n| Mix the [[tomato pur\u00e9e]], beef stock and [[mustard]]\n| Pour the stock mixture into the dish so that the meat is just over half covered.  Add extra water if required\n| Roll out the [[pastry]] to 2mm (1/8\") thick and cut a long strip to fit around the edge of the pie dish\n| [[Brush]] the edge of the dish with water and press the strip on to it.\n| [[Brush]] the top of the strip with more water and lay the [[pastry]] on the top to make a lid\n| Trim away any excess and crimp the edge. Cut a 1cm (&frac12;\")  hole in the top to allow [[steam]] to escape or fit a [[pie funnel]].\n| Refrigerate the pie for 15 minutes\n| [[Bake]] in the oven for 30 minutes in the middle of the oven until the [[pastry]] has risen and is golden brown\n| Once the pastry has risen, lay a sheet of [[tin foil]] very loosely over the top of the pastry to prevent it overcooking\n| Lower the heat to 180\u00b0 C (350\u00b0 F - Gas 4 - moderate) and cook for a further 90 minutes\n}}\n<gallery widths=300px heights=300px perrow=5>\nImage:Steak and kidney covered.jpg|thumb|300px|right|Once the pastry has risen, loosely cover with tin foil\nImage:Steak and kidney pie pre-lid.jpg|thumb|300px|right|Ready for the lid\n</gallery>\n===Serving suggestions===\n\nServe with [[minty peas]] and [[boiled potatoes]]\n===Variations===\n\nYield to the Delia (Smith) Effect: Add a few tablespoons of [[mushroom ketchup]] to the stock mixture.\n==See also==\n* [[Steak and kidney pudding]]\n* A recipe for [[Steak and kidney pudding (PC)|steak and kidney pudding]], cooked in a pressure cooker\n{{RecipeLine}}\n[[Category:Recipes]]\n[[Category:Beef recipes]]\n[[Category:Main courses]]\n[[Category:Meat recipes]]\n[[Category:Offal recipes]]\n[[Category:Pies tarts and flans]]\n[[Category:British recipes]]\n[[Category:Cooking with wine]]\n[[Category:Favourite recipes]]\n[[Category:Baked or roasted]]\n\n<!-- footer hashtags --><code 'hashtagrev:032020'>#steakandkidneypie #pastry #puffpastry #parsley #brush #mustard #onion #flour #gravy #steam #mushroomketchup </code><!-- /footer_hashtags -->"
