Meanwhile, in a wide non-stick [[Wok|wok]]/[[Frying pan|frying pan]], over a medium heat, [[Fry|fry]] the [[Bacon|bacon]] pieces until starting to [[Brown|brown]], then remove from the heat and set aside. \n| Next, add 2 tablespoons of [[Sesame oil|sesame oil]] to the [[Wok|wok]]/[[Frying pan|frying pan]] and add the [[Carrots|carrots]] and [[Parsnip|parsnip]] slices and saut\u00e9 over a high heat, stirring continuously for 5 mins.\n| Now, add the sliced [[Fennel|fennel]], [[Onion|onion]], a couple of tablespoons of water and season with [[Black pepper|black pepper]], cover and cook over a medium heat for a further 15 minutes until the [[Root vegetables|root vegetables]] are tender and the onion is transparent. \n| Finally, strain the cooked Purple Majesty [[Potatoes|potatoes]] and add
