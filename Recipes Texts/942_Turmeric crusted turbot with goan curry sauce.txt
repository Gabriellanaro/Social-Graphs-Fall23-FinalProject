Blitz them in a liquidizer with the [[Rest|rest]] of the spice mix ingredients. Set aside.\n| To make the [[Sauce|sauce]], heat a [[Dessert|dessert]] spoon of [[Coconut|coconut]] or [[Ground nut|ground nut]] [[Oil|oil]] in a heavy bottomed [[Saucepan|saucepan]] and [[Fry|fry]] the chopped [[Shallots|shallots]] over a medium heat till they\u2019re soft, and are starting to go golden.\n| Add the [[Chilli powder|chilli powder]] and stir for a minute, then add the [[Tomatoes|tomatoes]] and stir till they\u2019ve disintegrated, and are starting to go quite dry.\n| Turn the heat down low, and add the [[Spice|spice]] mix you\u2019ve set aside, and cook slowly, stirring, till the [[Oil|oil]] is starting to separate from the mix.\n| Add the [[Nage|nage]] and [[Simmer|simmer]] for a few minutes, then bring the heat back up and add the [[Coconut milk|coconut milk]] and simmer gently for five minutes.\n| Season, and check the flavour; it should be quite fiery, with a sharp sweet/sour flavour.\n| [[Sieve]] the [[Sauce|sauce]] into a bowl, clean the pan, discarding the remaining contents, and return the sauce to the pan. Then heat a [[Dessert|dessert]] spoon of [[Coconut oil|coconut oil]] in a [[Frying pan|frying pan]] and add the fish, skin side down.\n| [[Fry]] for about 2 minutes, so the skin is nice and crisp, then turn it over, take the heat down a bit, and cook for another 3 minutes.\n| Meanwhile, put neat [[Pile|pile]]s of [[Rice|rice]] on two plates (we use those steel tower thingies, they\u2019re open ended metal rings 7cm in diameter and 6cm tall), then place a piece of [[Fish|fish]] on each plate, add a neat pile of [[Spinach|spinach]], then pour a generous dollop of [[Sauce|sauce]] neatly around, and serve.\n}}\n===Recipe source===\n\n* https://sites.google.com/site/nikandedwinascookbook/Home/fish/turmeric-crusted-turbot-with-goan-curry-sauce\n{{RecipeLine}}\n[[Category:Recipes]]\n[[Category:British recipes]]\n[[Category:Indian recipes]]\n[[Category:Main courses]]\n[[Category:Spicy recipes]]\n[[Category:Chilli pepper recipes]]\n[[Category:Fish recipes]]\n[[Category:Sea fish recipes]]\n[[Category:Unusual recipes]]\n[[Category:Nik and Edwina's Cookbook recipes]]\n[[Category:Pan fried]]\n[[Category:Recipes from other sites]]\n\n<!-- footer hashtags --><code 'hashtagrev:032020'>#sauce #fish #seeds #fry #curryleaves #onion #nage #dessert #shallots #tomatoes #turmeric </code><!-- /footer_hashtags -->"
