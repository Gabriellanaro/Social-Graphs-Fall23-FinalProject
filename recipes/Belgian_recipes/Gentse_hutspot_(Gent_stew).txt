meat, has a cooking time of about 4 hours.\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| 500 g boneless [[Beef|beef]] [[Shank|shank]], in one piece\n|* Stewing steak is fine for this if you cannot obtain shank.\n| 250 g [[pigs trotters]] or [[ham hocks]] (optional)\n| 125 g [[Smoked|smoked]], streaky [[Bacon|bacon]], in one piece\n| 1 litre light [[Beef stock|beef stock]] or water\n| 1 teaspoon [[Salt|salt]]\n| [[Ground black pepper]], to taste\n| 2 [[Bay leaves|bay leaves]]\n| 4 [[Cloves|cloves]]\n| 3 medium [[Carrots|carrots]], peeled and [[Diced|diced]]\n| 2 [[Leeks|leeks]], dark green parts removed, sliced\n| 3 large sticks [[Celery|celery]], [[Diced|diced]]\n| &frac12; medium white [[Cabbage|cabbage]], [[Shredded|shredded]]\n| 4 medium [[Potatoes|potatoes]], peeled and quartered\n}}\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Put the [[Meats|meats]], seasonings, [[Bay leaves|bay leaves]] and [[Cloves|cloves]] in a pan with the [[Stock|stock]] or water and bring to the [[Boil|boil]].\n| Skim, lower the heat, cover and [[Simmer|simmer]] for about three hours.\n| Add the [[Carrots|carrots]], [[Leeks|leeks]] and [[Celery|celery]] and [[Simmer|simmer]] for a further 30 minutes.\n| Add the [[Cabbage|cabbage]] and the [[Potatoes|potatoes]], [[Simmering|simmering]] until the potatoes are cooked.\n| Remove and discard the [[pigs' trotters]].\n| Slice the [[Beef|beef]] and [[Bacon|bacon]] and arrange them, with the [[Vegetables|vegetables]] on plates or serving platters.\n| Serve the [[Stock|stock]] separately as a [[Gravy|gravy]].\n}}\n===Variations===\n[[Sausages]] can also be added, especially if not using the [[pigs' trotters]].\n\nYou can use other [[Vegetables|vegetables]] such as [[Onions|onions]] and [[Turnips|turnips]].\n===Chef's notes===\nUsing bottled water for the [[Stock|stock]], although not very ecological, will reduce the need to skim.
