The [[Mustard|mustard]] [[Vinaigrette|vinaigrette]], [[Pickled beetroot|pickled beetroot]], acidulated [[Butter|butter]],  and [[Horseradish cream|horseradish cream]] can be made a day or two in [[Butter|butter]] '''\n{{RecipeIngredientsNB\n| 50g [[Onions|onions]], peeled and roughly chopped\n| 90g [[Dry white wine|dry white wine]] \n| 150g [[White wine vinegar|white wine vinegar]] \n| 125g [[Unsalted butter|unsalted butter]], cubed \n}}\n'''For the [[Pickled beetroot|pickled beetroot]] '''\n{{RecipeIngredientsNB\n| 200g [[Cider vinegar|cider vinegar]] \n| 1 teaspoon [[Caster [[Sauce|sauce]]'''\n{{RecipeIngredientsNB\n| 40g fresh [[Horseradish|horseradish]], cut into chunks\n| 1 teaspoon water\n| 1 teaspoon [[Wine vinegar|wine vinegar]]\n| A dash of [[Lemon juice|lemon '''\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Place all of the ingredients into the bowl, except for the seasoning 10 seconds / Speed 8\n| Season to taste and mix 3 seconds / Speed 5\n| Reserve in a sealed container in the [[Fridge|fridge]] until needed\n}}\n'''For the acidulated [[Butter|butter]] '''\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Give the bowl a wipe with kitchen paper\n| Add the [[Onions|onions]], [[Wine|wine]] and [[Vinegar|vinegar]] 5 seconds / Speed 7\n| Scrap down the bowl\n| Cook at Speed 1 / Temp Varoma / MC Off until the liquid has reduced by about three quarters - this could take up to 15-20 minutes\n| During this time, scrap down the bowl a couple of times and check the liquid level - it will be impossible to be really precise because of the inclusion of the [[Onions|onions]]\n| Remove the bowl from the body of the [[Thermomix]] and allow to cool - alternatively, if you have too much time on your hands, you can just leave the bowl where it is and start the next stage once the temperature drops to 50\u00b0 C\n| Attach the [[Butterfly]] to the blade\n| Set for 4 minutes / Speed 3 / 50\u00b0 C\n| Gradually add the cubed [[Butter|butter]] through the hole in the lid (but keep the lid on between additions)\n| You will hear when the [[Butter|butter]] cubes have been emulsified when the whisking sounds less noisy - obviously the colder the butter, the longer it will take\n| If necessary emulsify for a minute or so longer\n| Leave to [[Rest for|rest for]] 20 minutes at room temperature\n| Push through a very fine [[Sieve|sieve]] agitating the solids with the spatula - you should left with a very small amount indeed of solid matter\n| Pour into a small bowl and [[Refrigerate|refrigerate]] until required\n}}\n'''For the [[Pickled beetroot|pickled beetroot]] '''\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Whisk the [[Vinegar|vinegar]], [[Sugar|sugar]] and [[Salt|salt]] together by [[Hand|hand]] in a small bowl\n| Add the cubed [[Beetroot|beetroot]] and set aside in the [[Fridge|fridge]] for at least 30 minutes\n}}\n'''For the [[Creamed horseradish|creamed horseradish]] [[Sauce|sauce]]'''\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Using a clean bowl, grate the [[Horseradish|horseradish]] with the water, [[Vinegar|vinegar]] and [[Lemon juice|lemon juice]] 12 seconds / Speed 6-8-10\n| Scrape down the bowl\n| Stir in the [[Cream|cream]] 6 seconds / Speed 4, adding [[Salt|salt]] to taste\n}}\n'''For the [[Risotto|risotto]]'''\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Using a clean bowl, grate the [[Parmesan cheese]] and set aside\n| Add the [[Beetroot|beetroot]] [[Juice|juice]] 5-8 minutes / Speed Spoon / 100\u00b0 C / MC Off until reduce by half.  Keep an eye on this as beetroot contains natural [[Sugar|sugar]] and it may reduce more quickly than desired\n| Pour the [[Juice|juice]] into a jug and reserve\n| Wipe the bowl with [[Kitchen towel|kitchen towel]]\n| Add the [[Olive oil|olive oil]] to the bowl 4 minutes / Speed 1 / 100\u00b0 C\n| Add the pearled [[Spelt|spelt]] 5 minutes / Speed Spoon / 100\u00b0 C / Reverse blade - the spelt should have coloured slightly, but if not, cook for a little longer\n| Add the [[Dry white wine|dry white wine]] 5 minutes / Speed Spoon / Temp Varoma / Reverse blade or until the [[Wine|wine]] has reduced by three quarters - again this can be difficult to judge\n| Add 300g of the warm [[Stock|stock]] 10 minutes / Speed Spoon / Temp Varoma / Reverse blade until most of the stock has been absorbed\n| Add the remaining [[Stock|stock]] at the same Speed, Temp etc - you can do this 100g at a time ensuring that before each addition, the previous stock has been absorbed.  However, it is possible to add the whole 300g in one go\n| Add the [[Beetroot|beetroot]] [[Juice|juice]] and continue cooking for a further 5 minutes / Speed Spoon / Temp Varoma / Reverse blade\n}}\n'''To serve'''\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Stir in the [[Grated|grated]] [[Parmesan]], [[Salt|salt]], acidulated [[Butter|butter]], [[Yoghurt|yogurt]] and the [[Fennel seeds|fennel seeds]] 20 seconds / Speed Spoon / Reverse blade and leave to [[Rest for|rest for]] a few minutes\n| Serve on plates or in bowls with a dollop of [[Creamed horseradish|creamed horseradish]] and a scattering of drained [[Beetroot|beetroot]]\n| Accompany with [[Salad|salad]] leave of your choice, dressed in the [[Mustard|mustard]] [[Vinaigrette|vinaigrette]]\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nThis [[Risotto|risotto]] also goes well as an [[Accompaniment|accompaniment]] to [[Fish|fish]] and [[Roasted|roasted]] [[Game|game]] birds, in which case leave out the [[Salad|salad]] leaves and [[Vinaigrette|vinaigrette]] if desired\n===Variations===\nUse [[Chicken stock|chicken stock]] instead of [[Vegetable|vegetable]] if preferred\n===Chef's notes===\nThe optional [[Soy lecithin|soy lecithin]] for the [[Vinaigrette|vinaigrette]] is to emulsify the other ingredients, ie the [[Sauce|sauce]] will not separate during storage\n{{RecipeLine}}\n[[Category:Recipes]]\n[[Category:British recipes]]\n[[Category:Accompaniments]]\n[[Category:Main courses]]\n[[Category:Pickles and preserves]]\n[[Category:Spicy recipes]]\n[[Category:Vegetarian recipes]]\n[[Category:Grain and bean recipes]]\n[[Category:Vegetable recipes]]\n[[Category:Thermomix recipes]]\n[[Category:Boiled or simmered]]\n\n<!-- footer hashtags --><code 'hashtagrev:032020'>#butter #vinaigrette #beetroot #mustard #creamedhorseradish #sauce #juice #risotto #salad #drywhitewine #pickledbeetroot </code><!-- /footer_hashtags -->"
