Just roll it our flat and cut into rough strips with a knife.\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| 2 portions of [[Home made pasta]] or fresh pasta, rolled flat and cut into rough rustic strips\n| ''OR'' 300g [[Dried|dried]] [[Tagliatelle|tagliatelle]]\n| 300g small [[Prawns#Brown_shrimp|brown shrimp]]\n| 500 g [[Frozen peas|frozen peas]]\n| 4 cloves of [[Garlic]], peeled and crushed\n| 2 tablespoons finely chopped [[Mint leaves|mint leaves]]\n| [[Juice]] from 1 [[Lemon|lemon]]\n| 3 tablespoons of [[butter]] or a mixture of [[butter]] and [[olive oil]] for the more health conscious. \n| [[Salt]] and [[Freshly ground black pepper|freshly ground black pepper]]\n}}\n\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Melt half of the [[Butter|butter]] in a pan, add the [[Garlic|garlic]] and gently saut\u00e9 until soft, don't allow it to burn or colour.\n| Add the [[Prawns#Brown_shrimp|shrimps]], half of the [[Lemon juice|lemon juice]] and season with [[Salt|salt]], [[Pepper|pepper]].
