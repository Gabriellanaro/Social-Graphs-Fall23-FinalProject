starting off in a cast [[Iron|iron]] [[Frying pan|frying pan]] to [[Brown|brown]] the [[Duck|duck]], [[chorizo]] and [[leeks]].\n{{RecipeIngredients\n\n| 1 large [[Duck|duck]] leg, skin on, from herbs]]\n}}\n\n===Method===\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Remove the skin from the chorizo by peeling it off\n| Chop the chorizo into rings\n| Using no [[Fat|fat]] at all, [[Fry|fry]] the [[Duck|duck]] leg for 10 minutes in a heavy based pan to [[Brown|brown]] and release the [[Duck fat|duck fat]] from the skin\n| Chop the [[Bacon|bacon]] rashers into bite-sized pieces and [[Fry|fry]] them together with the [[chorizo slices]] for 3 or 4 minutes to colour them\n| Leaving the [[Fat|fat]] in the pan, remove all the [[Meat|meat]] and place in the [[Slow Cooker|slow cooker]] while you cook the [[leeks]]\n| [[Sautee]] the [[leeks]] for 5 minutes until until well [[Browned|browned]]\n| Add the [[Garlic|garlic]] and [[Stir-fry|stir-fry]] for a minute or so\n| Tip the contents of the pan into the [[Slow Cooker|slow cooker]] and [[Deglaze|deglaze]] the pan with the [[Sherry vinegar|sherry vinegar]]\n| Mix all of the [[Tomatoes|tomatoes]], drained [[Beans|beans]], [[Chillies|chillies]], [[Herbs|herbs]] and seasoning together and stir well to combine\n| Add all of this to the [[Slow Cooker|slow cooker]] together with the [[Budwesier]] mixed with the stock cube and slow cook on high for 2 hours\n| After 2 hours, stir well, remove and discard the whole [[Chillies|chillies]], turn the [[Slow-cooker|slow-cooker]] down to low and cook for the remaining two hours.\n| Remove the duck leg from the slow cooker, shred the meat from the leg and serve directly onto the plates and pour over the beans and sausage mixture.\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nServe with plain boiled potatoes\n===Recipe
