Slowly add the [[Milk|milk]], whisking all the time, until the [[White sauce|white sauce]] is thick and smooth. Add the [[Nutmeg|nutmeg]] and more seasoning then stir and put to one side.\n| Bring a large pan of [[Salted|salted]] water to the [[Boil|boil]]. Add the [[Pasta|pasta]], stir well, bring back to the boil then cook for 10 minutes until [[Al dente|al dente]]. Remove from the heat, stir in the [[Spinach|spinach]] and leave for 2 minutes until the spinach wilts. Drain the pasta and spinach well and put to one side.\n| In a shallow ovenproof dish, layer the three mixtures - [[Pasta|pasta]] first, then [[Sausage|sausage]] mix, then [[White sauce|white sauce]].
