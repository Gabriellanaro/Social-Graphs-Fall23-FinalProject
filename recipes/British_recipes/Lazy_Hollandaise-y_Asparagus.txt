And, [[Hollandaise|hollandaise]] without whisking - genius!\n{{RecipeIngredients\n\n| 1 [[duck|duck egg]] or 2 chicken [[eggs]] per person\n| 6 [[Asparagus spears|asparagus spears]]\n| 100g pack of [[Pancetta|pancetta]], [[Prosciutto|prosciutto]] or [[Parma ham|parma ham]]\n| A knob of [[Butter|butter]]\n| [[Sea Salt|sea salt]] and [[Black pepper|black pepper]]\n| A drop of [[White wine vinegar|white wine vinegar]] (optional)\n}}\n\n===Method===\n{{RecipeMethod\n\n| Get your [[Eggs|eggs]] so they can warm to room temp. Place your [[Eggs|eggs]] in the [[Sauce|sauce]] pan. Set the timer for 7 mins.
