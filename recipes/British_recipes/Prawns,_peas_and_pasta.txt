Stir well to mix and then remove from the heat.\n| Meanwhile cook the [[Peas|peas]] until tender, either by microwaving or [[Boiling|boiling]] - about 4 minutes\n| Cook the [[Tagliatelle|tagliatelle]] in salted [[Boiling|boiling]] water until [[Al dente|al dente]]\n| Drain the [[Pasta|pasta]] and add to the [[prawns]], Stir in the remaining [[Lemon juice|lemon juice]], [[Butter|butter]] and peas.
