[[Rest for]] 10 minutes then slice in half lengthways.\n| Add [[Pesto|pesto]] ingredients to a [[Food processor|food processor]] and combine. Season to taste. Any left over pesto can be covered tightly and [[Frozen|frozen]].\n| Slice the [[Avocado|avocado]] in half, remove the [[Seed|seed]], [[Scoop|scoop]] out the flesh and slice into thin strips.\n| Take a slice of [[Bread|bread]], spread with a heaped tablespoon of [[Pea|pea]] [[Pesto|pesto]], add 4 [[Sausage|sausage]] halves, then one half of the [[Avocado|avocado]] slices and 2 slices of [[Cheese|cheese]] and season with [[Salt|salt]] and [[Pepper|pepper]]. Spread a tablespoon of [[Mayonnaise|mayonnaise]] over the remaining slice of bread and place the mayonnaise side down on top of the cheese. Push together slightly to form the [[Sandwich|sandwich]].\n| [[Butter]] the [[Sandwich|sandwich]] on the outside and toast in a [[Frying pan|frying pan]] for 3-4 minutes on both sides.\n| Place the pan into an [[Oven|oven]] for 5 minutes to melt the [[Cheese|cheese]].\n| Place on [[Kitchen paper|kitchen paper]] to absorb any [[Oil|oil]], slice in half and serve.\n}}\n===Chef's notes===\n[[Pea]] [[Pesto|pesto]] works amazingly well with [[Sausages|sausages]], and could even be stirred through [[Mashed potato|mashed potato]] to liven up [[Bangers and mash|bangers and mash]]. Buttering the outside of the [[Bread|bread]] helps create a golden crust.
