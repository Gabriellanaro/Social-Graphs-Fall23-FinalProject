[[Stew|stew]]'''\n{{RecipeIngredientsNB\n| [[Plain flour]], seasoned with [[Salt|salt]] and [[Pepper|pepper]]\n| 200g [[Belly pork|belly pork]], skin removed and reserved, and [[Meat|meat]] cut into coarsely chopped, to garnish\n}}\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Place the [[Marinade|marinade]] ingredients in a bowl and leave overnight in the [[Fridge|fridge]], covered.\n| Remove the [[Beef|beef]] and [[Marinade|marinade]] from the [[Fridge|fridge]] and allow to come to room temperature.\n| Drain off the [[Marinade|marinade]] into a separate bowl and reserve.\n| Dry the [[Meat|meat]] with kitchen paper and place in a further bowl.\n| Lightly dredge with the seasoned [[Flour|flour]] (a [[Flour|flour]] dredger would be useful for this) and toss the [[Beef|beef]] in the flour.\n| Cook the [[Belly pork|belly pork]] (minus the skin) in a non-stick [[Casserole|casserole]] or [[Deep frying|deep frying]] pan until it starts to produce its own [[Dripping|dripping]].\n| Remove the [[Pork|pork]] pieces and reserve.\n| Add the [[Olive oil|olive oil]], bring to a high-ish heat and add the [[Beef|beef]].\n| When the [[Beef|beef]] has [[Browned|browned]] on all sides, remove and reserve with the [[Belly pork|belly pork]].\n| Add the [[Onions|onions]], [[Garlic|garlic]], [[Bacon|bacon]] and [[Mushrooms|mushrooms]] and cook until they start to colour.\n| Add the reserved [[Marinade|marinade]], and [[Boil|boil]] until the smell of [[Alcohol|alcohol]] has disappeared.\n| Add the reserved [[Pork|pork]], [[Beef|beef]] and [[Pork|pork]] skin.\n| Bring to the [[Boil|boil]], then [[Simmer|simmer]] on a low heat, covered, for 2-3 hours.\n| About 15-20 minutes before the end, remove the lid and cook on a higher heat until the [[Sauce|sauce]] has thickened.\n| Serve, garnished with the chopped [[Parsley|parsley]].\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nYou can serve with [[Mashed potatoes|mashed potatoes]] and [[Vegetables|vegetables]].
