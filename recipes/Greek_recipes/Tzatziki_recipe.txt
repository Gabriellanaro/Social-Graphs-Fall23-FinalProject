Tzatziki is made of strained [[yogurt]] \u2014 in Greece and Turkey usually [[sheeps' milk]] or [[goats' milk]] [[yogurt]] \u2014 to which are added [[cucumbers]] (either pureed and strained or seeded and finely diced), [[Garlic|garlic]], [[salt]], [[cracked black pepper]] and usually [[olive oil]], [[dill]], sometimes [[lime juice]] and [[parsley]], or [[mint]]. [[Olive oil]], [[olives]], and [[herbs]] are often used as garnishes as well.\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| 500 g (large pot) of good [[Greek yogurt]] such as Total\n| 4 Cloves [[Garlic|garlic]], peeled and minced\n| 1 tablespoon of [[dill]] or [[tarragon]]\n| 2 tablespoons [[olive oil]]\n| 4 tablespoons [[lemon juice]]\n| 1 [[cucumber]], peeled (deseeded if you're fussy!) and finely [[diced]]\n| [[Sea salt]] and [[white pepper]] to taste\n}}\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| In a bowl, add the [[yogurt]], [[Garlic|garlic]], [[olive oil]] and [[lemon juice]] and mix really well\n| Stir in the chopped [[cucumber]] and most of the [[dill]], reserving a little for garnish\n| If desired, thin with a few tablespoons of cold water\n| Refrigerate for an hour\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\n\nServe chilled, sprinkled with the remaining [[dill]]\n{{RecipeLine}}\n[[Category:Recipes]]\n[[Category:Accompaniments]]\n[[Category:Dips and sauces]]\n[[Category:Garlic recipes]]\n[[Category:Greek recipes]]\n[[Category:Salad recipes]]\n[[Category:Starters]]\n[[Category:Snacks and light bites]]\n[[Category:Ingredients]]\n[[Category:Condiments]]\n[[Category:Dairy products]]\n[[Category:Favourite recipes]]\n[[Category:Uncooked]]\n[[Category:Unusual recipes]]\n\n<!-- footer hashtags --><code 'hashtagrev:032020'>#oliveoil #dill #garlic #yogurt #tzatzikirecipe #lemonjuice #cucumber #diced #olives #whitepepper #crackedblackpepper </code><!-- /footer_hashtags -->"
