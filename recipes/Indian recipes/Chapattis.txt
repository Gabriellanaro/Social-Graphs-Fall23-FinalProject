If you do not have one, roll out the [[Dough|dough]] between two sheets of [[Bake-O-Glide]] or similar.\n\nChapattis can be [[Frozen|frozen]], after which they should be thoroughly [[defrosted]] and gently reheated.\n===Chef's note===\nIf you don't have a [[tortilla press]], use 2 chopping boards or just two sheets of wood as a substitute.
