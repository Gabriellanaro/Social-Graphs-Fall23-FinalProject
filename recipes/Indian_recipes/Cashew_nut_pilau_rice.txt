ideal to serve with [[Curries|curries]] or to eat on its own as a [[Snack|snack]].\n\n{{RecipeIngredients\n| 225g [[Basmati rice|basmati rice]]\n| Up to 75 ml [[Peanut oil|peanut oil]]\n| 1 medium [[Onion|onion]], sliced\n| 2 bulbs [[Garlic|garlic]] (or to taste), crushed\n| 1 large [[Carrot|carrot]], coarsely [[Grated|grated]]\n| 1 teaspoon of [[Cumin seeds|cumin seeds]]\n| 2 teaspoons of [[Ground coriander|ground coriander]]\n| 2 teaspoons of black [[Mustard seeds|mustard seeds]]\n| The [[Seeds|seeds]] of 4 [[Green cardamom pods|green cardamom pods]]\n| 450ml warm [[Vegetable Stock|vegetable stock]]\n| 2 [[Bay leaves|bay leaves]]\n| 100g [[Savoury nuts|savoury cashew nuts]]\n| Seasoning\n| [[Coriander leaves]], chopped (to garnish)\n}}\n===Method===\n\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Soak the [[Rice|rice]] in cold water for at least 30 minutes, then drain well.\n| Heat the [[Oil|oil]] in a saucepan, add the [[Onion|onion]], [[Carrot|carrot]] and [[Garlic|garlic]] and cook gently for 3-4 minutes.\n| Add the [[Rice|rice]] and [[Spices|spices]] and cook for a further 2 minutes, stirring constantly so that all the [[Grains|grains]] of rice are coated in the [[Oil|oil]].\n| Pour in the [[Stock|stock]] and add the [[Bay leaves|bay leaves]] and seasoning.\n| Bring to the [[Boil|boil]], reduce the heat and [[Simmer|simmer]], covered, until the [[Rice|rice]] is cooked and has absorbed the liquid - about 12-15 minutes.\n| Discard the [[Bay leaves|bay leaves]] and stir in the [[Nuts|nuts]].\n| Adjust seasoning and garnish with the [[Coriander leaves|coriander leaves]].\n}}\n===Variations===\nUse any [[Nuts|nuts]] you like and add [[Chillies|chillies]] and/or other [[Vegetables|vegetables]].
