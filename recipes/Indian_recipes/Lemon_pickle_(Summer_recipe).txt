Trim off the stalk and any odd bits.\n| Halve them and remove any pips\n| Cut the [[Lemons|lemons]] in to small pieces about 2 cm / &frac34; inch on each side, and place in a bowl.\n| [[Dry-roast]] the [[Fenugreek|fenugreek]], black [[Mustard|mustard]] and [[Asafoetida|asafoetida]] together in a pan on a medium heat for about 5 minutes. Don't burn them!\n| Grind the [[Roasted|roasted]] [[Spices|spices]] in a grinder, or a [[Mortar and pestle|mortar and pestle]].\n| Add the [[Ground|ground]] [[Spices|spices]], [[Turmeric powder|turmeric powder]], [[Salt|salt]], [[Sugar|sugar]] and [[Chili powder|chili powder]] to the bowl and mix together well.\n| Pack the mixture into a 2 litre (2 quart) jar, or use a couple of smaller jars. Don't fill quite to the top - allow at least 1 cm space.\n}}\n'''[[Fermentation]]'''\n{{RecipeMethod\n| Sit the jar where it will get the heat of the sun. Bring the jar inside in the evening, and put it back out in the morning. Do this for at least a month in summer, two months in winter.
