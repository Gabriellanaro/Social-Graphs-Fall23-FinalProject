Kimchi is also a common ingredient and combined with other ingredients to make dishes such as [[kimchi stew]] ([[kimchi jjigae]]) and kimchi fried rice (kimchi bokkeumbap). Kimchi is so common in Korea cuisine that the Korea Aerospace Research Institute (KARI) developed space kimchi to accompany the first Korean astronaut to the Russian-manned space ship Soyuz.\n===Varieties of kimchi===\nKimchi varieties are determined by the main [[vegetable]] ingredients and the mix of seasonings used to flavour the kimchi.
