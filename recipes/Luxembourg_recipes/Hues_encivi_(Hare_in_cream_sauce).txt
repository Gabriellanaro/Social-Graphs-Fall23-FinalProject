[[Bacon|bacon]] rashers and add to a pan with the [[Carrot|carrot]], [[Onion|onion]], [[Bay leaf|bay leaf]] and [[Nutmeg|nutmeg]].\n| Add about 170 ml [[Chicken stock|chicken stock]] and the platter and cover with the [[Sauce|sauce]].\n| Garnish with more [[Capers|capers]] and [[Lemon slices|lemon slices]].\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nServe with buttered
