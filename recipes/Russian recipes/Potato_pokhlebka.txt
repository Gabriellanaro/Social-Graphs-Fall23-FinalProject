further 5 minutes.\n| Add the [[Dill|dill]] and [[Parsley|parsley]] and cook for 2 minutes more.\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nServe with dark, hot, [[Rye|rye]] [[Bread|bread]].\n===Variations===\nThere are many types of pokhlebka, each taking its name from the main [[Vegetable|vegetable]] or pulse it contains, so you can substitute the [[Potato|potato]] for [[Lentils|lentils]] and any sort of vegetable you like. \n===Chef's notes===\nAlthough water is traditionally used instead of [[Stock|stock]], I would be inclined to use a homemade [[Vegetable
