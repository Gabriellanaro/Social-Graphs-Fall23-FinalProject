However the Catalans put the [[Oil|oil]] on before the [[Tomato|tomato]].  Sometimes, sliced [[ham]] is added on the top.\n\n===Chef's notes===\nThere are some cheat's methods of making this, especially if you cannot get ripe [[Tomatoes|tomatoes]].  Firstly, you can grate the [[Tomato|tomato]] finely and spread it over the toast. or try using a tinned [[Plum tomato|plum tomato]].
