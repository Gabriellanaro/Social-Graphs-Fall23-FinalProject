cover.\n| Once they are cooked, reserve the [[Stock|stock]] if you are going to use it for the [[Sauce|sauce]]\n| Cut the [[Prawns|prawns]] into small cubes and crumble the hake, removing any skin and [[bones]].\n| To make the [[Sauce|sauce]], heat the [[Sunflower oil|sunflower oil]] in a pan and add the [[Flour|flour]].\n| Cook lightly until the [[Flour|flour]] is golden [[Brown|brown]] and add tablespoons reserved sauce.\n| Fill the [[Peppers|peppers]] with the [[Fish|fish]] mixture.\n| Wrap each [[Pepper|pepper]] in [[Puff pastry|puff pastry]] and using a [[lattice cutter]], cut pieces of [[Pastry|pastry]] to put on top of each wrapped pepper.
