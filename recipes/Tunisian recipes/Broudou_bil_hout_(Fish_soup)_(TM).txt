Varoma / Speed 1'''.\n| A minute before the end of the cooking time, add the [[Parsley|parsley]] and [[Coriander|coriander]] to the TM bowl.\n| Place the [[Fish|fish]], [[Potatoes|potatoes]] and [[Fennel|fennel]] in bowls and pour the [[Sauce|sauce]] over them.\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nServe with [[Bread|bread]].\n===Variations===\nYou can also add 2 tablespoons of [[Capers|capers]], 2 tablespoons of [[Caraway seeds|caraway seeds]] and/or 1 teaspoon [[Ground coriander|ground coriander]].
