Once it has melted take out of the oven and enjoy!\n}}\n===Serving suggestions===\nServe with natural [[Yoghurt|yogurt]], and alongside [[Rice|rice]], [[Chips|chips]] or [[Potatoes|potatoes]].\n===Chef's notes===\nAfiyet olsun!
